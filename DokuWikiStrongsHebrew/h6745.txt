====== H6745: צֵלָחָה (tsêlâchâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h6743|H6743]];

===== Meaning =====

something **protracted** or **flattened out**, i.e. a **platter**

===== Definitions =====

  - protracted
  - flattened out
  - platter

===== Usage =====

pan.
