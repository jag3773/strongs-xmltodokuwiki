====== H3955: לְשַׁד (lᵉshad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from an unused root of uncertain meaning; apparently **juice**, i.e.

===== Meaning =====

(figuratively) **vigor**; also a sweet or fat **cake**

===== Definitions =====

  - juice
  - vigor
  - cake

===== Usage =====

fresh, moisture.
