====== H4489: מֹנֶה (môneh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H4487;

===== Meaning =====

properly, something **weighed out**, i.e. (figuratively) a **portion** of time, i.e. an **instance**

===== Definitions =====

  - weighed out
  - portion
  - instance

===== Usage =====

time.
