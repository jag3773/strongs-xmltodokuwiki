====== H5411: נָתִין (Nâthîyn) ======

===== Source =====

or    (Ezra 8:17), (the proper form as passive participle), from  H5414 ;  , i.e. (in the plural only)

===== Meaning =====

one given the  Nethinim , or  Temple-servants  (as given to that duty)

===== Definitions =====

  - one given
  - Nethinim
  - Temple-servants

===== Usage =====

Nethinims.
