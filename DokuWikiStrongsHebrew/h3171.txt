====== H3171: יְחִיאֵל (Yᵉchîyʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

or (2 Chronicles 29:14) ; from H2421 and H410; God will live;

===== Meaning =====

**Jechiel** (or Jechavel), the name of eight Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - Jechiel

===== Usage =====

Jehiel.
