====== H4701: מִצְנֶפֶת (mitsnepheth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h6801|H6801]];

===== Meaning =====

a **tiara**, i.e. official **turban** (of a king or high priest)

===== Definitions =====

  - tiara
  - turban

===== Usage =====

diadem, mitre.
