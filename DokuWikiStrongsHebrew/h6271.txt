====== H6271: עֲתַלְיָה (ʻĂthalyâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m n-pr-f

===== Source =====

or ; from the same as H6270 and H3050; **Jah has constrained**;

===== Meaning =====

**Athaljah**, the name of an Israelitess and two Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - Jah has constrained
  - Athaljah

===== Usage =====

Athaliah.
