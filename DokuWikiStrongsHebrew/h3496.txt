====== H3496: יַתְנִיאֵל (Yathnîyʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to endure, and H410; **continued of God**;

===== Meaning =====

**Jathniel**, an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - continued of God
  - Jathniel

===== Usage =====

Jathniel.
