====== H8064: שָׁמַיִם (shâmayim) ======

===== Source =====

dual of an unused singular   ; from an unused root meaning to  ;

===== Meaning =====

be lofty the  sky  (as  aloft ; the dual perhaps alluding to the visible arch in which the clouds move, as well as to the higher ether where the celestial bodies revolve)

===== Definitions =====

  - be lofty
  - sky
  - aloft

===== Usage =====

air, × astrologer, heaven(-s).
