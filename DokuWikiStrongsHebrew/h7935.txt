====== H7935: שְׁכַנְיָה (Shᵉkanyâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

or (prolonged) ; from H7931 and H3050; **Jah has dwelt**;

===== Meaning =====

**Shekanjah**, the name of nine Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - Jah has dwelt
  - Shekanjah

===== Usage =====

Shecaniah, Shechaniah.
