====== H6298: פָּגַשׁ (pâgash) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **come in contact with**, whether by accident or violence; figuratively, to **concur**

===== Definitions =====

  - come in contact with
  - concur

===== Usage =====

meet (with, together).
