====== H8198: שִׁפְחָה (shiphchâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine from an unused root meaning to spread out (as a family; see H4940);

===== Meaning =====

a **female slave** (as a member of the household)

===== Definitions =====

  - female slave

===== Usage =====

(bond-, hand-) maid(-en, -servant), wench, bondwoman, womanservant.
