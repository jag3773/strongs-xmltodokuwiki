====== H343: אֵיד (ʼêyd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from the same as H181 (in the sense of bending down);

===== Meaning =====

**oppression**; by implication **misfortune**, **ruin**

===== Definitions =====

  - oppression
  - misfortune
  - ruin

===== Usage =====

calamity, destruction.
