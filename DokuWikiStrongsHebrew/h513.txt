====== H513: אֶלְתּוֹלַד (ʼEltôwlad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

probably from H410 and a masculine form of H8435 (compare H8434); God (is) generator;

===== Meaning =====

**Eltolad**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Eltolad

===== Usage =====

Eltolad.
