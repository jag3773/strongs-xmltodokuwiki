====== H2818: חֲשַׁח (chăshach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v n-f

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) a collateral root to one corresponding to H2363 in the sense of readiness;

===== Meaning =====

to **be necessary** (from the idea of convenience) or (transitively) to **need**

===== Definitions =====

  - be necessary
  - need

===== Usage =====

careful, have need of.
