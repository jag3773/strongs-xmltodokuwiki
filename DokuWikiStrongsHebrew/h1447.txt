====== H1447: גָּדֵר (gâdêr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h1443|H1443]];

===== Meaning =====

a **circumvallation**; by implication, an **inclosure**

===== Definitions =====

  - circumvallation
  - inclosure

===== Usage =====

fence, hedge, wall.
