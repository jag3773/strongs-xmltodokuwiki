====== H5871: עַיִן (ʻAyin) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - locative

===== Source =====

the same as [[en:lexicon:h5869|H5869]]; **fountain**;

===== Meaning =====

**Ajin**, the name (thus simply) of two places in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - fountain
  - Ajin

Ain.
