====== H571: אֶמֶת (ʼemeth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

contracted from [[en:strongs:h539|H539]];

===== Meaning =====

**stability**; (figuratively) **certainty**, **truth**, **trustworthiness**

===== Definitions =====

  - stability
  - certainty
  - truth
  - trustworthiness

===== Usage =====

assured(-ly), establishment, faithful, right, sure, true (-ly, -th), verity.
