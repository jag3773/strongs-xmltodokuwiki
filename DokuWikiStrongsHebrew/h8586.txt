====== H8586: תַּעֲלוּל (taʻălûwl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h5953|H5953]];

===== Meaning =====

**caprice** (as a fit **coming on**), i.e. **vexation**; concretely a **tyrant**

===== Definitions =====

  - caprice
  - coming on
  - vexation
  - tyrant

===== Usage =====

babe, delusion.
