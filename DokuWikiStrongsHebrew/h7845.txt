====== H7845: שַׁחַת (shachath) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h7743|H7743]];

===== Meaning =====

a **pit** (especially as a trap); figuratively, **destruction**

===== Definitions =====

  - pit
  - destruction

===== Usage =====

corruption, destruction, ditch, grave, pit.
