====== H7180: קִשֻּׁא (qishshuʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from an unused root (meaning to be **hard**);

===== Meaning =====

a **cucumber** (from the difficulty of **digestion**)

===== Definitions =====

  - hard
  - cucumber
  - digestion

===== Usage =====

cucumber.
