====== H2186: זָנַח (zânach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root meaning to **push** aside, i.e.

===== Meaning =====

**reject**, **forsake**, **fail**

===== Definitions =====

  - push
  - reject
  - forsake
  - fail

===== Usage =====

cast away (off), remove far away (off).
