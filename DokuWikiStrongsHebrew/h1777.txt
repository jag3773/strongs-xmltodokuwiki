====== H1777: דִּין (dîyn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

or (Genesis 6:3) ; a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **rule**; by implication to **judge** (as umpire); also to **strive** (as at law)

===== Definitions =====

  - rule
  - judge
  - strive

===== Usage =====

contend contend execute (judgment) judge minister judgment plead (the cause) at strife strive.
