====== H3759: כַּרְמֶל (karmel) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h3754|H3754]];

===== Meaning =====

a planted **field** (garden, orchard, vineyard or park); by implication, garden **produce**

===== Definitions =====

  - field
  - produce

===== Usage =====

full (green) ears (of corn), fruitful field (place), plentiful (field).
