====== H8462: תְּחִלָּה (tᵉchillâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from H2490 in the sense of opening;

===== Meaning =====

a **commencement**; relatively **original** (adverb, -ly)

===== Definitions =====

  - commencement
  - original

===== Usage =====

begin(-ning), first (time).
