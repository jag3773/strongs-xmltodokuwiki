====== H467: אֱלִיפֶלֶט (ʼĔlîypheleṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

or (shortened) ; from H410 and H6405; God of deliverance;

===== Meaning =====

**Eliphelet** or **Elpelet**, the name of six Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - Eliphelet
  - Elpelet

===== Usage =====

Eliphalet, Eliphelet, Elpalet.
