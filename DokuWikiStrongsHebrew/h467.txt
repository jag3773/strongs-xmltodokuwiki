====== H467: אֱלִיפֶלֶט (ʼĔlîypheleṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (shortened) [[en:strongs:|]]; from [[en:strongs:h410|H410]] and [[en:strongs:h6405|H6405]]; God of deliverance;

===== Meaning =====

**Eliphelet** or **Elpelet**, the name of six Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - Eliphelet
  - Elpelet

===== Usage =====

Eliphalet, Eliphelet, Elpalet.
