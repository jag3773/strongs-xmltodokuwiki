====== H2486: חָלִילָה (châlîylâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

inj

===== Source =====

or ; a directive from H2490;

===== Meaning =====

literal for a **profaned** thing; used (interj.) **far be it!**

===== Definitions =====

  - profaned
  - far be it!

===== Usage =====

be far, (× God) forbid.
