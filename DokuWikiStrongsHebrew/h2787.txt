====== H2787: חָרַר (chârar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **glow**, i.e. literally (to **melt**, **burn**, **dry** up) or figuratively (to **show** or **incite passion**)

===== Definitions =====

  - glow
  - melt
  - burn
  - dry
  - show
  - incite passion

===== Usage =====

be angry, burn, dry, kindle.
