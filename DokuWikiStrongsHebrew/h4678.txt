====== H4678: מַצֶּבֶת (matstsebeth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from H5324;

===== Meaning =====

something **stationary**, i.e. a monumental **stone**; also the **stock** of a tree

===== Definitions =====

  - stationary
  - stone
  - stock

===== Usage =====

pillar, substance.
