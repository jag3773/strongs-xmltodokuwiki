====== H3972: מְאוּמָה (mᵉʼûwmâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

x

===== Source =====

apparently a form of H3971;

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **speck** or **point**, i.e. (by implication) something; with negative, **nothing**

===== Definitions =====

  - speck
  - point
  - nothing

===== Usage =====

fault,   no(-ught), ought, somewhat, any (no-)thing.
