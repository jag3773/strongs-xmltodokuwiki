====== H6121: עָקֹב (ʻâqôb) ======

===== Source =====

from  H6117 ; in the original sense, a knoll (as swelling up); in the denominative sense

===== Meaning =====

(transitive)  fraudulent  or (intransitive)  tracked

===== Definitions =====

  - fraudulent
  - tracked

===== Usage =====

crooked, deceitful, polluted.
