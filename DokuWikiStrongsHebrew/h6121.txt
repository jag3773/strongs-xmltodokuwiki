====== H6121: עָקֹב (ʻâqôb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h6117|H6117]]; in the original sense, a knoll (as swelling up); in the denominative sense

===== Meaning =====

(transitive) **fraudulent** or (intransitive) **tracked**

===== Definitions =====

  - fraudulent
  - tracked

===== Usage =====

crooked, deceitful, polluted.
