====== H1268: בֵּרוֹתָה (Bêrôwthâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

or ; probably from H1266; cypress or cypresslike;

===== Meaning =====

**Berothah** or **Berothai**, a place north of Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Berothah
  - Berothai

===== Usage =====

Berothah, Berothai.
