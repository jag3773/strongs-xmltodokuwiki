====== H2839: חִשֻּׁק (chishshuq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h2836|H2836]];

===== Meaning =====

**conjoined**, i.e. a **wheel-spoke** or rod connecting the hub with the rim

===== Definitions =====

  - conjoined
  - wheel-spoke

===== Usage =====

felloe.
