====== H7014: קַיִן (Qayin) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

the same as H7013 (with a play upon the affinity to H7069);

===== Meaning =====

**Kajin**, the name of the first child, also of a place in Palestine, and of an Oriental tribe

===== Definitions =====

  - Kajin

===== Usage =====

Cain, Kenite(-s).
