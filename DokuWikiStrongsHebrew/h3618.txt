====== H3618: כַּלָּה (kallâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h3634|H3634]];

===== Meaning =====

a **bride** (as if perfect); hence, a **son's wife**

===== Definitions =====

  - bride
  - son's wife

===== Usage =====

bride, daughter-in-law, spouse.
