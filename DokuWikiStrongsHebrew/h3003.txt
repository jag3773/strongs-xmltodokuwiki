====== H3003: יָבֵשׁ (Yâbêsh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc n-pr-m

===== Source =====

the same as H3002 (also ; often with the addition of H1568, i.e. Jabesh of Gilad);

===== Meaning =====

**Jobesh**, the name of an Israelite and of a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Jobesh

===== Usage =====

Jobesh (-Gilead).
