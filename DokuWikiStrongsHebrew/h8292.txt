====== H8292: שְׁרוּקָה (shᵉrûwqâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

or (by permutation) [[en:strongs:|]]; feminine passive participle of [[en:strongs:h8319|H8319]];

===== Meaning =====

a **whistling** (in scorn); by analogy, a **piping**

===== Definitions =====

  - whistling
  - piping

===== Usage =====

bleating, hissing.
