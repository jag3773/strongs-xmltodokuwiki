====== H8292: שְׁרוּקָה (shᵉrûwqâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

or (by permutation) ; feminine passive participle of H8319;

===== Meaning =====

a **whistling** (in scorn); by analogy, a **piping**

===== Definitions =====

  - whistling
  - piping

===== Usage =====

bleating, hissing.
