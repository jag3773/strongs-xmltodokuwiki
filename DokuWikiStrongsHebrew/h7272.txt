====== H7272: רֶגֶל (regel) ======

===== Source =====

from  H7270 ;

===== Meaning =====

a  foot  (as used in walking); by implication, a  step ; by euphemistically the  pudenda

===== Definitions =====

  - foot
  - step
  - pudenda

===== Usage =====

× be able to endure, × according as, × after, × coming, × follow, (broken-)foot(-ed, -stool), × great toe, × haunt, × journey, leg,   piss,   possession, time.
