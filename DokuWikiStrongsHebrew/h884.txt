====== H884: בְּאֵר שֶׁבַע (Bᵉʼêr Shebaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from H875 and H7651 (in the sense of H7650); well of an oath;

===== Meaning =====

**Beer-Sheba**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Beer-Sheba

===== Usage =====

Beer-shebah.
