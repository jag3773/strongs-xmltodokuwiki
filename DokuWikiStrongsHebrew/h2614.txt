====== H2614: חָנַק (chânaq) ======

===== Source =====

a primitive root (compare  H2596 );

===== Meaning =====

to  be narrow ; by implication, to  throttle , or (reflex.) to  choke oneself to death  (by a rope)

===== Definitions =====

  - be narrow
  - throttle
  - choke oneself to death

===== Usage =====

hang self, strangle.
