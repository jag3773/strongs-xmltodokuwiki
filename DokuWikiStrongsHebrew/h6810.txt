====== H6810: צָעִיר (tsâʻîyr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; from [[en:strongs:h6819|H6819]];

===== Meaning =====

**little**; (in number) **few**; (in age) **young**, (in value) **ignoble**

===== Definitions =====

  - little
  - few
  - young
  - ignoble

===== Usage =====

least, little (one), small (one),   young(-er, -est).
