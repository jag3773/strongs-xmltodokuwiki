====== H358: אֵילוֹן בֵּית חָנָן (ʼÊylôwn Bêyth Chânân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from H356, H1004, and H2603; oak-grove of (the) house of favor;

===== Meaning =====

**Elon of Beth-chanan**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Elon of Beth-chanan

===== Usage =====

Elon-bethhanan.
