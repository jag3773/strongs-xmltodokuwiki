====== H3272: יְעַט (yᵉʻaṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v n-m

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to [[en:strongs:h3289|H3289]];

===== Meaning =====

to **counsel**; reflexively, to **consult**

===== Definitions =====

  - counsel
  - consult

===== Usage =====

counsellor, consult together.
