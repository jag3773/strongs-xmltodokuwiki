====== H1999: הֲמֻלָּה (hămullâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

or (too fully) [[en:strongs:|]]; (Jeremiah 11:16), feminine passive participle of an unused root meaning to rush (as rain with a windy roar);

===== Meaning =====

a **sound**

===== Definitions =====

  - sound

===== Usage =====

speech, tumult.
