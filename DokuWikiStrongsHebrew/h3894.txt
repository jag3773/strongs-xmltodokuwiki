====== H3894: לָחוּם (lâchûwm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; passive participle of [[en:strongs:h3898|H3898]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **eaten**, i.e. **food**; also **flesh**, i.e. **body**

===== Definitions =====

  - eaten
  - food
  - flesh
  - body

===== Usage =====

while...is eating, flesh.
