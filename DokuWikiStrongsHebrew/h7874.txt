====== H7874: שִׂיד (sîyd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root probably meaning to boil up (compare [[en:strongs:h7736|H7736]]); used only as denominative from [[en:strongs:h7875|H7875]];

===== Meaning =====

to **plaster**

===== Definitions =====

  - plaster

===== Usage =====

plaister.
