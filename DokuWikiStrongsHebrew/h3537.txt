====== H3537: כַּד (kad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to deepen;

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **pail**; but generally of earthenware; a **jar** for domestic purposes

===== Definitions =====

  - pail
  - jar

===== Usage =====

barrel, pitcher.
