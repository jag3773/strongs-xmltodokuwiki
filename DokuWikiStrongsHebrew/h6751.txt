====== H6751: צָלַל (tsâlal) ======

===== Source =====

a primitive root (identical with through the idea of   over (compare  H6754 ));

===== Meaning =====

hovering to  shade , as twilight or an opaque object

===== Definitions =====

  - hovering
  - shade

===== Usage =====

begin to be dark, shadowing.
