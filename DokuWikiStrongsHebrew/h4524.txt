====== H4524: מֵסַב (mêçab) ======

===== Source =====

plural masculine   ; or feminine   ; from  H5437 ;

===== Meaning =====

a divan  (as enclosing the room); abstractly (adverbial)  around

===== Definitions =====

  - a divan
  - around

===== Usage =====

that compass about, (place) round about, at table.
