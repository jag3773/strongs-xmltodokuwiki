====== H4524: מֵסַב (mêçab) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

plural masculine [[en:strongs:|]]; or feminine [[en:strongs:|]]; from [[en:strongs:h5437|H5437]];

===== Meaning =====

**a divan** (as enclosing the room); abstractly (adverbial) **around**

===== Definitions =====

  - a divan
  - around

===== Usage =====

that compass about, (place) round about, at table.
