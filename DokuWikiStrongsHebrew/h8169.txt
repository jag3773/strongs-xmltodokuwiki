====== H8169: שַׁעַלְבִים (Shaʻalbîym) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

or ; plural from H7776; **fox-holes**;

===== Meaning =====

**Shaalbim** or **Shaalabbin**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - fox-holes
  - Shaalbim
  - Shaalabbin

===== Usage =====

Shaalabbin, Shaalbim.
