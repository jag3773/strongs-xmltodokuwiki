====== H4185: מוּשׁ (mûwsh) ======

===== Source =====

a primitive root (perhaps rather the same as  H4184  through the idea of receding by  );

===== Meaning =====

contact to  withdraw  (both literally and figuratively, whether intransitive or transitive)

===== Definitions =====

  - contact
  - withdraw

===== Usage =====

cease, depart, go back, remove, take away.
