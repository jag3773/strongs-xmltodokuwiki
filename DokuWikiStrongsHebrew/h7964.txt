====== H7964: שִׁלּוּחַ (shillûwach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or ; from H7971;

===== Meaning =====

(only in plural) a **dismissal**, i.e. (of a wife) **divorce** (especially the document); also (of a daughter) **dower**

===== Definitions =====

  - dismissal
  - divorce
  - dower

===== Usage =====

presents, have sent back.
