====== H3868: לוּז (lûwz) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root; (compare H3867, H3874 and H3885)

===== Meaning =====

to **turn** aside , i.e. (literally) to **depart**, (figuratively) **be perverse**

===== Definitions =====

  - turn
  - depart
  - be perverse

===== Usage =====

depart, froward, perverse(-ness).
