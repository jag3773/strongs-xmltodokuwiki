====== H3582: כָּחַד (kâchad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **secrete**, by act or word; hence (intensively) to **destroy**

===== Definitions =====

  - secrete
  - destroy

===== Usage =====

conceal, cut down (off), desolate, hide.
