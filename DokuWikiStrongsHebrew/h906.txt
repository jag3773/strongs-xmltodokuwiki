====== H906: בַּד (bad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

perhaps from H909 (in the sense of **divided** fibres);

===== Meaning =====

flaxen **thread** or yarn; hence, a **linen** garment

===== Definitions =====

  - divided
  - thread
  - linen

===== Usage =====

linen.
