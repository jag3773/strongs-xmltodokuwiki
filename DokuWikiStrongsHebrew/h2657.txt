====== H2657: חֶפְצִי בָּהּ (Chephtsîy bâhh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-f

===== Source =====

from H2656 with suffixes; **my delight** (is) **in her**;

===== Meaning =====

**Cheptsi-bah**, a fanciful name for Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - my delight
  - in her
  - Cheptsi-bah

===== Usage =====

Hephzi-bah.
