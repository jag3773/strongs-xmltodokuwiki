====== H2672: חָצַב (châtsab) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

or ; a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **cut** or carve (wood, stone or other material); by implication, to **hew**, **split**, **square**, **quarry**, **engrave**

===== Definitions =====

  - cut
  - hew
  - split
  - square
  - quarry
  - engrave

===== Usage =====

cut, dig, divide, grave, hew (out, -er), made, mason.
