====== H2491: חָלָל (châlâl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h2490|H2490]];

===== Meaning =====

**pierced** (especially to death); figuratively, **polluted**

===== Definitions =====

  - pierced
  - polluted

===== Usage =====

kill, profane, slain (man), × slew, (deadly) wounded.
