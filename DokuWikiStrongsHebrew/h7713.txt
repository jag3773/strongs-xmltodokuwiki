====== H7713: שְׂדֵרָה (sᵉdêrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to regulate;

===== Meaning =====

a **row**, i.e. **rank** (of soldiers), **story** (of rooms)

===== Definitions =====

  - row
  - rank
  - story

===== Usage =====

board, range.
