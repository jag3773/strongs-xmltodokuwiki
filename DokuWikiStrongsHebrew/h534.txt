====== H534: אָמִיר (ʼâmîyr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

apparently from [[en:strongs:h559|H559]] (in the sense of **self-exaltation**);

===== Meaning =====

a **summit** (of a tree or mountain)

===== Definitions =====

  - self-exaltation
  - summit

===== Usage =====

bough, branch.
