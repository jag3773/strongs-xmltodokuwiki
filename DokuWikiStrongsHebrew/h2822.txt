====== H2822: חֹשֶׁךְ (chôshek) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H2821;

===== Meaning =====

the **dark**; hence (literally) **darkness**; figuratively, **misery**, **destruction**, **death**, **ignorance**, **sorrow**, **wickedness**

===== Definitions =====

  - dark
  - darkness
  - misery
  - destruction
  - death
  - ignorance
  - sorrow
  - wickedness

===== Usage =====

dark(-ness), night, obscurity.
