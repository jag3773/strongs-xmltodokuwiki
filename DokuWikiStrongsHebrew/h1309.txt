====== H1309: בְּשׂוֹרָה (bᵉsôwrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

or (shortened) ; feminine from H1319;

===== Meaning =====

**glad tidings**; by implication, **reward for good news**

===== Definitions =====

  - glad tidings
  - reward for good news

===== Usage =====

reward for tidings.
