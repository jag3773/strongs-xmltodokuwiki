====== H6076: עֹפֶל (ʻôphel) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h6075|H6075]];

===== Meaning =====

a **tumor**; also a **mound**, i.e. **fortress**

===== Definitions =====

  - tumor
  - mound
  - fortress

===== Usage =====

emerod, fort, strong hold, tower.
