====== H1747: דּוּמִיָּה (dûwmîyâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from H1820;

===== Meaning =====

**stillness**; adverbially, **silently**; abstractly **quiet**, **trust**

===== Definitions =====

  - stillness
  - silently
  - quiet
  - trust

===== Usage =====

silence, silent, waiteth.
