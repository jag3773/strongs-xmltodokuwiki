====== H671: אֲפַרְסְכַי (ʼĂpharçᵉkay) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) or ; (Aramaic), of foreign origin (only in the plural);

===== Meaning =====

**an Apharsekite** or **Apharsathkite**, an unknown Assyrian tribe

===== Definitions =====

  - an Apharsekite
  - Apharsathkite

===== Usage =====

Apharsachites, Apharasthchites.
