====== H4209: מְזִמָּה (mᵉzimmâh) ======

===== Source =====

from  H2161 ;

===== Meaning =====

a  plan , usually evil ( machination ), sometimes good ( sagacity )

===== Definitions =====

  - plan
  - machination
  - sagacity

===== Usage =====

(wicked) device, discretion, intent, witty invention, lewdness, mischievous (device), thought, wickedly.
