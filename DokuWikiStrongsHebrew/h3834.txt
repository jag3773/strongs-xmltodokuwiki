====== H3834: לָבִיבָה (lâbîybâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or rather [[en:strongs:|]]; from [[en:strongs:h3823|H3823]] in its original sense of **fatness** (or perhaps of **folding**);

===== Meaning =====

a **cake** (either as **fried** or **turned**)

===== Definitions =====

  - fatness
  - folding
  - cake
  - fried
  - turned

===== Usage =====

cake.
