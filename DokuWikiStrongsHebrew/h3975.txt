====== H3975: מְאוּרָה (mᵉʼûwrâh) ======

===== Source =====

feminine passive participle of  H215 ;

===== Meaning =====

something  lighted , i.e. an  aperture ; by implication, a  crevice  or  hole  (of a serpent)

===== Definitions =====

  - lighted
  - aperture
  - crevice
  - hole

===== Usage =====

den.
