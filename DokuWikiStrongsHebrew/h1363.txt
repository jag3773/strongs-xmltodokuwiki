====== H1363: גֹּבַהּ (gôbahh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H1361;

===== Meaning =====

**elation**, **grandeur**, **arrogance**

===== Definitions =====

  - elation
  - grandeur
  - arrogance

===== Usage =====

excellency, haughty, height, high, loftiness, pride.
