====== H7528: רָצַף (râtsaph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a denominative from [[en:strongs:h7529|H7529]];

===== Meaning =====

to **tessellate**, i.e. **embroider** (as if with bright stones)

===== Definitions =====

  - tessellate
  - embroider

===== Usage =====

pave.
