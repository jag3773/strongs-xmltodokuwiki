====== H211: אוֹפִיר (ʼÔwphîyr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

or (shortened) ; and ; of uncertain derivation;

===== Meaning =====

**Ophir**, the name of a son of Joktan, and of a gold region in the East

===== Definitions =====

  - Ophir

===== Usage =====

Ophir.
