====== H8318: שֶׁרֶץ (sherets) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h8317|H8317]];

===== Meaning =====

a **swarm**, i.e. **active mass of minute animals**

===== Definitions =====

  - swarm
  - active mass of minute animals

===== Usage =====

creep(-ing thing), move(-ing creature).
