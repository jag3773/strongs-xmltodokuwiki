====== H6823: צָפָה (tsâphâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root (probably identical with H6822 through the idea of **expansion** in outlook, transferring to action);

===== Meaning =====

to **sheet** over (especially with metal)

===== Definitions =====

  - expansion
  - sheet

===== Usage =====

cover, overlay.
