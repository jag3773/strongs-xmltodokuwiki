====== H192: אֱוִיל מְרֹדַךְ (ʼĔvîyl Mᵉrôdak) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

of Aramaic derivation and probably meaning soldier of Merodak;

===== Meaning =====

**Evil-Merodak**, a Babylonian king

===== Definitions =====

  - Evil-Merodak

===== Usage =====

Evil-merodach.
