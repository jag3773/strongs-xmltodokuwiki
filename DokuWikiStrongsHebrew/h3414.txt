====== H3414: יִרְמְיָה (Yirmᵉyâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

or ; from H7311 and H3050; **Jah will rise**;

===== Meaning =====

**Jirmejah**, the name of eight or nine Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - Jah will rise
  - Jirmejah

===== Usage =====

Jeremiah.
