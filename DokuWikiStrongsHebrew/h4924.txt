====== H4924: מַשְׁמָן (mashmân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h8080|H8080]];

===== Meaning =====

**fat**, i.e. (literally and abstractly) **fatness**; but usually (figuratively and concretely) a **rich** dish, a **fertile** field, a **robust** man

===== Definitions =====

  - fat
  - fatness
  - rich
  - fertile
  - robust

===== Usage =====

fat (one, -ness, -test, -test place).
