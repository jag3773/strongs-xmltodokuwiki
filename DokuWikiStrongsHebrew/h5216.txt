====== H5216: נִיר (nîyr) ======

===== Source =====

or   ; also   ; or   ; or (feminine)   ; from a primitive root (see  H5214 ;  H5135 ) properly, meaning to  ;

===== Meaning =====

glisten a  lamp  (i.e. the burner) or  light  (literally or figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - glisten
  - lamp
  - light

===== Usage =====

candle, lamp, light.
