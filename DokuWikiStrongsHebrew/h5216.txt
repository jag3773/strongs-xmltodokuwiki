====== H5216: נִיר (nîyr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or ; also ; or ; or (feminine) ; from a primitive root (see H5214; H5135) properly, meaning to **glisten**;

===== Meaning =====

a **lamp** (i.e. the burner) or **light** (literally or figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - glisten
  - lamp
  - light

===== Usage =====

candle, lamp, light.
