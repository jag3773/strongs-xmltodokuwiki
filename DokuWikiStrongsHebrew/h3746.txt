====== H3746: כָּרִי (kârîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

perhaps an abridged plural of H3733 in the sense of leader (of the flock);

===== Meaning =====

a **life-guardsman**

===== Definitions =====

  - life-guardsman

===== Usage =====

captains, Cherethites (from the margin).
