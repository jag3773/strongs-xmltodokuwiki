====== H6439: פְּנוּאֵל (Pᵉnûwʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculinenoun - proper noun - locative

===== Source =====

or (more properly,) [[en:strongs:|]]; from [[en:strongs:h6437|H6437]] and [[en:strongs:h410|H410]]; **face of God**;

===== Meaning =====

**Penuel** or **Peniel**, a place East of Jordan; also (as Penuel) the name of two Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - face of God
  - Penuel
  - Peniel

===== Usage =====

Peniel, Penuel.
