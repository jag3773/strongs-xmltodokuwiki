====== H6439: פְּנוּאֵל (Pᵉnûwʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

or (more properly,) ; from H6437 and H410; **face of God**;

===== Meaning =====

**Penuel** or **Peniel**, a place East of Jordan; also (as Penuel) the name of two Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - face of God
  - Penuel
  - Peniel

===== Usage =====

Peniel, Penuel.
