====== H6439: פְּנוּאֵל (Pᵉnûwʼêl) ======

===== Source =====

or (more properly,)   ; from  H6437  and  H410 ;  ;

===== Meaning =====

face of God Penuel  or  Peniel , a place East of Jordan; also (as Penuel) the name of two Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - face of God
  - Penuel
  - Peniel

===== Usage =====

Peniel, Penuel.
