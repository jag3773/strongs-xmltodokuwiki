====== H6152: עֲרָב (ʻĂrâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or ; from H6150 in the figurative sense of **sterility**;

===== Meaning =====

**Arab** (i.e. **Arabia**), a country East of Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - sterility
  - Arab
  - Arabia

===== Usage =====

Arabia.
