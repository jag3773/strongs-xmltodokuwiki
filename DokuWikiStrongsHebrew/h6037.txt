====== H6037: עַנְוָה (ʻanvâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:strongs:h6035|H6035]];

===== Meaning =====

**mildness** (royal); also (concretely) **oppressed**

===== Definitions =====

  - mildness
  - oppressed

===== Usage =====

gentleness, meekness.
