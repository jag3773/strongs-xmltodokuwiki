====== H4580: מָעוֹג (mâʻôwg) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h5746|H5746]]; also with [[en:strongs:h3934|H3934]] a **table-buffoon**, i.e.

===== Meaning =====

a **cake of bread**; **parasite**

===== Definitions =====

  - table-buffoon
  - cake of bread
  - parasite

===== Usage =====

cake, feast.
