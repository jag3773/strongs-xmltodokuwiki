====== H243: אַזְנוֹת תָּבוֹר (ʼAznôwth Tâbôwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from H238 and H8396; flats (i.e. tops) of Tabor (i.e. situated on it);

===== Meaning =====

**Aznoth-Tabor**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Aznoth-Tabor

===== Usage =====

Aznoth-tabor.
