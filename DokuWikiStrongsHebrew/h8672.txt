====== H8672: תֵּשַׁע (têshaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n

===== Source =====

or (masculine) ; perhaps from H8159 through the idea of a turn to the next or full number ten;

===== Meaning =====

**nine** or (ordinal) **ninth**

===== Definitions =====

  - nine
  - ninth

===== Usage =====

nine (  -teen,   -teenth, -th).
