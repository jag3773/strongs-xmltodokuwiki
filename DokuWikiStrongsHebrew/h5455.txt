====== H5455: סַבְתְּכָא (Çabtᵉkâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

probably of foreign derivation;

===== Meaning =====

**Sabteca**, the name of a son of Cush, and the region settled by him

===== Definitions =====

  - Sabteca

===== Usage =====

Sabtecha, Sabtechah.
