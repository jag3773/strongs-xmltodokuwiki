====== H1397: גֶּבֶר (geber) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H1396;

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **valiant** man or **warrior**; generally, a **person** simply

===== Definitions =====

  - valiant
  - warrior
  - person

===== Usage =====

every one, man, × mighty.
