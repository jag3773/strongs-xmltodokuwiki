====== H5601: סַפִּיר (çappîyr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H5608;

===== Meaning =====

a **gem** (perhaps used for **scratching** other substances), probably the **sapphire**

===== Definitions =====

  - gem
  - scratching
  - sapphire

===== Usage =====

sapphire.
