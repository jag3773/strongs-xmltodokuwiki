====== H4766: מַרְבֶה (marbeh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H7235;

===== Meaning =====

properly, **increasing**; as noun, **greatness**, or (adverbially) **greatly**

===== Definitions =====

  - increasing
  - greatness
  - greatly

===== Usage =====

great, increase.
