====== H4805: מְרִי (mᵉrîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h4784|H4784]];

===== Meaning =====

**bitterness**, i.e. (figuratively) **rebellion**; concretely, **bitter**, or **rebellious**

===== Definitions =====

  - bitterness
  - rebellion
  - bitter
  - rebellious

===== Usage =====

bitter, (most) rebel(-lion, -lious).
