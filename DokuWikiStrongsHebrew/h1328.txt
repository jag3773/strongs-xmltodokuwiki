====== H1328: בְּתוּאֵל (Bᵉthûwʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

apparently from the same as H1326 and H410; destroyed of God;

===== Meaning =====

**Bethuel**, the name of a nephew of Abraham, and of a place in Palestine

  - Bethuel
