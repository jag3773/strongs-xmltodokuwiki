====== H5219: נְכֹאת (nᵉkôʼth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from H5218;

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **smiting**, i.e. (concretely) an aromatic **gum** (perhaps styrax) (as powdered)

===== Definitions =====

  - smiting
  - gum

===== Usage =====

spicery(-ces).
