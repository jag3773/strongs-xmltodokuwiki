====== H1035: בֵּית לֶחֶם (Bêyth Lechem) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from H1004 and H3899; **house of bread**;

===== Meaning =====

**Beth-Lechem**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - house of bread
  - Beth-Lechem

===== Usage =====

Bethlehem.
