====== H5555: סֶלַע הַמַּחְלְקוֹת (Çelaʻ ham-machlᵉqôwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from H5553 and the plural of H4256 with the article interposed; rock of the divisions;

===== Meaning =====

**Sela-ham-Machlekoth**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Sela-ham-Machlekoth

===== Usage =====

Sela-hammalekoth.
