====== H6203: עֹרֶף (ʻôreph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H6202;

===== Meaning =====

the **nape** or back of the neck (as **declining**); hence, the **back** generally (whether literal or figurative)

===== Definitions =====

  - nape
  - declining
  - back

===== Usage =====

back ((stiff-) neck((-ed).
