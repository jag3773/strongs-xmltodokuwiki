====== H6006: עָמַס (ʻâmaç) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **load**, i.e. **impose** a burden (or figuratively, infliction)

===== Definitions =====

  - load
  - impose

===== Usage =====

be borne, (heavy) burden (self), lade, load, put.
