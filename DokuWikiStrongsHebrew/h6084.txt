====== H6084: עׇפְרָה (ʻOphrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:strongs:h6082|H6082]]; **female fawn**;

===== Meaning =====

**Ophrah**, the name of an Israelite and of two places in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - female fawn
  - Ophrah

===== Usage =====

Ophrah.
