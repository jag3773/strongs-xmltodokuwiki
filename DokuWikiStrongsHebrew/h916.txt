====== H916: בְּדֹלַח (bᵉdôlach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

probably from H914;

===== Meaning =====

**something in pieces**, i.e. **bdellium**, a (fragrant) **gum** (perhaps amber); others a **pearl**

===== Definitions =====

  - something in pieces
  - bdellium
  - gum
  - pearl

===== Usage =====

bdellium.
