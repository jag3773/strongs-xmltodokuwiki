====== H5989: עַמִּיהוּד (ʻAmmîyhûwd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from H5971 and H1935; **people of splendor**;

===== Meaning =====

**Ammihud**, the name of three Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - people of splendor
  - Ammihud

===== Usage =====

Ammihud.
