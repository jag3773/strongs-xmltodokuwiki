====== H5483: סוּס (çûwç) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; from an unused root meaning to **skip** (properly, for joy);

===== Meaning =====

a **horse** (as leaping); also a **swallow** (from its rapid **flight**)

===== Definitions =====

  - skip
  - horse
  - swallow
  - flight

===== Usage =====

crane, horse (-back, -hoof). Compare
