====== H6630: צַאֲנָן (Tsaʼănân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from the same as H6629 used denominatively; **sheep pasture**;

===== Meaning =====

**Zaanan**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - sheep pasture
  - Zaanan

===== Usage =====

Zaanan.
