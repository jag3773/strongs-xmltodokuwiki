====== H3018: יְגִיעַ (yᵉgîyaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h3021|H3021]];

===== Meaning =====

**toil**; hence, a **work**, **produce**, **property** (as the result of labor)

===== Definitions =====

  - toil
  - work
  - produce
  - property

===== Usage =====

labour, work.
