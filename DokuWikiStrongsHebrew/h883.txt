====== H883: בְּאֵר לַחַי רֹאִי (Bᵉʼêr la-Chay Rôʼîy) ======

===== Source =====

from  H875  and  H2416  (with prefix) and  H7203 ; well of a living (One) my Seer;

===== Meaning =====

Beer-Lachai-Roi , a place in the Desert

===== Definitions =====

  - Beer-Lachai-Roi

===== Usage =====

Beer-lahai-roi.
