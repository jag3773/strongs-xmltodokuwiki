====== H1353: גְּאֻלָּה (gᵉʼullâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine passive participle of H1350;

===== Meaning =====

**redemption** (including the right and the object); by implication, **relationship**

===== Definitions =====

  - redemption
  - relationship

===== Usage =====

kindred, redeem, redemption, right.
