====== H4283: מׇחֳרָת (mochŏrâth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

or ; (1 Samuel 30:17), feminine from the same as H4279;

===== Meaning =====

the **morrow** or (adverbially) **tomorrow**

===== Definitions =====

  - morrow
  - tomorrow

===== Usage =====

morrow, next day.
