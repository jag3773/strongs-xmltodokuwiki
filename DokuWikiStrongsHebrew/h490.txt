====== H490: אַלְמָנָה (ʼalmânâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:strongs:h488|H488]];

===== Meaning =====

a **widow**; also a **desolate place**

===== Definitions =====

  - widow
  - desolate place

===== Usage =====

desolate house (palace), widow.
