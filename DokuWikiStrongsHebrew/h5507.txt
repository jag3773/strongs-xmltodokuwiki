====== H5507: סֹחֵרָה (çôchêrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

properly, active participle feminine of H5503;

===== Meaning =====

something **surrounding** the person, i.e. a **shield**

===== Definitions =====

  - surrounding
  - shield

===== Usage =====

buckler.
