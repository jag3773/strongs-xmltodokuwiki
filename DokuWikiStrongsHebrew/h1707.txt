====== H1707: דַּבֶּשֶׁת (dabbesheth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

intensive from the same as [[en:strongs:h1706|H1706]];

===== Meaning =====

a sticky **mass**, i.e. the **hump** of a camel

===== Definitions =====

  - mass
  - hump

===== Usage =====

hunch (of a camel).
