====== H4735: מִקְנֶה (miqneh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H7069;

===== Meaning =====

something **bought**, i.e. **property**, but only live **stock**; abstractly, **acquisition**

===== Definitions =====

  - bought
  - property
  - stock
  - acquisition

===== Usage =====

cattle, flock, herd, possession, purchase, substance.
