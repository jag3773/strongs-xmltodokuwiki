====== H3799: כָּתַם (kâtham) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **carve** or **engrave**, i.e. (by implication) to **inscribe indelibly**

===== Definitions =====

  - carve
  - engrave
  - inscribe indelibly

===== Usage =====

mark.
