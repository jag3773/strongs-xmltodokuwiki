====== H8429: תְּוַהּ (tᵉvahh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to H8539 or perhaps to H7582 through the idea of sweeping to ruin (compare H8428);

===== Meaning =====

to **amaze**, i.e. (reflex. by implication) **take alarm**

===== Definitions =====

  - amaze
  - take alarm

===== Usage =====

be astonied.
