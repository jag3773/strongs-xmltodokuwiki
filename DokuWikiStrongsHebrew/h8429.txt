====== H8429: תְּוַהּ (tᵉvahh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to [[en:strongs:h8539|H8539]] or perhaps to [[en:strongs:h7582|H7582]] through the idea of sweeping to ruin (compare [[en:strongs:h8428|H8428]]);

===== Meaning =====

to **amaze**, i.e. (reflex. by implication) **take alarm**

===== Definitions =====

  - amaze
  - take alarm

===== Usage =====

be astonied.
