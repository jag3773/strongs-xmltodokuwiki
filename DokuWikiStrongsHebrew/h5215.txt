====== H5215: נִיר (nîyr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; from [[en:strongs:h5214|H5214]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **plowing**, i.e. (concretely) **freshly plowed land**

===== Definitions =====

  - plowing
  - freshly plowed land

===== Usage =====

fallow ground, plowing, tillage.
