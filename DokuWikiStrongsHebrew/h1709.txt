====== H1709: דָּג (dâg) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or (fully) ; (Nehemiah 13:16), from H1711; a fish (as prolific); or perhaps rather from H1672 (as timid); but still better from H1672 (in the sense of squirming, i.e. moving by the vibratory action of the tail);

===== Meaning =====

a **fish** (often used collectively)

===== Definitions =====

  - fish

===== Usage =====

fish.
