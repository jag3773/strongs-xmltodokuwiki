====== H3591: כִּידוֹן (kîydôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from the same as H3589; (perhaps smaller than H2595)

===== Meaning =====

properly, something to **strike** with, i.e. a **dart**

===== Definitions =====

  - strike
  - dart

===== Usage =====

lance, shield, spear, target.
