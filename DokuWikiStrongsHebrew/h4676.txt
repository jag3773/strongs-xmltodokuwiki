====== H4676: מַצֵּבָה (matstsêbâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine (causatively) participle of H5324;

===== Meaning =====

something **stationed**, i.e. a **column** or (memorial stone); by analogy, an **idol**

===== Definitions =====

  - stationed
  - column
  - idol

===== Usage =====

garrison, (standing) image, pillar.
