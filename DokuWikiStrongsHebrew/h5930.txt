====== H5930: עֹלָה (ʻôlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

or ; feminine active participle of H5927;

===== Meaning =====

a **step** or (collectively, stairs, as ascending); usually a **holocaust** (as going up in smoke)

===== Definitions =====

  - step
  - holocaust

===== Usage =====

ascent, burnt offering (sacrifice), go up to. See also
