====== H4467: מַמְלָכָה (mamlâkâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h4427|H4427]];

===== Meaning =====

**dominion**, i.e. (abstractly) the estate (**rule**) or (concretely) the country (**realm**)

===== Definitions =====

  - dominion
  - rule
  - realm

===== Usage =====

kingdom, king's, reign, royal.
