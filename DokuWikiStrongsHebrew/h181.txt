====== H181: אוּד (ʼûwd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **rake** together;

===== Meaning =====

a **poker** (for **turning** or **gathering** embers)

===== Definitions =====

  - rake
  - poker
  - turning
  - gathering

===== Usage =====

(fire-) brand.
