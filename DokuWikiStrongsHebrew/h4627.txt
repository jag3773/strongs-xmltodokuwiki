====== H4627: מַעֲרָב (maʻărâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h6148|H6148]], in the sense of trading;

===== Meaning =====

**traffic**; by implication, **mercantile goods**

===== Definitions =====

  - traffic
  - mercantile goods

===== Usage =====

market, merchandise.
