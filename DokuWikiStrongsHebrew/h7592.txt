====== H7592: שָׁאַל (shâʼal) ======

===== Source =====

or   ; a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to  inquire ; by implication, to  request ; by extension, to  demand

===== Definitions =====

  - inquire
  - request
  - demand

===== Usage =====

ask (counsel, on), beg, borrow, lay to charge, consult, demand, desire, × earnestly, enquire,   greet, obtain leave, lend, pray, request, require,   salute, × straitly, × surely, wish.
