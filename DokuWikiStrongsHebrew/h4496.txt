====== H4496: מְנוּחָה (mᵉnûwchâh) ======

===== Source =====

or   ; feminine of  H4495 ;

===== Meaning =====

repose  or (adverbially)  peacefully ; figuratively,  consolation  (specifically,  matrimony ); hence (concretely) an  abode

===== Definitions =====

  - repose
  - peacefully
  - consolation
  - matrimony
  - abode

===== Usage =====

comfortable, ease, quiet, rest(-ing place), still.
