====== H4392: מָלֵא (mâlêʼ) ======

===== Source =====

from  H4390 ;

===== Meaning =====

full  (literally or figuratively) or  filling  (literally); also (concretely)  fulness ; adverbially,  fully

===== Definitions =====

  - full
  - filling
  - fulness
  - fully

===== Usage =====

× she that was with child, fill(-ed, -ed with), full(-ly), multitude, as is worth.
