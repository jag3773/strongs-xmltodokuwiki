====== H1189: בַּעַל צְפוֹן (Baʻal Tsᵉphôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from H1168 and H6828 (in the sense of **cold**) (according to others an Egyptian form of **Typhon**, the destroyer); **Baal of winter**;

===== Meaning =====

**Baal-Tsephon**, a place in Eqypt

===== Definitions =====

  - cold
  - Typhon
  - Baal of winter
  - Baal-Tsephon

===== Usage =====

Baal-zephon.
