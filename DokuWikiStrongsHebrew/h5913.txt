====== H5913: עָכַס (ʻâkaç) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root; properly, to tie, specifically, with fetters; but used only as denominative from H5914;

===== Meaning =====

to **put on anklets**

===== Definitions =====

  - put on anklets

===== Usage =====

make a tinkling ornament.
