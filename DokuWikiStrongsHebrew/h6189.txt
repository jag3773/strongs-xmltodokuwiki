====== H6189: עָרֵל (ʻârêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h6188|H6188]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **exposed**, i.e. projecting loose (as to the prepuce); used only technically, **uncircumcised** (i.e. still having the prepuce uncurtailed)

===== Definitions =====

  - exposed
  - uncircumcised

===== Usage =====

uncircumcised (person).
