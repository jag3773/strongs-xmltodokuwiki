====== H4138: מוֹלֶדֶת (môwledeth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h3205|H3205]];

===== Meaning =====

**nativity** (plural birth-place); by implication, **lineage**, **native country**; also **offspring**, **family**

===== Definitions =====

  - nativity
  - lineage
  - native country
  - offspring
  - family

===== Usage =====

begotten, born, issue, kindred, native(-ity).
