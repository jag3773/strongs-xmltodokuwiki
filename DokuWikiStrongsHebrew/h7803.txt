====== H7803: שׁוּתֶלַח (Shûwthelach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

probably from H7582 and the same as H8520; **crash of breakage**;

===== Meaning =====

**Shuthelach**, the name of two Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - crash of breakage
  - Shuthelach

===== Usage =====

Shuthelah.
