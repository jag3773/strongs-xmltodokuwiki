====== H3187: יָחַשׂ (yâchas) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root; to **sprout**; used only as denominative from H3188;

===== Meaning =====

to **enroll** by pedigree

===== Definitions =====

  - sprout
  - enroll

===== Usage =====

(number after, number throughout the) genealogy (to be reckoned), be reckoned by genealogies.
