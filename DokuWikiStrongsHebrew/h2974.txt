====== H2974: יָאַל (yâʼal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root (probably rather the same as [[en:strongs:h2973|H2973]] through the idea of mental weakness);

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **yield**, especially **assent**; hence (pos.) to **undertake** as an act of volition

===== Definitions =====

  - yield
  - assent
  - undertake

===== Usage =====

assay, begin, be content, please, take upon, × willingly, would.
