====== H2755: חֲרֵי־יוֹנִים (chărêy-yôwnîym) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from the plural of [[en:strongs:h2716|H2716]] and the plural of [[en:strongs:h3123|H3123]]; or perhaps rather the plural of a single word [[en:strongs:|]], probably a kind of vegetable

**excrements of doves**

===== Definitions =====

  - excrements of doves

===== Usage =====

doves' dung.
