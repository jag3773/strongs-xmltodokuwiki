====== H2755: חֲרֵי־יוֹנִים (chărêy-yôwnîym) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from the plural of H2716 and the plural of H3123; or perhaps rather the plural of a single word , probably a kind of vegetable

===== Meaning =====

**excrements of doves**

===== Definitions =====

  - excrements of doves

===== Usage =====

doves' dung.
