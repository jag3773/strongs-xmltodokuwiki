====== H6188: עָרֵל (ʻârêl) ======

===== Source =====

a primitive root; properly, to strip; but used as denominative from  H6189 ;

===== Meaning =====

to  expose  or  remove the prepuce , whether literal (to go naked) or figurative (to refrain from using)

===== Definitions =====

  - expose
  - remove the prepuce

===== Usage =====

count uncircumcised, foreskin to be uncovered.
