====== H1627: גָּרוֹן (gârôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or (shortened) ; from H1641; (compare H1621)

===== Meaning =====

the **throat** (as **roughened** by swallowing)

===== Definitions =====

  - throat
  - roughened

===== Usage =====

× aloud, mouth, neck, throat.
