====== H6879: צָרַע (tsâraʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **scourge**, i.e. (intransitive and figurative) to **be stricken with leprosy**

===== Definitions =====

  - scourge
  - be stricken with leprosy

===== Usage =====

leper, leprous.
