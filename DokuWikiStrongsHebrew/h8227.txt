====== H8227: שָׁפָן (shâphân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h8226|H8226]];

===== Meaning =====

a species of **rock-rabbit** (from its **hiding**), i.e. probably the **hyrax**

===== Definitions =====

  - rock-rabbit
  - hiding
  - hyrax

===== Usage =====

coney.
