====== H7100: קֶצַח (qetsach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from an unused root apparently meaning to **incise**;

===== Meaning =====

**fennel-flower** (from its **pungency**)

===== Definitions =====

  - incise
  - fennel-flower
  - pungency

===== Usage =====

fitches.
