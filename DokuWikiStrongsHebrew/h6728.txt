====== H6728: צִיִּי (tsîyîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from the same as H6723;

===== Meaning =====

a **desert-dweller**, i.e. **nomad** or wild **beast**

===== Definitions =====

  - desert-dweller
  - nomad
  - beast

===== Usage =====

wild beast of the desert, that dwell in (inhabiting) the wilderness.
