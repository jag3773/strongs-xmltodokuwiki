====== H4599: מַעְיָן (maʻyân) ======

===== Source =====

or   ; (Psalm 114:8), or (feminine)   ; from  H5869  (as a denominative in the sense of a spring);

===== Meaning =====

a  fountain  (also collectively), figuratively, a  source  (of satisfaction)

===== Definitions =====

  - fountain
  - source

===== Usage =====

fountain, spring, well.
