====== H386: אֵיתָן (ʼêythân) ======

===== Source =====

or (shortened)    ; from an unused root (meaning to  );

===== Meaning =====

continue permanence ; hence (concrete)  permanent ; specifically a  chieftain

===== Definitions =====

  - continue
  - permanence
  - permanent
  - chieftain

===== Usage =====

hard, mighty, rough, strength, strong.
