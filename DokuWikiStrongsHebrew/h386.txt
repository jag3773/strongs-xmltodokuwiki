====== H386: אֵיתָן (ʼêythân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

or (shortened) [[en:strongs:|]] ; from an unused root (meaning to **continue**);

===== Meaning =====

**permanence**; hence (concrete) **permanent**; specifically a **chieftain**

===== Definitions =====

  - continue
  - permanence
  - permanent
  - chieftain

===== Usage =====

hard, mighty, rough, strength, strong.
