====== H5333: נְצִיב (nᵉtsîyb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; from [[en:strongs:h5324|H5324]];

===== Meaning =====

something **stationary**, i.e. a **prefect**, a military **post**, a **statue**

===== Definitions =====

  - stationary
  - prefect
  - post
  - statue

===== Usage =====

garrison, officer, pillar.
