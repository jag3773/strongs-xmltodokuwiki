====== H3849: לֹג (lôg) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from an unused root apparently meaning to deepen or hollow (like [[en:strongs:h3537|H3537]]);

===== Meaning =====

a **log** or **measure for liquids**

===== Definitions =====

  - log
  - measure for liquids

===== Usage =====

log (of oil).
