====== H7247: רִבְלָה (Riblâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - locative

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **be fruitful**; **fertile**;

===== Meaning =====

**Riblah**, a place in Syria

===== Definitions =====

  - be fruitful
  - fertile
  - Riblah

===== Usage =====

Riblah.
