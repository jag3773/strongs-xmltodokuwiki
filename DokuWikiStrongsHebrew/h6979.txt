====== H6979: קוּר (qûwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root; also denominative from [[en:strongs:h7023|H7023]]

===== Meaning =====

to **trench**; by implication, to **throw forth**; to **wall up**, whether literal (to build a wall) or figurative (to estop)

===== Definitions =====

  - trench
  - throw forth
  - wall up

===== Usage =====

break down, cast out, destroy, dig.
