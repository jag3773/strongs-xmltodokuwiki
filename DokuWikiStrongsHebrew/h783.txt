====== H783: אַרְתַּחְשַׁשְׁתָּא (ʼArtachshashtâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; or by permutation [[en:strongs:|]]; of foreign origin;

===== Meaning =====

**Artachshasta** (or Artaxerxes), a title (rather than name) of several Persian kings

===== Definitions =====

  - Artachshasta

===== Usage =====

Artaxerxes.
