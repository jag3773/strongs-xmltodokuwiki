====== H817: אָשָׁם (ʼâshâm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H816;

===== Meaning =====

**guilt**; by implication, a **fault**; also a **sin-offering**

===== Definitions =====

  - guilt
  - fault
  - sin-offering

===== Usage =====

guiltiness, (offering for) sin, trespass (offering).
