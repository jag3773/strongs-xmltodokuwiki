====== H2606: חֲנַנְאֵל (Chănanʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from H2603 and H410; God has favored;

===== Meaning =====

**Chananel**, probably an Israelite, from whom a tower of Jerusalem was named

===== Definitions =====

  - Chananel

===== Usage =====

Hananeel.
