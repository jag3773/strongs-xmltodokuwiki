====== H7951: שָׁלָה (shâlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; (Job 3:26), a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

**to be tranquil**, i.e. **secure** or **successful**

===== Definitions =====

  - to be tranquil
  - secure
  - successful

===== Usage =====

be happy, prosper, be in safety.
