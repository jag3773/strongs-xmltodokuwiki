====== H3539: כַּדְכֹּד (kadkôd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:strongs:h3537|H3537]] in the sense of striking fire from a metal forged;

===== Meaning =====

a **sparkling gem**, probably the **ruby**

===== Definitions =====

  - sparkling gem
  - ruby

===== Usage =====

agate.
