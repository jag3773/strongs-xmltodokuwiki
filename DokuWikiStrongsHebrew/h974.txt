====== H974: בָּחַן (bâchan) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **test** (especially metals); generally and figuratively, to **investigate**

===== Definitions =====

  - test
  - investigate

===== Usage =====

examine, prove, tempt, try (trial).
