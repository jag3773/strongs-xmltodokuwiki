====== H4029: מִגְדַּל־עֵדֶר (Migdal-ʻÊder) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n

===== Source =====

from H4026 and H5739; **tower of a flock**;

===== Meaning =====

**Migdal-Eder**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - tower of a flock
  - Migdal-Eder

===== Usage =====

Migdal-eder, tower of the flock.
