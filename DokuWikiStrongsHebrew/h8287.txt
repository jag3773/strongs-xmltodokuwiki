====== H8287: שָׁרוּחֶן (Shârûwchen) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

probably from H8281 (in the sense of dwelling (compare H8271) and H2580; **abode of pleasure**;

===== Meaning =====

**Sharuchen**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - abode of pleasure
  - Sharuchen

===== Usage =====

Sharuhen.
