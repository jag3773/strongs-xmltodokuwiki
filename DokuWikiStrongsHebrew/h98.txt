====== H98: אֲגַם (ʼăgam) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from an unused root (meaning to **collect** as water);

===== Meaning =====

a **marsh**; hence a **rush** (as growing in swamps); hence a **stockade** of reeds

===== Definitions =====

  - collect
  - marsh
  - rush
  - stockade

===== Usage =====

pond, pool, standing (water).
