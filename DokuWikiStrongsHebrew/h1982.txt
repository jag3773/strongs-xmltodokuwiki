====== H1982: הֵלֶךְ (hêlek) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H1980;

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **journey**, i.e. (by implication) a **wayfarer**; also a **flowing**

===== Definitions =====

  - journey
  - wayfarer
  - flowing

===== Usage =====

× dropped, traveller.
