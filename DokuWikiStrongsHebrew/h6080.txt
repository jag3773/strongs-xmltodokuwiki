====== H6080: עָפַר (ʻâphar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

; a primitive root; meaning either to be gray or perhaps rather to pulverize; used only as denominative from H6083,

===== Meaning =====

to **be dust**

===== Definitions =====

  - be dust

===== Usage =====

cast (dust).
