====== H1337: בַּת רַבִּים (Bath Rabbîym) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - locative

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h1323|H1323]] and a masculine plural from [[en:strongs:h7227|H7227]];

===== Meaning =====

the **daughter** (i.e. city) **of Rabbah**

===== Definitions =====

  - daughter
  - of Rabbah

===== Usage =====

Bath-rabbim.
