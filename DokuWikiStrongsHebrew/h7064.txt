====== H7064: קֵן (qên) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

contracted from [[en:strongs:h7077|H7077]];

===== Meaning =====

a **nest** (as fixed), sometimes including the **nestlings**; figuratively, a **chamber** or **dwelling**

===== Definitions =====

  - nest
  - nestlings
  - chamber
  - dwelling

===== Usage =====

nest, room.
