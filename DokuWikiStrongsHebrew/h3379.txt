====== H3379: יָרׇבְעָם (Yârobʻâm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from H7378 and H5971; (the) **people will contend**;

===== Meaning =====

**Jarobam**, the name of two Israelite kings

===== Definitions =====

  - people will contend
  - Jarobam

===== Usage =====

Jeroboam.
