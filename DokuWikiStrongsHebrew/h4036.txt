====== H4036: מָגוֹר מִסָּבִיב (Mâgôwr miç-Çâbîyb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from H4032 and H5439 with the preposition inserted; affright from around;

===== Meaning =====

**Magor-mis-Sabib**, a symbolic name of Pashur

===== Definitions =====

  - Magor-mis-Sabib

===== Usage =====

Magormissabib.
