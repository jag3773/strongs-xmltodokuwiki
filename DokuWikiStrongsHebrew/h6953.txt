====== H6953: קֹהֶלֶת (qôheleth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

feminine of active participle from H6950;

===== Meaning =====

a (female) **assembler** (i.e. lecturer); abstractly, **preaching** (used as a 'nom de plume', Koheleth)

===== Definitions =====

  - assembler
  - preaching

===== Usage =====

preacher.
