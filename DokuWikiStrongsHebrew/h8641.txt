====== H8641: תְּרוּמָה (tᵉrûwmâh) ======

===== Source =====

or   ; (Deuteronomy 12:11), from  H7311 ;

===== Meaning =====

a  present  (as offered  up ), especially in  sacrifice  or as  tribute

===== Definitions =====

  - present
  - up
  - sacrifice
  - tribute

===== Usage =====

gift, heave offering (shoulder), oblation, offered(-ing).
