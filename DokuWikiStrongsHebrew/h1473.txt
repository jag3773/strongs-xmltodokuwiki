====== H1473: גּוֹלָה (gôwlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

or (shortened) ; active participle feminine of H1540;

===== Meaning =====

**exile**; concretely and collectively **exiles**

===== Definitions =====

  - exile
  - exiles

===== Usage =====

(carried away), captive(-ity), removing.
