====== H1819: דָּמָה (dâmâh) ======

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to  compare ; by implication, to  resemble ,  liken ,  consider

===== Definitions =====

  - compare
  - resemble
  - liken
  - consider

===== Usage =====

compare, devise, (be) like(-n), mean, think, use similitudes.
