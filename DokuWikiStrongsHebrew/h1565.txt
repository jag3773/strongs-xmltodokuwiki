====== H1565: גַּלְמוּד (galmûwd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

probably by prolonged from [[en:strongs:h1563|H1563]];

===== Meaning =====

**sterile** (as **wrapped** up too hard); figuratively, **desolate**

===== Definitions =====

  - sterile
  - wrapped
  - desolate

===== Usage =====

desolate, solitary.
