====== H2734: חָרָה (chârâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root (compare [[en:strongs:h2787|H2787]]);

===== Meaning =====

to **glow** or **grow warm**; figuratively (usually) to **blaze up**, of anger, zeal, jealousy

===== Definitions =====

  - glow
  - grow warm
  - blaze up
