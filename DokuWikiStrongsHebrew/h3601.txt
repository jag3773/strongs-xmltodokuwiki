====== H3601: כִּישׁוֹר (kîyshôwr) ======

===== Source =====

from  H3787 ; compare  H6418

===== Meaning =====

literally a  director , i.e. the  spindle  or  shank of a distaff , by which it is twirled

===== Definitions =====

  - director
  - spindle
  - shank of a distaff

===== Usage =====

spindle.
