====== H2420: חִידָה (chîydâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from H2330;

===== Meaning =====

a **puzzle**, hence, a **trick**, **conundrum**, sententious **maxim**

===== Definitions =====

  - puzzle
  - trick
  - conundrum
  - maxim

===== Usage =====

dark saying (sentence, speech), hard question, proverb, riddle.
