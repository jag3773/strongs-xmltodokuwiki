====== H7175: קֶרֶשׁ (qeresh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **split** off;

===== Meaning =====

a **slab** or plank; by implication, a **deck** of a ship

===== Definitions =====

  - split
  - slab
  - deck

===== Usage =====

bench, board.
