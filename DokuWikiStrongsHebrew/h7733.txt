====== H7733: שׁוֹבֵק (Shôwbêq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

active participle from a primitive root meaning to leave (compare [[en:strongs:h7662|H7662]]); **forsaking**;

===== Meaning =====

**Shobek**, an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - forsaking
  - Shobek

===== Usage =====

Shobek.
