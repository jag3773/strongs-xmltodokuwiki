====== H2904: טוּל (ṭûwl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **pitch** over or **reel**; hence (transitively) to **cast** down or out

===== Definitions =====

  - pitch
  - reel
  - cast

===== Usage =====

carry away, (utterly) cast (down, forth, out), send out.
