====== H798: אַשְׁדּוֹת הַפִּסְגָּה (ʼAshdôwth hap-Piçgâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from the plural of [[en:strongs:h794|H794]] and [[en:strongs:h6449|H6449]] with the article interposed; ravines of the Pisgah;

===== Meaning =====

**Ashdoth-Pisgah**, a place east of the Jordan

===== Definitions =====

  - Ashdoth-Pisgah

===== Usage =====

Ashdoth-pisgah.
