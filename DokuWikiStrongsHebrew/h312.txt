====== H312: אַחֵר (ʼachêr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h309|H309]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **hinder**; generally, **next**, **other**, etc.

===== Definitions =====

  - hinder
  - next
  - other

===== Usage =====

(an-) other man, following, next, strange.
