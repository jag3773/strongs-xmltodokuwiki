====== H5473: סוּג (çûwg) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root (probably rather identical with H5472 through the idea of shrinking from a hedge; compare H7735);

===== Meaning =====

to **hem in**, i.e. **bind**

===== Definitions =====

  - hem in
  - bind

===== Usage =====

set about.
