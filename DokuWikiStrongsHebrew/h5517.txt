====== H5517: סִיעָא (Çîyʻâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

or ; from an unused root meaning to **converse**; **congregation**;

===== Meaning =====

**Sia** or **Siaha**, one of the Nethinim

===== Definitions =====

  - converse
  - congregation
  - Sia
  - Siaha

===== Usage =====

Sia, Siaha.
