====== H155: אַדֶּרֶת (ʼaddereth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine of H117; ; also the same as H145

===== Meaning =====

something **ample** (as a large vine, a wide dress)

===== Definitions =====

  - ample

===== Usage =====

garment, glory, goodly, mantle, robe.
