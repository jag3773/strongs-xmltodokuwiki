====== H33: אֲבִי הָעֶזְרִי (ʼĂbîy hâ-ʻEzrîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from H44 with the article inserted; father of the Ezrite;

===== Meaning =====

an **Abiezrite** or descendant of Abiezer

===== Definitions =====

  - Abiezrite

===== Usage =====

Abiezrite.
