====== H3231: יָמַן (yâman) ======

===== Source =====

a primitive root; to   (physically)   (i.e. firm); but used only as denominative from  H3225  and transitive,

===== Meaning =====

be right to  be right-handed  or  take the right-hand  side

===== Definitions =====

  - be
  - right
  - be right-handed
  - take the right-hand

===== Usage =====

go (turn) to (on, use) the right hand.
