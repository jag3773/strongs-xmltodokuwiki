====== H3231: יָמַן (yâman) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root; to **be** (physically) **right** (i.e. firm); but used only as denominative from H3225 and transitive,

===== Meaning =====

to **be right-handed** or **take the right-hand** side

===== Definitions =====

  - be
  - right
  - be right-handed
  - take the right-hand

===== Usage =====

go (turn) to (on, use) the right hand.
