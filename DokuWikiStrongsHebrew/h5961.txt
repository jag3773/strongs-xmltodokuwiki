====== H5961: עֲלָמוֹת (ʻĂlâmôwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

plural of H5959;

===== Meaning =====

properly, **girls**, i.e. the **soprano** or female voice, perhaps **falsetto**

===== Definitions =====

  - girls
  - soprano
  - falsetto

===== Usage =====

Alamoth.
