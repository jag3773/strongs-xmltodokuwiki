====== H6341: פַּח (pach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h6351|H6351]];

===== Meaning =====

a (metallic) **sheet** (as pounded thin); also a **spring net** (as spread out like a lamina)

===== Definitions =====

  - sheet
  - spring net

===== Usage =====

gin, (thin) plate, snare.
