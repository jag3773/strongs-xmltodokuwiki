====== H7387: רֵיקָם (rêyqâm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adv

===== Source =====

from H7386;

===== Meaning =====

**emptily**; figuratively (objective) **ineffectually**, (subjective) **undeservedly**

===== Definitions =====

  - emptily
  - ineffectually
  - undeservedly

===== Usage =====

without cause, empty, in vain, void.
