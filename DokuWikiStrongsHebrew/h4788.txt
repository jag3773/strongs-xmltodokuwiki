====== H4788: מָרוּד (mârûwd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h7300|H7300]] in the sense of **maltreatment**;

===== Meaning =====

an **outcast**; (abstractly) **destitution**

===== Definitions =====

  - maltreatment
  - outcast
  - destitution

===== Usage =====

cast out, misery.
