====== H2108: זוּלָה (zûwlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h2107|H2107]];

===== Meaning =====

probably **scattering**, i.e. **removal**; used adverbially, **except**

===== Definitions =====

  - scattering
  - removal
  - except

===== Usage =====

beside, but, only, save.
