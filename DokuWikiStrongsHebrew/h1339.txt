====== H1339: בַּת־שֶׁבַע (Bath-Shebaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-f

===== Source =====

from H1323 and H7651 (in the sense of H7650); daughter of an oath;

===== Meaning =====

**Bath-Sheba**, the mother of Solomon

===== Definitions =====

  - Bath-Sheba

===== Usage =====

Bath-sheba.
