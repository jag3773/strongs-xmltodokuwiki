====== H4846: מְרֹרָה (mᵉrôrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; from [[en:strongs:h4843|H4843]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **bitterness**; concretely, a **bitter thing**; specifically **bile**; also **venom** (of a serpent)

===== Definitions =====

  - bitterness
  - bitter thing
  - bile
  - venom

===== Usage =====

bitter (thing), gall.
