====== H5863: עִיֵּי הָעֲבָרִים (ʻÎyêy hâ-ʻĂbârîym) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from the plural of H5856 and the plural of the active participle of H5674 with the article interposed; **ruins of the passers**;

===== Meaning =====

**Ije-ha-Abarim**, a place near Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - ruins of the passers
  - Ije-ha-Abarim

===== Usage =====

Ije-abarim.
