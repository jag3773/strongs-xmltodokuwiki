====== H403: אָכֵן (ʼâkên) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adverb

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h3559|H3559]] (compare [[en:strongs:h3651|H3651]]);

===== Meaning =====

**firmly**; figuratively, **surely**; also (adversative) **but**

===== Definitions =====

  - firmly
  - surely
  - but

===== Usage =====

but, certainly, nevertheless, surely, truly, verily.
