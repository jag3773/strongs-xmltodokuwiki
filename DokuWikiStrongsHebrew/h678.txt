====== H678: אָצִיל (ʼâtsîyl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H680 (in its secondary sense of **separation**);

===== Meaning =====

an **extremity** (Isaiah 41:9), also a **noble**

===== Definitions =====

  - separation
  - extremity
  - noble

===== Usage =====

chief man, noble.
