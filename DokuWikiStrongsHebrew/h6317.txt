====== H6317: פּוּטִיאֵל (Pûwṭîyʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from an unused root (probably meaning to disparage) and H410; **contempt of God**;

===== Meaning =====

**Putiel**, an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - contempt of God
  - Putiel

===== Usage =====

Putiel.
