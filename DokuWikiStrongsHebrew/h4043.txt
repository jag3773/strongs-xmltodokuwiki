====== H4043: מָגֵן (mâgên) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

also (in plural) feminine [[en:strongs:|]]; from [[en:strongs:h1598|H1598]];

===== Meaning =====

a **shield** (i.e. the small one or buckler); figuratively, a **protector**; also the scaly **hide** of the crocodile

===== Definitions =====

  - shield
  - protector
  - hide

===== Usage =====

× armed, buckler, defence, ruler,   scale, shield.
