====== H2976: יָאַשׁ (yâʼash) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **desist**, i.e. (figuratively) to **despond**

===== Definitions =====

  - desist
  - despond

===== Usage =====

(cause to) despair, one that is desperate, be no hope.
