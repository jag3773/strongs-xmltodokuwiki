====== H5394: נָשַׁל (nâshal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **pluck off**, i.e. **divest**, **eject** or **drop**

===== Definitions =====

  - pluck off
  - divest
  - eject
  - drop

===== Usage =====

cast (out), drive, loose, put off (out), slip.
