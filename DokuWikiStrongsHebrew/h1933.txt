====== H1933: הָוָא (hâvâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; a primitive root (compare [[en:strongs:h183|H183]], [[en:strongs:h1961|H1961]]) supposed to mean properly, to breathe;

===== Meaning =====

to **be** (in the sense of existence)

===== Definitions =====

  - be

===== Usage =====

be, × have.
