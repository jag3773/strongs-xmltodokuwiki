====== H5183: נַחַת (Nachath) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n

===== Source =====

from H5182; also (intransitively; perhaps from H5117)

===== Meaning =====

a **descent**, i.e. imposition, unfavorable (**punishment**) or favorable (**food**); **restfulness**

===== Definitions =====

  - descent
  - punishment
  - food
  - restfulness

===== Usage =====

lighting down, quiet(-ness), to rest, be set on.
