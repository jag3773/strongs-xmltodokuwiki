====== H7759: שׁוּלַמִּית (Shûwlammîyth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

from H7999;

===== Meaning =====

**peaceful** (with the article always prefixed, making it a pet name); the **Shulammith**, an epithet of Solomon's queen

===== Definitions =====

  - peaceful
  - Shulammith

===== Usage =====

Shulamite.
