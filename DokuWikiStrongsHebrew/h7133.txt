====== H7133: קׇרְבָּן (qorbân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or ; from H7126;

===== Meaning =====

something **brought near** the altar, i.e. a sacrificial **present**

===== Definitions =====

  - brought near
  - present

===== Usage =====

oblation, that is offered, offering.
