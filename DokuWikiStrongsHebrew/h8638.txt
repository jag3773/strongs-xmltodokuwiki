====== H8638: תִּרְגַּם (tirgam) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a denominative from [[en:strongs:h7275|H7275]] in the sense of throwing over;

to **transfer**, i.e. **translate**

===== Definitions =====

  - translate

===== Usage =====
