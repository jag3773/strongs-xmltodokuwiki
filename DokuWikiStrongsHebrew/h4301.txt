====== H4301: מַטְמוֹן (maṭmôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; or [[en:strongs:|]]; from [[en:strongs:h2934|H2934]];

===== Meaning =====

a **secret** storehouse; hence, a **secreted** valuable (buried); generally **money**

===== Definitions =====

  - secret
  - secreted
  - money

===== Usage =====

hidden riches, (hid) treasure(-s).
