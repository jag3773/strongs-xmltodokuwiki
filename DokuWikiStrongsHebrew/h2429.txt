====== H2429: חַיִל (chayil) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to [[en:strongs:h2428|H2428]];

===== Meaning =====

an **army**, or **strength**

===== Definitions =====

  - army
  - strength

===== Usage =====

aloud, army, × most (mighty), power.
