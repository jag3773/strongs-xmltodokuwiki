====== H7704: שָׂדֶה (sâdeh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; from an unused root meaning to spread out;

===== Meaning =====

a **field** (as flat)

===== Definitions =====

  - field

===== Usage =====

country, field, ground, land, soil, × wild.
