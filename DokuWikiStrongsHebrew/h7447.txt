====== H7447: רָסִיס (râçîyç) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h7450|H7450]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **dripping** to pieces, i.e. a **ruin**; also a dew-**drop**

===== Definitions =====

  - dripping
  - ruin
  - drop

===== Usage =====

breach, drop.
