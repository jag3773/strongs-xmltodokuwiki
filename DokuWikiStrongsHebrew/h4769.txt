====== H4769: מַרְבֵּץ (marbêts) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h7257|H7257]];

===== Meaning =====

a **reclining place**, i.e. **fold** (for flocks)

===== Definitions =====

  - reclining place
  - fold

===== Usage =====

couching place, place to lie down.
