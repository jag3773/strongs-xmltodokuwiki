====== H8597: תִּפְאָרָה (tiphʼârâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

or ; from H6286;

===== Meaning =====

**ornament** (abstractly or concretely, literally or figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - ornament

===== Usage =====

beauty(-iful), bravery, comely, fair, glory(-ious), honour, majesty.
