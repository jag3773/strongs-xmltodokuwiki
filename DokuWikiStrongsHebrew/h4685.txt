====== H4685: מָצוֹד (mâtsôwd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or (feminine) ; or ; from H6679; also (by interchange for H4679)

===== Meaning =====

a **net** (for **capturing** animals or fishes); a **fastness** or (beseiging) **tower**

===== Definitions =====

  - net
  - capturing
  - fastness
  - tower

===== Usage =====

bulwark, hold, munition, net, snare.
