====== H7801: שׁוּשַׁנְכִי (Shûwshankîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) of foreign origin;

===== Meaning =====

a **Shushankite** (collectively) or inhabitants of some unknown place in Assyrian

===== Definitions =====

  - Shushankite

===== Usage =====

Susanchites.
