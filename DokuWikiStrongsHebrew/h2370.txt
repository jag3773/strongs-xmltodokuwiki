====== H2370: חֲזָא (chăzâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) or ; (Aramaic), corresponding to H2372;

===== Meaning =====

to **gaze upon**; mentally to **dream**, **be usual** (i.e. seem)

===== Definitions =====

  - gaze upon
  - dream
  - be usual

===== Usage =====

behold, have (a dream), see, be wont.
