====== H5854: עַטְרוֹת בֵּית יוֹאָב (ʻAṭrôwth bêyth Yôwʼâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from the same as H5852 and H1004 and H3097; crowns of the house of Joab;

===== Meaning =====

**Atroth-beth-Joab**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Atroth-beth-Joab

===== Usage =====

Ataroth the house of Joab.
