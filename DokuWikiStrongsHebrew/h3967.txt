====== H3967: מֵאָה (mêʼâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; properly, a primitive numeral

===== Meaning =====

a **hundred**; also as a multiplicative and a fraction

===== Definitions =====

  - hundred

===== Usage =====

hundred((-fold), -th),   sixscore.
