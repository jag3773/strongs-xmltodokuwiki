====== H4806: מְרִיא (mᵉrîyʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H4754 in the sense of **grossness**, through the idea of **domineering** (compare H4756);

===== Meaning =====

**stall-fed**; often (as noun) a **beeve**

===== Definitions =====

  - grossness
  - domineering
  - stall-fed
  - beeve

===== Usage =====

fat (fed) beast (cattle, -ling).
