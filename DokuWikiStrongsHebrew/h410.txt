====== H410: אֵל (ʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

shortened from H352;

===== Meaning =====

**strength**; as adjective, **mighty**; especially the **Almighty** (but used also of any **deity**)

===== Definitions =====

  - strength
  - mighty
  - Almighty
  - deity

===== Usage =====

God (god), × goodly, × great, idol, might(-y one), power, strong. Compare names in '-el.'
