====== H2513: חֶלְקָה (chelqâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:strongs:h2506|H2506]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **smoothness**; figuratively, **flattery**; also an **allotment**

===== Definitions =====

  - smoothness
  - flattery
  - allotment

===== Usage =====

field, flattering(-ry), ground, parcel, part, piece of land (ground), plat, portion, slippery place, smooth (thing).
