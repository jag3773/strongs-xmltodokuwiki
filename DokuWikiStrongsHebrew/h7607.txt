====== H7607: שְׁאֵר (shᵉʼêr) ======

===== Source =====

from  H7604 ;

===== Meaning =====

flesh  (as  swelling  out), as living or for food; generally  food  of any kind; figuratively,  kindred  by blood

===== Definitions =====

  - flesh
  - swelling
  - food
  - kindred

===== Usage =====

body, flesh, food, (near) kin(-sman, -swoman), near (nigh) (of kin).
