====== H1860: דְּרָאוֹן (dᵉrâʼôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; from an unused root (meaning to **repulse**);

===== Meaning =====

an object of **aversion**

===== Definitions =====

  - repulse
  - aversion

===== Usage =====

abhorring, contempt.
