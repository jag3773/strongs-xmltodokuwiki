====== H4054: מִגְרָשׁ (migrâsh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

also (in plural) feminine (Ezekiel 27:28) ; from H1644;

===== Meaning =====

a **suburb** (i.e. open country whither flocks are driven from pasture); hence, the **area around a building**, or the **margin of the sea**

===== Definitions =====

  - suburb
  - area around a building
  - margin of the sea

===== Usage =====

cast out, suburb.
