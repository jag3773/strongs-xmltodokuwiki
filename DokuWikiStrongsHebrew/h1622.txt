====== H1622: גִּרְגָּשִׁי (Girgâshîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

patrial from an unused name (of uncertain derivation);

===== Meaning =====

a **Girgashite**, one of the native tribes of Canaan

===== Definitions =====

  - Girgashite

===== Usage =====

Girgashite, Girgasite.
