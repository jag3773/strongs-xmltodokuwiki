====== H6667: צִדְקִיָּה (Tsidqîyâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

or ; from H6664 and H3050; **right of Jah**;

===== Meaning =====

**Tsidkijah**, the name of six Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - right of Jah
  - Tsidkijah

===== Usage =====

Zedekiah, Zidkijah.
