====== H8593: תַּעַר (taʻar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n

===== Source =====

from H6168;

===== Meaning =====

a **knife** or **razor** (as **making** bare); also a **scabbard** (as **being bare**, i.e. empty)

===== Definitions =====

  - knife
  - razor
  - making
  - scabbard
  - being bare

===== Usage =====

(pen-) knife, razor, scabbard, shave, sheath.
