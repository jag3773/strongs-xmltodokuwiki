====== H8668: תְּשׁוּעָה (tᵉshûwʻâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

or ; from H7768 in the sense of H3467;

===== Meaning =====

**rescue** (literal or figurative, persons, national or spiritual)

===== Definitions =====

  - rescue

===== Usage =====

deliverance, help, safety, salvation, victory.
