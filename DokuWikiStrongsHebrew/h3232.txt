====== H3232: יִמְנָה (Yimnâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr

===== Source =====

from H3231; **prosperity** (as betokened by the right hand);

===== Meaning =====

**Jimnah**, the name of two Israelites; also (with the article) of the posterity of one of them

===== Definitions =====

  - prosperity
  - Jimnah

===== Usage =====

Imna, Imnah, Jimnah, Jimnites.
