====== H892: בָּבָה (bâbâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine active participle of an unused root meaning to hollow out;

===== Meaning =====

something** hollowed** (as a gate), i.e. **pupil of the eye**

===== Definitions =====

  -  hollowed
  - pupil of the eye

===== Usage =====

apple (of the eye).
