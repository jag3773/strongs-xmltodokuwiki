====== H1905: הַגְרִי (Hagrîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr

===== Source =====

or (prolonged) ; perhaps patronymically from H1904;

===== Meaning =====

a **Hagrite** or member of a certain Arabian clan

===== Definitions =====

  - Hagrite

===== Usage =====

Hagarene, Hagarite, Haggeri.
