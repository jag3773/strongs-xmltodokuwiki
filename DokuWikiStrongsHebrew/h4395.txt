====== H4395: מְלֵאָה (mᵉlêʼâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:strongs:h4392|H4392]];

===== Meaning =====

something **fulfilled**, i.e. **abundance** (of produce)

===== Definitions =====

  - fulfilled
  - abundance

===== Usage =====

(first of ripe) fruit, fulness.
