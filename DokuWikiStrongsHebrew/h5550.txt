====== H5550: סֹלְלָה (çôlᵉlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

or ; active participle feminine of H5549, but used passively;

===== Meaning =====

a military **mound**, i.e. rampart of **besiegers**

===== Definitions =====

  - mound
  - besiegers

===== Usage =====

bank, mount.
