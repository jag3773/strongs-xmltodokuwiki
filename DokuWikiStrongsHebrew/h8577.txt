====== H8577: תַּנִּין (tannîyn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or ; (Ezekiel 29:3), intensive from the same as H8565;

===== Meaning =====

a marine or land **monster**, i.e. **sea-serpent** or **jackal**

===== Definitions =====

  - monster
  - sea-serpent
  - jackal

===== Usage =====

dragon, sea-monster, serpent, whale.
