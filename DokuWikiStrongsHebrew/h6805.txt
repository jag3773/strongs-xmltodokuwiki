====== H6805: צָעַד (tsâʻad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **pace**, i.e. **step regularly**; (upward) to **mount**; (along) to **march**; (down and causatively) to **hurl**

===== Definitions =====

  - pace
  - step regularly
  - mount
  - march
  - hurl

===== Usage =====

bring, go, march (through), run over.
