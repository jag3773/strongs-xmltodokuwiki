====== H5279: נַעֲמָה (Naʻămâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-f n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

feminine of H5277; **pleasantness**;

===== Meaning =====

**Naamah**, the name of an antediluvian woman, of an Ammonitess, and of a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - pleasantness
  - Naamah

===== Usage =====

Naamah.
