====== H361: אֵילָם (ʼêylâm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or (shortened)  ; or (feminine)  ; probably from H352;

===== Meaning =====

a **pillar-space** (or colonnade), i.e. a **pale** (or portico)

===== Definitions =====

  - pillar-space
  - pale

===== Usage =====

arch.
