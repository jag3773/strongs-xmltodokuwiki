====== H8109: שְׁמֻרָה (shᵉmurâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine of passive participle of [[en:strongs:h8104|H8104]];

===== Meaning =====

something **guarded**, i.e. an **eye-lid**

===== Definitions =====

  - guarded
  - eye-lid

===== Usage =====

waking.
