====== H6567: פָּרָשׁ (pârâsh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **separate**, literally (to disperse) or figuratively (to specify); also (by implication) to **wound**

===== Definitions =====

  - separate
  - wound

===== Usage =====

scatter, declare, distinctly, shew, sting.
