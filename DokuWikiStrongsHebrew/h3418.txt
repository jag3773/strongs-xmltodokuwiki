====== H3418: יֶרֶק (yereq) ======

===== Source =====

from  H3417  (in the sense of vacuity of color);

===== Meaning =====

properly,  pallor , i.e. hence, the  yellowish green  of young and sickly vegetation; concretely,  verdure , i.e.  grass  or  vegetation

===== Definitions =====

  - pallor
  - yellowish green
  - verdure
  - grass
  - vegetation

===== Usage =====

grass, green (thing).
