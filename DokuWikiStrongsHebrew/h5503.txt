====== H5503: סָחַר (çâchar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **travel round** (specifically as a pedlar); intensively, to **palpitate**

===== Definitions =====

  - travel round
  - palpitate

===== Usage =====

go about, merchant(-man), occupy with, pant, trade, traffick.
