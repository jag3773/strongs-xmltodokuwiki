====== H5707: עֵד (ʻêd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

contracted from H5749;

===== Meaning =====

concretely, a **witness**; abstractly, **testimony**; specifically, a **recorder**, i.e. **prince**

===== Definitions =====

  - witness
  - testimony
  - recorder
  - prince

===== Usage =====

witness.
