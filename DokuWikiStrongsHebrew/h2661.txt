====== H2661: חֲפֹר (chăphôr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from H2658; a **hole**; only in connection with H6512, which ought rather to be joined as one word, thus ; by reduplication from H2658;

===== Meaning =====

a **burrower**, i.e. probably a **rat**

===== Definitions =====

  - hole
  - burrower
  - rat

===== Usage =====

  mole.
