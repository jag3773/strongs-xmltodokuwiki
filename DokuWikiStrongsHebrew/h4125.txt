====== H4125: מוֹאָבִי (Môwʼâbîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

feminine ; or ; patronymical from H4124;

===== Meaning =====

a **Moabite** or **Moabitess**, i.e. a descendant from Moab

===== Definitions =====

  - Moabite
  - Moabitess

===== Usage =====

(woman) of Moab, Moabite(-ish, -ss).
