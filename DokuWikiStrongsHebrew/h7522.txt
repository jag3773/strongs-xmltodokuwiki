====== H7522: רָצוֹן (râtsôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or ; from H7521;

===== Meaning =====

**delight** (especially as shown)

===== Definitions =====

  - delight

===== Usage =====

(be) acceptable(-ance, -ed), delight, desire, favour, (good) pleasure, (own, self, voluntary) will, as...(what) would.
