====== H8556: תִּמְנַת חֶרֶס (Timnath Chereç) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

or ; from H8553 and H2775; portion of (the) sun;

===== Meaning =====

**Timnath-Cheres**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Timnath-Cheres

===== Usage =====

Timnath-heres, Timnath-serah.
