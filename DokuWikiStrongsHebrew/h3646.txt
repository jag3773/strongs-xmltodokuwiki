====== H3646: כַּמֹּן (kammôn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **store** up or **preserve**;

===== Meaning =====

**'cummin'** (from its use as a **condiment**)

===== Definitions =====

  - store
  - preserve
  - 'cummin'
  - condiment

===== Usage =====

cummin.
