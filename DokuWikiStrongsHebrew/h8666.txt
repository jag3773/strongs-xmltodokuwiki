====== H8666: תְּשׁוּבָה (tᵉshûwbâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; from [[en:strongs:h7725|H7725]];

===== Meaning =====

a **recurrence** (of time or place); a **reply** (as returned)

===== Definitions =====

  - recurrence
  - reply

===== Usage =====

answer, be expired, return.
