====== H350: אִי־כָבוֹד (ʼÎy-kâbôwd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h336|H336]] and [[en:strongs:h3519|H3519]]; (there is) no glory, i.e. inglorious;

===== Meaning =====

**Ikabod**, a son of Phineas

===== Definitions =====

  - Ikabod

===== Usage =====

I-chabod.
