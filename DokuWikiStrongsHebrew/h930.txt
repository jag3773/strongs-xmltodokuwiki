====== H930: בְּהֵמוֹת (bᵉhêmôwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

in form a plural or H929, but really a singular of Egyptian derivation;

===== Meaning =====

a **water-ox**, i.e. the **hippopotamus** or **Nile-horse**

===== Definitions =====

  - water-ox
  - hippopotamus
  - Nile-horse

===== Usage =====

Behemoth.
