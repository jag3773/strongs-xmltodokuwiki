====== H1039: בֵּית נִמְרָה (Bêyth Nimrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from H1004 and the feminine of H5246; **house of** (the) **leopard**;

===== Meaning =====

**Beth-Nimrah**, a place east of the Jordan

===== Definitions =====

  - house of
  - leopard
  - Beth-Nimrah

===== Usage =====

Beth-Nimrah. Compare
