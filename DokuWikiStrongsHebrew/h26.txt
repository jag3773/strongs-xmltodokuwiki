====== H26: אֲבִיגַיִל (ʼĂbîygayil) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-f

===== Source =====

or shorter ; from H1 and H1524; father (i.e. source) of joy;

===== Meaning =====

**Abigail** or **Abigal**, the name of two Israelitesses

===== Definitions =====

  - Abigail
  - Abigal

===== Usage =====

Abigal.
