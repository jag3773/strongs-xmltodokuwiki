====== H26: אֲבִיגַיִל (ʼĂbîygayil) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or shorter [[en:strongs:|]]; from [[en:strongs:h1|H1]] and [[en:strongs:h1524|H1524]]; father (i.e. source) of joy;

===== Meaning =====

**Abigail** or **Abigal**, the name of two Israelitesses

===== Definitions =====

  - Abigail
  - Abigal

===== Usage =====

Abigal.
