====== H2143: זֵכֶר (zêker) ======

===== Source =====

or   ; from  H2142 ;

===== Meaning =====

a  memento , abstractly  recollection  (rarely if ever); by implication,  commemoration

===== Definitions =====

  - memento
  - recollection
  - commemoration

===== Usage =====

memorial, memory, remembrance, scent.
