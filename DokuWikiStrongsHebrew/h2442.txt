====== H2442: חָכָה (châkâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root (apparently akin to [[en:strongs:h2707|H2707]] through the idea of **piercing**);

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **adhere** to; hence, to await

===== Definitions =====

  - piercing
  - adhere

===== Usage =====

long, tarry, wait.
