====== H2223: זַרְזִיר (zarzîyr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

by reduplication from [[en:strongs:h2115|H2115]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **tightly girt**, i.e. probably a **racer**, or **some fleet animal** (as being slender in the waist)

===== Definitions =====

  - tightly girt
  - racer
  - some fleet animal

===== Usage =====

  greyhound.
