====== H4441: מַלְכִּיָּה (Malkîyâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

or ; (Jeremiah 38:6), from H4428 and H3050; **king of** (i.e. appointed by) **Jah**;

===== Meaning =====

**Malkijah**, the name of ten Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - king of
  - Jah
  - Malkijah

===== Usage =====

Malchiah, Malchijah.
