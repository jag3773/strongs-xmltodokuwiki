====== H5736: עֲדַף (ʻădaph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **be** (causatively **have**) **redundant**

===== Definitions =====

  - be
  - have
  - redundant

===== Usage =====

be more, odd number, be (have) over (and above), overplus, remain.
