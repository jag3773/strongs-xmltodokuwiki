====== H4148: מוּסָר (mûwçâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H3256;

===== Meaning =====

properly, **chastisement**; figuratively, **reproof**, **warning** or **instruction**; also **restraint**

===== Definitions =====

  - chastisement
  - reproof
  - warning
  - instruction
  - restraint

===== Usage =====

bond, chastening(-eth), chastisement, check, correction, discipline, doctrine, instruction, rebuke.
