====== H7055: קְמוּאֵל (Qᵉmûwʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from H6965 and H410; **raised of God**;

===== Meaning =====

**Kemuel**, the name of a relative of Abraham, and of two Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - raised of God
  - Kemuel

===== Usage =====

Kemuel.
