====== H2377: חָזוֹן (châzôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h2372|H2372]];

===== Meaning =====

a **sight** (mentally), i.e. a **dream**, **revelation**, or **oracle**

===== Definitions =====

  - sight
  - dream
  - revelation
  - oracle

===== Usage =====

vision.
