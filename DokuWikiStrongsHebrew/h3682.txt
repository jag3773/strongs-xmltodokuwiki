====== H3682: כְּסוּת (kᵉçûwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h3680|H3680]];

===== Meaning =====

a **cover** (**garment**); figuratively, a **veiling**

===== Definitions =====

  - cover
  - garment
  - veiling

===== Usage =====

covering, raiment, vesture.
