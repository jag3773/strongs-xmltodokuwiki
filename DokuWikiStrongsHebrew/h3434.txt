====== H3434: יָשׇׁבְעָם (Yâshobʻâm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from H7725 and H5971; **people will return**;

===== Meaning =====

**Jashobam**, the name of two or three Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - people will return
  - Jashobam

===== Usage =====

Jashobeam.
