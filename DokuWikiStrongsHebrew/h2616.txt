====== H2616: חָסַד (châçad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root

===== Meaning =====

properly, perhaps to **bow** (the neck only [compare [[en:strongs:h2603|H2603]]] in courtesy to an equal), i.e. to **be kind**; also (by euphemistically [compare [[en:strongs:h1288|H1288]]], but rarely) to **reprove**

===== Definitions =====

  - bow
  - be kind
  - reprove

===== Usage =====

shew self merciful, put to shame.
