====== H145: אֶדֶר (ʼeder) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h142|H142]];

===== Meaning =====

**amplitude**, i.e. (concrete) a **mantle**; also (figuratively) **splendor**

===== Definitions =====

  - amplitude
  - mantle
  - splendor

===== Usage =====

goodly, robe.
