====== H3649: כָּמָר (kâmâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h3648|H3648]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, an **ascetic** (as if shrunk with self-maceration), i.e. an **idolatrous priest** (only in plural)

===== Definitions =====

  - ascetic
  - idolatrous priest

===== Usage =====

Chemarims (idolatrous) priests.
