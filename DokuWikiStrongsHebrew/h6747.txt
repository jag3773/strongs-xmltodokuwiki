====== H6747: צַלַּחַת (tsallachath) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from H6743;

===== Meaning =====

something **advanced** or **deep**, i.e. a **bowl**; figuratively, the **bosom**

===== Definitions =====

  - advanced
  - deep
  - bowl
  - bosom

===== Usage =====

bosom, dish.
