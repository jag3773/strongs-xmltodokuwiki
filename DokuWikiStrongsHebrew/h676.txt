====== H676: אֶצְבַּע (ʼetsbaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from the same as H6648 (in the sense of **grasping**);

===== Meaning =====

something to **sieze** with, i.e. a **finger**; by analogy, a **toe**

===== Definitions =====

  - grasping
  - sieze
  - finger
  - toe

===== Usage =====

finger, toe.
