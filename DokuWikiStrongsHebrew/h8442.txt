====== H8442: תּוֹעָה (tôwʻâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine active participle of [[en:strongs:h8582|H8582]];

===== Meaning =====

**mistake**, i.e. (morally) **impiety**, or (political) **injury**

===== Definitions =====

  - mistake
  - impiety
  - injury

===== Usage =====

error, hinder.
