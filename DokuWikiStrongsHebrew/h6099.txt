====== H6099: עָצוּם (ʻâtsûwm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; passive participle of [[en:strongs:h6105|H6105]];

===== Meaning =====

**powerful** (specifically, a paw); by implication, **numerous**

===== Definitions =====

  - powerful
  - numerous

===== Usage =====

  feeble, great, mighty, must, strong.
