====== H8032: שִׁלְשׁוֹם (shilshôwm) ======

===== Source =====

or   ; from the same as  H8028 ;

===== Meaning =====

trebly , i.e. (in time)  day before yesterday

===== Definitions =====

  - trebly
  - day before yesterday

===== Usage =====

  before (that time, -time), excellent things (from the margin),   heretofore, three days,   time past.
