====== H1836: דֵּן (dên) ======

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) an orthographical variation of  H1791 ;

===== Meaning =====

this

===== Definitions =====

  - this

===== Usage =====

(afore-) time,   after this manner, here (-after), one...another, such, there(-fore), these, this (matter),   thus, where(-fore), which.
