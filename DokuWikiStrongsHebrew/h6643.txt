====== H6643: צְבִי (tsᵉbîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h6638|H6638]] in the sense of **prominence**;

===== Meaning =====

**splendor** (as **conspicuous**)

===== Definitions =====

  - prominence
  - splendor
  - conspicuous

===== Usage =====

beautiful(-ty), glorious (-ry), goodly, pleasant, roe(-buck).
