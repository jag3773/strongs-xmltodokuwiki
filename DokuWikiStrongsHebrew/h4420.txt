====== H4420: מְלֵחָה (mᵉlêchâh) ======

===== Source =====

from  H4414  (in its denominative sense); understood as land ( H776 )

===== Meaning =====

properly,  salted  (i.e. land), i.e. a  desert

===== Definitions =====

  - salted
  - desert

===== Usage =====

barren land(-ness), salt (land).
