====== H5642: סְתַר (çᵉthar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to [[en:strongs:h5641|H5641]];

===== Meaning =====

to **conceal**; figuratively, to **demolish**

===== Definitions =====

  - conceal
  - demolish

===== Usage =====

destroy, secret thing.
