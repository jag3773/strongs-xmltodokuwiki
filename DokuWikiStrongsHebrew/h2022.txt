====== H2022: הַר (har) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

a shortened form of H2042;

===== Meaning =====

a **mountain** or **range** of hills (sometimes used figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - mountain
  - range

===== Usage =====

hill (country), mount(-ain), × promotion.
