n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from H7413 and H4707 with the article interpolated; height of the watch-tower;

===== Meaning =====

**Ramath-ham-Mitspeh**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Ramath-ham-Mitspeh

===== Usage =====
