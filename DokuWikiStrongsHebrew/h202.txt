====== H202: אוֹן (ʼôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

probably from the same as H205 (in the sense of **effort**, but successful);

===== Meaning =====

**ability**, **power**, (figuratively) **wealth**

===== Definitions =====

  - effort
  - ability
  - power
  - wealth

===== Usage =====

force, goods, might, strength, substance.
