====== H8411: תְּדִירָא (tᵉdîyrâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from [[en:strongs:h1753|H1753]] in the original sense of enduring;

===== Meaning =====

**permanence**, i.e. (adverb) **constantly**

===== Definitions =====

  - permanence
  - constantly

===== Usage =====

continually.
