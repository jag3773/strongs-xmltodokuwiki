====== H2522: חָלַשׁ (châlash) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **prostrate**; by implication, to **overthrow**, **decay**

===== Definitions =====

  - prostrate
  - overthrow
  - decay

===== Usage =====

discomfit, waste away, weaken.
