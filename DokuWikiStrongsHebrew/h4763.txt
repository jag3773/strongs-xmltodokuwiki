====== H4763: מְרַאֲשָׁה (mᵉraʼăshâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

formed like [[en:strongs:h4761|H4761]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **headpiece**, i.e. (plural for adverbial) at (or as) the **head-rest** (or pillow)

===== Definitions =====

  - headpiece
  - head-rest

===== Usage =====

bolster, head, pillow. Compare
