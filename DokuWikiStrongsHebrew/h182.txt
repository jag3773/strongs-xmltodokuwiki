====== H182: אוֹדוֹת (ʼôwdôwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

or (shortened)  (only thus in the plural); from the same as H181;

===== Meaning =====

**turnings** (i.e. **occasions**); (adverb) on **account** of

===== Definitions =====

  - turnings
  - occasions
  - account

===== Usage =====

(be-) cause, concerning, sake.
