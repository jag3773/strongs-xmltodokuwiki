====== H6580: פַּשׁ (pash) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

probably from an unused root meaning to **disintegrate**;

===== Meaning =====

**stupidity** (as a result of grossness or of degeneracy)

===== Definitions =====

  - disintegrate
  - stupidity

===== Usage =====

extremity.
