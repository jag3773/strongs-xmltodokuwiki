====== H1802: דָּלָה (dâlâh) ======

===== Source =====

a primitive root (compare  H1809 );

===== Meaning =====

properly, to  dangle , i.e. to  let down a bucket  (for drawing out water); figuratively, to  deliver

===== Definitions =====

  - dangle
  - let down a bucket
  - deliver

===== Usage =====

draw (out), × enough, lift up.
