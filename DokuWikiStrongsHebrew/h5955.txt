====== H5955: עֹלֵלָה (ʻôlêlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine active participle of [[en:strongs:h5953|H5953]];

===== Meaning =====

only in plural **gleanings**; by extension **gleaning-time**

===== Definitions =====

  - gleanings
  - gleaning-time

===== Usage =====

(gleaning) (of the) grapes, grapegleanings.
