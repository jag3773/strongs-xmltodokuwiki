====== H4968: מְתוּשֶׁלַח (Mᵉthûwshelach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from H4962 and H7973; **man of a dart**;

===== Meaning =====

**Methushelach**, an antediluvian patriarch

===== Definitions =====

  - man of a dart
  - Methushelach

===== Usage =====

Methuselah.
