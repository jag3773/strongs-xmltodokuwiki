====== H206: אָוֶן (ʼÂven) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

the same as H205; idolatry;

===== Meaning =====

**Aven**, the contemptuous synonym of three places, one in Coele-Syria, one in Egypt (On), and one in Palestine (Bethel)

===== Definitions =====

  - Aven
