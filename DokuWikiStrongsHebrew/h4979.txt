====== H4979: מַתָּנָה (mattânâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:strongs:h4976|H4976]];

===== Meaning =====

a **present**; specifically (in a good sense), a **sacrificial offering**, (in a bad sense) a **bribe**

===== Definitions =====

  - present
  - sacrificial offering
  - bribe

===== Usage =====

gift.
