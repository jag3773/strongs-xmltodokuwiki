====== H3713: כְּפוֹר (kᵉphôwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h3722|H3722]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **cover**, i.e. (by implication) a **tankard** (or covered goblet); also **white frost** (as covering the ground)

===== Definitions =====

  - cover
  - tankard
  - white frost

===== Usage =====

bason, hoar(-y) frost.
