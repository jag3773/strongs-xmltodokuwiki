====== H314: אַחֲרוֹן (ʼachărôwn) ======

===== Source =====

or (shortened)   ; from  H309 ;

===== Meaning =====

hinder ; generally,  late  or  last ; specifically (as facing the east)  western

===== Definitions =====

  - hinder
  - late
  - last
  - western

===== Usage =====

after (-ward), to come, following, hind(-er, -ermost, -most), last, latter, rereward, ut(ter) most.
