====== H2281: חָבֵת (châbêth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from an unused root probably meaning to cook (compare [[en:strongs:h4227|H4227]]);

===== Meaning =====

something **fried**, probably a **griddle-cake**

===== Definitions =====

  - fried
  - griddle-cake

===== Usage =====

pan.
