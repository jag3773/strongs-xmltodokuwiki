====== H970: בָּחוּר (bâchûwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or ; participle passive of H977;

===== Meaning =====

properly, **selected**, i.e. a **youth** (often collective)

===== Definitions =====

  - selected
  - youth

===== Usage =====

(choice) young (man), chosen, × hole.
