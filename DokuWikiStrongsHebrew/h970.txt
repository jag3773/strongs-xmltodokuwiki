====== H970: בָּחוּר (bâchûwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; participle passive of [[en:strongs:h977|H977]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **selected**, i.e. a **youth** (often collective)

===== Definitions =====

  - selected
  - youth

===== Usage =====

(choice) young (man), chosen, × hole.
