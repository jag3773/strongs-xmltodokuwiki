====== H5103: נְהַר (nᵉhar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from a root corresponding to [[en:strongs:h5102|H5102]];

===== Meaning =====

a **river**, especially the **Euphrates**

===== Definitions =====

  - river
  - Euphrates

===== Usage =====

river, stream.
