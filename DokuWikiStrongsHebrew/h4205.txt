====== H4205: מָזוֹר (mâzôwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; from [[en:strongs:h2115|H2115]] in the sense of binding up;

===== Meaning =====

a **bandage**, i.e. **remedy**; hence, a **sore** (as needing a compress)

===== Definitions =====

  - bandage
  - remedy
  - sore

===== Usage =====

bound up, wound.
