====== H7476: רַעְיוֹן (raʻyôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to H7475;

===== Meaning =====

a **grasp**. i.e. (figuratively) **mental conception**

===== Definitions =====

  - grasp
  - mental conception

===== Usage =====

cogitation, thought.
