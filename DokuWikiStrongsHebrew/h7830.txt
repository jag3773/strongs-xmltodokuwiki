====== H7830: שַׁחַץ (shachats) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from an unused root apparently meaning to **strut**;

===== Meaning =====

**haughtiness** (as evinced by the attitude)

===== Definitions =====

  - strut
  - haughtiness

===== Usage =====

× lion, pride.
