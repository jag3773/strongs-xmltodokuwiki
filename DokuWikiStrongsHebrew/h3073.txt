====== H3073: יְהֹוָה שָׁלוֹם (Yᵉhôvâh shâlôwm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from H3068 and H7965; Jehovah (is) peace;

===== Meaning =====

**Jehovah-Shalom**, a symbolical name of an altar in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Jehovah-Shalom

===== Usage =====

Jehovah-shalom.
