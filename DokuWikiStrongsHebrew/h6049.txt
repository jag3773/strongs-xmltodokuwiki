====== H6049: עָנַן (ʻânan) ======

===== Source =====

a primitive root; to cover; used only as a denominative from  H6051 , to cloud over;

===== Meaning =====

figuratively, to  act covertly , i.e.  practise magic

===== Definitions =====

  - act covertly
  - practise magic

===== Usage =====

× bring, enchanter, Meonemin, observe(-r of) times, soothsayer, sorcerer.
