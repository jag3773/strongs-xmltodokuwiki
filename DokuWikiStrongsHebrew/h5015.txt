====== H5015: נְבוֹ (Nᵉbôw) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

probably of foreign derivation;

===== Meaning =====

**Nebo**, the name of a Babylonian deity, also of a mountain in Moab, and of a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Nebo

===== Usage =====

Nebo.
