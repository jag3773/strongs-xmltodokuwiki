====== H1283: בְּרִיעָה (Bᵉrîyʻâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

apparently from the feminine of H7451 with a prepositional prefix; **in trouble**;

===== Meaning =====

**Beriah**, the name of four Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - in trouble
  - Beriah

===== Usage =====

Beriah.
