====== H3962: לֶשַׁע (Leshaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from an unused root thought to mean to break through; a boiling spring;

===== Meaning =====

**Lesha**, a place probably East of the Jordan

===== Definitions =====

  - Lesha

===== Usage =====

Lasha.
