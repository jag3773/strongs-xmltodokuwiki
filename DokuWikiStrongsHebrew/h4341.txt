====== H4341: מַכְאֹב (makʼôb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

sometimes ; also (feminine Isaiah 53:3) ; from H3510;

===== Meaning =====

**anguish** or (figuratively) **affliction**

===== Definitions =====

  - anguish
  - affliction

===== Usage =====

grief, pain, sorrow.
