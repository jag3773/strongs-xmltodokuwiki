====== H5524: סֻכּוֹת בְּנוֹת (Çukkôwth bᵉnôwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h5523|H5523]] and the (irregular) plural of [[en:strongs:h1323|H1323]];

===== Meaning =====

**booths of** (the) **daughters**; **brothels**, i.e. **idoalatrous tents for impure purpose**

===== Definitions =====

  - booths of
  - daughters
  - brothels
  - idoalatrous tents for impure purpose

===== Usage =====

Succothbenoth.
