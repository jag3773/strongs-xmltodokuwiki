====== H5945: עֶלְיוֹן (ʻelyôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m a

===== Source =====

from H5927;

===== Meaning =====

an **elevation**, i.e. (adj.) **lofty** (compar.); as title, the **Supreme**

===== Definitions =====

  - elevation
  - lofty
  - Supreme

===== Usage =====

(Most, on) high(-er, -est), upper(-most).
