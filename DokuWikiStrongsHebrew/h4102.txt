====== H4102: מָהַהּ (mâhahh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

apparently a denominative from [[en:strongs:h4100|H4100]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **question** or **hesitate**, i.e. (by implication) to **be reluctant**

===== Definitions =====

  - question
  - hesitate
  - be reluctant

===== Usage =====

delay, linger, stay selves, tarry.
