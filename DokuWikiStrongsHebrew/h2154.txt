====== H2154: זִמָּה (zimmâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

or ; from H2161;

===== Meaning =====

a **plan**, especially a **bad one**

===== Definitions =====

  - plan
  - bad one

===== Usage =====

heinous crime, lewd(-ly, -ness), mischief, purpose, thought, wicked (device, mind, -ness).
