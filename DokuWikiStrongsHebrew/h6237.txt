====== H6237: עָשַׂר (ʻâsar) ======

===== Source =====

a primitive root (identical with  H6238 ); to accumulate; but used only as denominative from  H6235 ;

===== Meaning =====

to  tithe , i.e. to  take  or  give a tenth

===== Definitions =====

  - tithe
  - take
  - give a tenth

===== Usage =====

× surely, give (take) the tenth, (have, take) tithe(-ing, -s), × truly.
