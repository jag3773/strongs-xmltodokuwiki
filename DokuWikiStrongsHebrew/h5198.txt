====== H5198: נָטָף (nâṭâph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h5197|H5197]];

===== Meaning =====

a **drop**; specifically, an aromatic **gum** (probably **stacte**)

===== Definitions =====

  - drop
  - gum
  - stacte

===== Usage =====

drop, stacte.
