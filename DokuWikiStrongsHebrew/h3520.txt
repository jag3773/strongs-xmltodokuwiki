====== H3520: כְּבוּדָּה (kᵉbûwddâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

irregular feminine passive participle of H3513;

===== Meaning =====

**weightiness**, i.e. **magnificence**, **wealth**

===== Definitions =====

  - weightiness
  - magnificence
  - wealth

===== Usage =====

carriage, all glorious, stately.
