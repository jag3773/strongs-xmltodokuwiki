====== H4635: מַעֲרֶכֶת (maʻăreketh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h6186|H6186]];

===== Meaning =====

an **arrangement**, i.e. (concretely) a **pile** (of loaves)

===== Definitions =====

  - arrangement
  - pile

===== Usage =====

row, shewbread.
