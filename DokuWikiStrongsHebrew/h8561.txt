====== H8561: תִּמֹּר (timmôr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

(plural or (feminine) [[en:strongs:|]]; (singular and plural), from the same root as [[en:strongs:h8558|H8558]];

===== Meaning =====

(architectural) a **palm-like pilaster** (i.e. umbellate)

===== Definitions =====

  - palm-like pilaster

===== Usage =====

palm tree.
