====== H8561: תִּמֹּר (timmôr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

(plural or (feminine) ; (singular and plural), from the same root as H8558;

===== Meaning =====

(architectural) a **palm-like pilaster** (i.e. umbellate)

===== Definitions =====

  - palm-like pilaster

===== Usage =====

palm tree.
