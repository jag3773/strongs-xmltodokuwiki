====== H464: אֱלִיפַז (ʼĔlîyphaz) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from H410 and H6337; **God of gold**;

===== Meaning =====

**Eliphaz**, the name of one of Job's friends, and of a son of Esau

===== Definitions =====

  - God of gold
  - Eliphaz

===== Usage =====

Eliphaz.
