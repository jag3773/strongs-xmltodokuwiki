====== H464: אֱלִיפַז (ʼĔlîyphaz) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h410|H410]] and [[en:strongs:h6337|H6337]]; **God of gold**;

===== Meaning =====

**Eliphaz**, the name of one of Job's friends, and of a son of Esau

===== Definitions =====

  - God of gold
  - Eliphaz

===== Usage =====

Eliphaz.
