====== H7264: רָגַז (râgaz) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **quiver** (with any violent emotion, especially anger or fear)

===== Definitions =====

  - quiver

===== Usage =====

be afraid, stand in awe, disquiet, fall out, fret, move, provoke, quake, rage, shake, tremble, trouble, be wroth.
