====== H6424: פָּלַס (pâlaç) ======

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to  roll flat , i.e.  prepare  (a road); also to  revolve , i.e.  weigh  (mentally)

===== Definitions =====

  - roll flat
  - prepare
  - revolve
  - weigh

===== Usage =====

make, ponder, weigh.
