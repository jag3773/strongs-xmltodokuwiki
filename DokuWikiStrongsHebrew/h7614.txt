====== H7614: שְׁבָא (Shᵉbâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

of foreign origin;

===== Meaning =====

**Sheba**, the name of three early progenitors of tribes and of an Ethiopian district

===== Definitions =====

  - Sheba

===== Usage =====

Sheba, Sabeans.
