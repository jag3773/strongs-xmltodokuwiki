====== H645: אֵפוֹ (ʼêphôw) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

prt

===== Source =====

or ; from H6311;

===== Meaning =====

strictly a demonstrative particle, **here**; but used of time, **now** or **then**

===== Definitions =====

  - here
  - now
  - then

===== Usage =====

here, now, where?
