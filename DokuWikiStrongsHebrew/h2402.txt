====== H2402: חַטָּאָה (chaṭṭâʼâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to [[en:strongs:h2401|H2401]];

===== Meaning =====

**an offence**, and **the penalty** or **sacrifice for it**

===== Definitions =====

  - an offence
  - the penalty
  - sacrifice for it

===== Usage =====

sin (offering).
