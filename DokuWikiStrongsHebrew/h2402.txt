====== H2402: חַטָּאָה (chaṭṭâʼâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to H2401;

===== Meaning =====

**an offence**, and **the penalty** or **sacrifice for it**

===== Definitions =====

  - an offence
  - the penalty
  - sacrifice for it

===== Usage =====

sin (offering).
