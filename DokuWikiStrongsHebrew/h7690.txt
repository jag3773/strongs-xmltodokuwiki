====== H7690: שַׂגִּיא (saggîyʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to H7689;

===== Meaning =====

**large** (in size, quantity or number, also adverbial)

===== Definitions =====

  - large

===== Usage =====

exceeding, great(-ly); many, much, sore, very.
