====== H3333: יְצֻקָה (yᵉtsuqâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

passive participle feminine of [[en:strongs:h3332|H3332]];

===== Meaning =====

**poured out**, i.e. **run into a mould**

===== Definitions =====

  - poured out
  - run into a mould

===== Usage =====

when it was cast.
