====== H7164: קָרַס (qâraç) ======

===== Source =====

a primitive root; properly, to  ; used only as denominative from  H7165  (for alliteration with  H7167 ),

===== Meaning =====

protrude to  hunch , i.e. be hump-backed

===== Definitions =====

  - protrude
  - hunch

===== Usage =====

stoop.
