====== H7164: קָרַס (qâraç) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root; properly, to **protrude**; used only as denominative from H7165 (for alliteration with H7167),

===== Meaning =====

to **hunch**, i.e. be hump-backed

===== Definitions =====

  - protrude
  - hunch

===== Usage =====

stoop.
