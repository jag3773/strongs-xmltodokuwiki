====== H6689: צוּף (Tsûwph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

or ; or ; from H6688; **honey-comb**;

===== Meaning =====

**Tsuph** or **Tsophai** or **Tsiph**, the name of an Israelite and of a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - honey-comb
  - Tsuph
  - Tsophai
  - Tsiph

===== Usage =====

Zophai, Zuph.
