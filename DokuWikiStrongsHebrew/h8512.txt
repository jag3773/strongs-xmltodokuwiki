====== H8512: תֵּל אָבִיב (Têl ʼÂbîyb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from H8510 and H24; **mound of green growth**;

===== Meaning =====

**Tel-Abib**, a place in Chaldaea

===== Definitions =====

  - mound of green growth
  - Tel-Abib

===== Usage =====

Tel-abib.
