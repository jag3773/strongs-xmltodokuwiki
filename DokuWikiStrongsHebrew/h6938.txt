====== H6938: קֵדָר (Qêdâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr

===== Source =====

from H6937; **dusky** (of the skin or the tent);

===== Meaning =====

**Kedar**, a son of Ishmael; also (collectively) Bedouin (as his descendants or representatives)

===== Definitions =====

  - dusky
  - Kedar

===== Usage =====

Kedar.
