====== H6517: פָּרוּר (pârûwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

passive participle of H6565 in the sense of **spreading** out (compare H6524);

===== Meaning =====

**a skillet** (as **flat** or **deep**)

===== Definitions =====

  - spreading
  - a skillet
  - flat
  - deep

===== Usage =====

pan, pot.
