====== H3548: כֹּהֵן (kôhên) ======

===== Source =====

active participle of  H3547 ;

===== Meaning =====

literally one  officiating , a  priest ; also (by courtesy) an  acting priest  (although a layman)

===== Definitions =====

  - officiating
  - priest
  - acting priest

===== Usage =====

chief ruler, × own, priest, prince, principal officer.
