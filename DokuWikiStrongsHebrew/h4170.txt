====== H4170: מוֹקֵשׁ (môwqêsh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or ; from H3369;

===== Meaning =====

a **noose** (for catching animals) (literally or figuratively); by implication, a **hook** (for the nose)

===== Definitions =====

  - noose
  - hook

===== Usage =====

be ensnared, gin, (is) snare(-d), trap.
