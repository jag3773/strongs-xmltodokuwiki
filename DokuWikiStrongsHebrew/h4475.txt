====== H4475: מֶמְשָׁלָה (memshâlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine of H4474;

===== Meaning =====

**rule**; also (concretely in plural) a **realm** or a **ruler**

===== Definitions =====

  - rule
  - realm
  - ruler

===== Usage =====

dominion, government, power, to rule.
