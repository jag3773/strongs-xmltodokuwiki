====== H5251: נֵס (nêç) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h5264|H5264]];

===== Meaning =====

a **flag**; also a **sail**; by implication, a **flagstaff**; generally a **signal**; figuratively, a **token**

===== Definitions =====

  - flag
  - sail
  - flagstaff
  - signal
  - token

===== Usage =====

banner, pole, sail, (en-) sign, standard.
