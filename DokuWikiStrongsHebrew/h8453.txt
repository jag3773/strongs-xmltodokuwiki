====== H8453: תּוֹשָׁב (tôwshâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or ; (1 Kings 17:1), from H3427;

===== Meaning =====

a **dweller** but not outlandish [H5237]; especially (as distinguished from a native citizen [active participle of H3427] and a temporary inmate [H1616] or mere lodger [H3885]) resident **alien**

===== Definitions =====

  - dweller
  - alien

===== Usage =====

foreigner, inhabitant, sojourner, stranger.
