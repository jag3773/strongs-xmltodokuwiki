====== H5402: נֶשֶׁק (nesheq) ======

===== Source =====

or   ; from  H5401 ;

===== Meaning =====

military  equipment , i.e. (collectively)  arms  (offensive or defensive), or (concretely) an  arsenal

===== Definitions =====

  - equipment
  - arms
  - arsenal

===== Usage =====

armed men, armour(-y), battle, harness, weapon.
