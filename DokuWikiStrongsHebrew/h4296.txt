====== H4296: מִטָּה (miṭṭâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from H5186;

===== Meaning =====

a **bed** (as extended) for sleeping or eating; by analogy, a **sofa**, **litter** or **bier**

===== Definitions =====

  - bed
  - sofa
  - litter
  - bier

===== Usage =====

bed(-chamber), bier.
