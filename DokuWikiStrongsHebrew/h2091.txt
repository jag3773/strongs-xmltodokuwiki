====== H2091: זָהָב (zâhâb) ======

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to shimmer;

===== Meaning =====

gold , figuratively,  something gold-colored  (i.e. yellow), as  oil , a  clear sky

===== Definitions =====

  - gold
  - something gold-colored
  - oil
  - clear sky

===== Usage =====

gold(-en), fair weather.
