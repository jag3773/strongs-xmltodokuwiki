====== H2334: חַוּוֹת יָעִיר (Chavvôwth Yâʻîyr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from the plural of H2333 and a modification of H3265;

===== Meaning =====

**hamlets of Jair**, a region of Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - hamlets of Jair

===== Usage =====

(Bashan-) Havoth-jair.
