====== H4486: מַנְדַּע (mandaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to [[en:strongs:h4093|H4093]];

===== Meaning =====

**wisdom** or **intelligence**

===== Definitions =====

  - wisdom
  - intelligence

===== Usage =====

knowledge, reason, understanding.
