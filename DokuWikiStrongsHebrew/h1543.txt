====== H1543: גֻּלָּה (gullâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine from [[en:strongs:h1556|H1556]];

===== Meaning =====

a **fountain**, **bowl** or **globe** (all as **round**)

===== Definitions =====

  - fountain
  - bowl
  - globe
  - round

===== Usage =====

bowl, pommel, spring.
