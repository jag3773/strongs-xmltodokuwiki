====== H7231: רָבַב (râbab) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root; (compare [[en:strongs:h7241|H7241]]); also as denominative from [[en:strongs:h7233|H7233]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **cast together** , i.e. **increase**, especially in number; to **multiply by the myriad**

===== Definitions =====

  - cast together
  - increase
  - multiply by the myriad

===== Usage =====

increase, be many(-ifold), be more, multiply, ten thousands.
