====== H4117: מָהַר (mâhar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root (perhaps rather the same as [[en:strongs:h4116|H4116]] through the idea of readiness in assent);

===== Meaning =====

to **bargain** (for a wife), i.e. to **wed**

===== Definitions =====

  - bargain
  - wed

===== Usage =====

endow, × surely.
