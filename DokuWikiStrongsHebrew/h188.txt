====== H188: אוֹי (ʼôwy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

inj

===== Source =====

probably from H183 (in the sense of **crying** out after);

===== Meaning =====

**lamentation**; also interjectionally **Oh!**

===== Definitions =====

  - crying
  - lamentation
  - Oh!

===== Usage =====

alas, woe.
