====== H8498: תְּכוּנָה (tᵉkûwnâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine passive participle of H8505;

===== Meaning =====

**adjustment**, i.e. **structure**; by implication, **equipage**

===== Definitions =====

  - adjustment
  - structure
  - equipage

===== Usage =====

fashion, store.
