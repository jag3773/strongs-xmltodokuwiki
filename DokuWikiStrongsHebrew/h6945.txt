====== H6945: קָדֵשׁ (qâdêsh) ======

===== Source =====

from  H6942 ;

===== Meaning =====

a (quasi)  sacred person , i.e. (technically) a (male)  devotee  (by prostitution) to  licentious idolatry

===== Definitions =====

  - sacred person
  - devotee
  - licentious idolatry

===== Usage =====

sodomite, unclean.
