====== H4929: מִשְׁמָר (mishmâr) ======

===== Source =====

from  H8104 ;

===== Meaning =====

a  guard  (the man, the post or the prison); a  deposit  (figuratively); also (as observed) a usage (abstractly), or an  example  (concretely)

===== Definitions =====

  - guard
  - deposit
  - example

===== Usage =====

diligence, guard, office, prison, ward, watch.
