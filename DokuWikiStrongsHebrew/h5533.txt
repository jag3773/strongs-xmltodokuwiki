====== H5533: סָכַן (çâkan) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

probably a denominative from H7915;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **cut**, i.e. **damage**; also to **grow** (causatively, **make**) **poor**

===== Definitions =====

  - cut
  - damage
  - grow
  - make
  - poor

===== Usage =====

endanger, impoverish.
