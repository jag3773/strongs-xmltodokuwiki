====== H4749: מִקְשָׁה (miqshâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine of H4748;

===== Meaning =====

**rounded** work, i.e. moulded by **hammering** (**repoussé**)

===== Definitions =====

  - rounded
  - hammering
  - repoussé

===== Usage =====

beaten (out of one piece, work), upright, whole piece.
