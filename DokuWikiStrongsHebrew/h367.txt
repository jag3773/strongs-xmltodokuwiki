====== H367: אֵימָה (ʼêymâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or (shortened) [[en:strongs:|]]; from the same as [[en:strongs:h366|H366]];

===== Meaning =====

**fright**; concrete, an **idol** (as a bugbear)

===== Definitions =====

  - fright
  - idol

===== Usage =====

dread, fear, horror, idol, terrible, terror.
