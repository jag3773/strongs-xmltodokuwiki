====== H367: אֵימָה (ʼêymâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

or (shortened) ; from the same as H366;

===== Meaning =====

**fright**; concrete, an **idol** (as a bugbear)

===== Definitions =====

  - fright
  - idol

===== Usage =====

dread, fear, horror, idol, terrible, terror.
