====== H375: אֵיפֹה (ʼêyphôh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adverb

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h335|H335]] and [[en:strongs:h6311|H6311]];

===== Meaning =====

**what place?**; also (of time) **when?**; or (of means) **how?**

===== Definitions =====

  - what place?
  - when?
  - how?

===== Usage =====

what manner, where.
