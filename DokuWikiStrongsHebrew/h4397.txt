====== H4397: מֲלְאָךְ (mălʼâk) ======

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to   as a deputy;

===== Meaning =====

despatch a  messenger ; specifically, of God, i.e. an  angel  (also a prophet, priest or teacher)

===== Definitions =====

  - despatch
  - messenger
  - angel

===== Usage =====

ambassador, angel, king, messenger.
