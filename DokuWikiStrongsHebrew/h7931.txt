====== H7931: שָׁכַן (shâkan) ======

===== Source =====

a primitive root (apparently akin (by transmission) to  H7901  through the idea of lodging; compare  H5531 ,  H7925 );

===== Meaning =====

to  reside  or  permanently stay  (literally or figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - reside
  - permanently stay

===== Usage =====

abide, continue, (cause to, make to) dwell(-er), have habitation, inhabit, lay, place, (cause to) remain, rest, set (up).
