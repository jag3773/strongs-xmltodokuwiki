====== H2226: זֶרַח (Zerach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

the same as [[en:strongs:h2225|H2225]];

===== Meaning =====

**Zerach**, the name of three Israelites, also of an Idumaean and an Ethiopian prince

===== Definitions =====

  - Zerach

===== Usage =====

Zarah, Zerah.
