====== H3374: יִרְאָה (yirʼâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine of H3373;

===== Meaning =====

**fear** (also used as infinitive); morally, **reverence**

===== Definitions =====

  - fear
  - reverence

===== Usage =====

× dreadful, × exceedingly, fear(-fulness).
