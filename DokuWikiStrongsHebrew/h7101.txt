====== H7101: קָצִין (qâtsîyn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H7096 in the sense of **determining**;

===== Meaning =====

a **magistrate** (as **deciding**) or other **leader**

===== Definitions =====

  - determining
  - magistrate
  - deciding
  - leader

===== Usage =====

captain, guide, prince, ruler. Compare
