====== H5246: נָמֵר (nâmêr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning properly, to **filtrate**, i.e. **be limpid** (comp H5247 and H5249); and thus to **spot** or **stain** as if by dripping;

===== Meaning =====

a **leopard** (from its stripes)

===== Definitions =====

  - filtrate
  - be limpid
  - spot
  - stain
  - leopard

===== Usage =====

leopard.
