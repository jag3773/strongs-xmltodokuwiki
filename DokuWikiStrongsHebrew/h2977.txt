====== H2977: יֹאשִׁיָּה (Yôʼshîyâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

or ; from the same root as H803 and H3050; **founded of Jah**;

===== Meaning =====

**Joshijah**, the name of two Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - founded of Jah
  - Joshijah

===== Usage =====

Josiah.
