====== H8492: תִּירוֹשׁ (tîyrôwsh) ======

===== Source =====

or   ; from  H3423  in the sense of expulsion;

===== Meaning =====

must  or  fresh grape-juice  (as just squeezed out); by implication (rarely)  fermented wine

===== Definitions =====

  - must
  - fresh grape-juice
  - fermented wine

===== Usage =====

(new, sweet) wine.
