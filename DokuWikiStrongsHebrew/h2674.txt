====== H2674: חָצוֹר (Châtsôwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

a collective form of H2691; village;

===== Meaning =====

**Chatsor**, the name (thus simply) of two places in Palestine and of one in Arabia

===== Definitions =====

  - Chatsor

===== Usage =====

Hazor.
