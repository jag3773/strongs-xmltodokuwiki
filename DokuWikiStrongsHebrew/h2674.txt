====== H2674: חָצוֹר (Châtsôwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - locative

===== Source =====

a collective form of [[en:strongs:h2691|H2691]]; village;

===== Meaning =====

**Chatsor**, the name (thus simply) of two places in Palestine and of one in Arabia

  - Chatsor
