====== H7025: קִיר חֶרֶשׂ (Qîyr Cheres) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

or (feminine of the latter word) ; from H7023 and H2789; **fortress of earthenware**;

===== Meaning =====

**Kir-Cheres** or **Kir-Chareseth**, a place in Moab

===== Definitions =====

  - fortress of earthenware
  - Kir-Cheres
  - Kir-Chareseth

===== Usage =====

Kir-haraseth, Kir-hareseth, Kirharesh, Kir-heres.
