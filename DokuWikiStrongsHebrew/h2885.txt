====== H2885: טַבַּעַת (ṭabbaʻath) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H2883;

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **seal** (as **sunk** into the wax), i.e. **signet** (for sealing); hence (generally) a **ring** of any kind

===== Definitions =====

  - seal
  - sunk
  - signet
  - ring

===== Usage =====

ring.
