====== H6370: פִּילֶגֶשׁ (pîylegesh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; of uncertain derivation;

===== Meaning =====

a **concubine**; also (masculine) a **paramour**

===== Definitions =====

  - concubine
  - paramour

===== Usage =====

concubine, paramour.
