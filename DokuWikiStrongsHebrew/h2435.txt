====== H2435: חִיצוֹן (chîytsôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

from H2434;

===== Meaning =====

properly, the (outer) **wall side**; hence, **exterior**; figuratively, **secular** (as opposed to sacred)

===== Definitions =====

  - wall side
  - exterior
  - secular

===== Usage =====

outer, outward, utter, without.
