====== H4948: מִשְׁקָל (mishqâl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h8254|H8254]];

===== Meaning =====

**weight** (numerically estimated); hence, **weighing** (the act)

===== Definitions =====

  - weight
  - weighing

===== Usage =====

(full) weight.
