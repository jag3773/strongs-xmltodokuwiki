====== H7610: שְׁאָר יָשׁוּב (Shᵉʼâr Yâshûwb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from H7605 and H7725; a **remnant will return**;

===== Meaning =====

**Shear-Jashub**, the symbolic name of one of Isaiah's sons

===== Definitions =====

  - remnant will return
  - Shear-Jashub

===== Usage =====

Shear-jashub.
