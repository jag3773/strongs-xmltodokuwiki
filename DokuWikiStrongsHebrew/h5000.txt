====== H5000: נָאוֶה (nâʼveh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h4998|H4998]] or [[en:strongs:h5116|H5116]];

===== Meaning =====

**suitable**, or **beautiful**

===== Definitions =====

  - suitable
  - beautiful

===== Usage =====
