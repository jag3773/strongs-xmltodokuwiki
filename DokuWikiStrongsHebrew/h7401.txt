====== H7401: רָכַךְ (râkak) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **soften** (intransitively or transitively), used figuratively

===== Definitions =====

  - soften

===== Usage =====

(be) faint(-hearted), mollify, (be, make) soft(-er), be tender.
