====== H2489: חֵלְכָא (chêlᵉkâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; apparently from an unused root probably meaning to be dark or (figuratively) unhappy;

===== Meaning =====

a **wretch**, i.e. **unfortunate**

===== Definitions =====

  - wretch
  - unfortunate

===== Usage =====

poor.
