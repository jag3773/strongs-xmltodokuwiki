====== H5631: סָרִיס (çârîyç) ======

===== Source =====

or   ; from an unused root meaning to  ;

===== Meaning =====

castrate a  eunuch ; by implication,  valet  (especially of the female apartments), and thus, a  minister  of state

===== Definitions =====

  - castrate
  - eunuch
  - valet
  - minister

===== Usage =====

chamberlain, eunuch, officer. Compare
