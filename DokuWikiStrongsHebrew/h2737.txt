====== H2737: חָרוּז (chârûwz) ======

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to perforate;

===== Meaning =====

properly,  pierced , i.e. a  bead of pearl ,  gems  or  jewels  (as strung)

===== Definitions =====

  - pierced
  - bead of pearl
  - gems
  - jewels

===== Usage =====

chain.
