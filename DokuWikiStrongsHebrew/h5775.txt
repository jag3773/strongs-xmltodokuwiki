====== H5775: עוֹף (ʻôwph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H5774;

===== Meaning =====

a **bird** (as covered with feathers, or rather as covering with wings), often collectively

===== Definitions =====

  - bird

===== Usage =====

bird, that flieth, flying, fowl.
