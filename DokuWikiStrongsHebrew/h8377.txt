====== H8377: תְּאוֹ (tᵉʼôw) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

and ; (the original form), from H8376;

===== Meaning =====

a species of **antelope** (probably from the white **stripe** on the cheek)

===== Definitions =====

  - antelope
  - stripe

===== Usage =====

wild bull (ox).
