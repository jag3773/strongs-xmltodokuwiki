====== H1215: בֶּצַע (betsaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h1214|H1214]];

===== Meaning =====

**plunder**; by extension, **gain** (usually unjust)

===== Definitions =====

  - plunder
  - gain

===== Usage =====

covetousness, (dishonest) gain, lucre, profit.
