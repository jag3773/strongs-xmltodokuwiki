====== H6278: עֵת קָצִין (ʻÊth Qâtsîyn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from H6256 and H7011; **time of a judge**;

===== Meaning =====

**Eth-Katsin**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - time of a judge
  - Eth-Katsin

===== Usage =====

Ittahkazin (by including directive enclitic).
