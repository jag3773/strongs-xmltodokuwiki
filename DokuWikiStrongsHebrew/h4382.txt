====== H4382: מְכֵרָתִי (Mᵉkêrâthîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

patrial from an unused name (the same as H4380) of a place in Palestine;

===== Meaning =====

a **Mekerathite**, or inhabitant of Mekerah

===== Definitions =====

  - Mekerathite

===== Usage =====

Mecherathite.
