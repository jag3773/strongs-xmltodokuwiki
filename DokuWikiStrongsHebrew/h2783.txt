====== H2783: חֲרַץ (chărats) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from a root corresponding to [[en:strongs:h2782|H2782]] in the sense of vigor;

===== Meaning =====

the **loin** (as the seat of strength)

===== Definitions =====

  - loin

===== Usage =====

loin.
