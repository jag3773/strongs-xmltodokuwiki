====== H6984: קוּשָׁיָהוּ (qûwshâyâhûw) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from the passive participle of H6983 and H3050; **entrapped of Jah**;

===== Meaning =====

**Kushajah**, an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - entrapped of Jah
  - Kushajah

===== Usage =====

Kushaiah.
