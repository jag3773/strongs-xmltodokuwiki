====== H7210: רֳאִי (rŏʼîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H7200;

===== Meaning =====

**sight**, whether abstractly (**vision**) or concretely (a **spectacle**)

===== Definitions =====

  - sight
  - vision
  - spectacle

===== Usage =====

gazingstock, look to, (that) see(-th).
