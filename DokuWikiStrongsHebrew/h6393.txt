====== H6393: פְּלָדָה (pᵉlâdâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **divide**;

===== Meaning =====

a **cleaver**, i.e. **iron armature** (of a chariot)

===== Definitions =====

  - divide
  - cleaver
  - iron armature

===== Usage =====

torch.
