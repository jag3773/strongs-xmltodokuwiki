====== H3940: לַפִּיד (lappîyd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; from an unused root probably meaning to **shine**;

===== Meaning =====

a **flambeau**, **lamp** or **flame**

===== Definitions =====

  - shine
  - flambeau
  - lamp
  - flame

===== Usage =====

(fire-) brand, (burning) lamp, lightning, torch.
