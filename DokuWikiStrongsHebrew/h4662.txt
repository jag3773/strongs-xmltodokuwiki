====== H4662: מִפְקָד (miphqâd) ======

===== Source =====

from  H6485 ;

===== Meaning =====

an appointment , i.e.  mandate ; concretely,  a designated spot ; specifically,  a census

===== Definitions =====

  - an appointment
  - mandate
  - a designated spot
  - a census

===== Usage =====

appointed place, commandment, number.
