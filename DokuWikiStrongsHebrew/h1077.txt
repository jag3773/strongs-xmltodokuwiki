====== H1077: בַּל (bal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adv

===== Source =====

from H1086;

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **failure**; by implication **nothing**; usually (adverb) **not** at all; also **lest**

===== Definitions =====

  - failure
  - nothing
  - not
  - lest

===== Usage =====

lest, neither, no, none (that...), not (any), nothing.
