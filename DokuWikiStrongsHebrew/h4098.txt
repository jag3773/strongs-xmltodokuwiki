====== H4098: מְדֻשָּׁה (mᵉdushshâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h1758|H1758]];

===== Meaning =====

a **threshing**, i.e. (concretely and figuratively) **down-trodden** people

===== Definitions =====

  - threshing
  - down-trodden

===== Usage =====

threshing.
