====== H2526: חָם (Châm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

the same as H2525; hot (from the tropical habitat);

===== Meaning =====

**Cham**, a son of Noah; also (as a patronymic) his descendants or their country

===== Definitions =====

  - Cham

===== Usage =====

Ham.
