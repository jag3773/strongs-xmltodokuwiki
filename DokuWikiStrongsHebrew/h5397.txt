====== H5397: נְשָׁמָה (nᵉshâmâh) ======

===== Source =====

from  H5395 ;

===== Meaning =====

a  puff , i.e.  wind ,  angry  or  vital breath ,  divine inspiration ,  intellect . or (concretely) an  animal

===== Definitions =====

  - puff
  - wind
  - angry
  - vital breath
  - divine inspiration
  - intellect
  - animal

===== Usage =====

blast, (that) breath(-eth), inspiration, soul, spirit.
