====== H4550: מַסַּע (maççaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h5265|H5265]];

===== Meaning =====

a **departure** (from **striking** the tents), i.e. march (not necessarily a single day's travel); by implication, a **station** (or point of **departure**)

===== Definitions =====

  - departure
  - striking
  - station
  - departure

===== Usage =====

journey(-ing).
