====== H3644: כְּמוֹ (kᵉmôw) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

prt

===== Source =====

or ; a form of the prefix **k**, but used separately [compare H3651];

===== Meaning =====

**as**, **thus**, **so**

===== Definitions =====

  - k
  - as
  - thus
  - so

===== Usage =====

according to, (such) as (it were, well as), in comparison of, like (as, to, unto), thus, when, worth.
