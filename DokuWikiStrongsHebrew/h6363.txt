====== H6363: פֶּטֶר (peṭer) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; from [[en:strongs:h6362|H6362]];

===== Meaning =====

a **fissure**, i.e. (concretely) **firstling** (as opening the matrix)

===== Definitions =====

  - fissure
  - firstling

===== Usage =====

firstling, openeth, such as open.
