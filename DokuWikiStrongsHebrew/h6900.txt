====== H6900: קְבוּרָה (qᵉbûwrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

or ; feminine passive participle of H6912;

===== Meaning =====

**sepulture**; (concretely) a **sepulchre**

===== Definitions =====

  - sepulture
  - sepulchre

===== Usage =====

burial, burying place, grave, sepulchre.
