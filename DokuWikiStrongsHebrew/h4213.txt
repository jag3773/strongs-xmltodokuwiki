====== H4213: מִזְעָר (mizʻâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:strongs:h2191|H2191]];

===== Meaning =====

**fewness**; by implication, as superlative **diminutiveness**

===== Definitions =====

  - fewness
  - diminutiveness

===== Usage =====

few, × very.
