====== H4937: מִשְׁעֵן (mishʻên) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or ; from H8172;

===== Meaning =====

a **support** (concretely), i.e. (figuratively) a **protector** or **sustenance**

===== Definitions =====

  - support
  - protector
  - sustenance

===== Usage =====

stay.
