====== H3731: כַּפְתֹּר (Kaphtôr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

or (Amos 9:7) ; apparently the same as H3730;

===== Meaning =====

**Caphtor** (i.e. a wreath-shaped island), the original seat of the Philistines

===== Definitions =====

  - Caphtor

===== Usage =====

Caphtor.
