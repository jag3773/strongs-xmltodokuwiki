====== H8050: שְׁמוּאֵל (Shᵉmûwʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from the passive participle of H8085 and H410; heard of God;

===== Meaning =====

**Shemuël**, the name of three Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - Shemuël

===== Usage =====

Samuel, Shemuel.
