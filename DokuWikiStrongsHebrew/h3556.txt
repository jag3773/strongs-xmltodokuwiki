====== H3556: כּוֹכָב (kôwkâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

probably from the same as H3522 (in the sense of rolling) or H3554 (in the sense of blazing);

===== Meaning =====

a **star** (as round or as shining); figuratively, a **prince**

===== Definitions =====

  - star
  - prince

===== Usage =====

star(-gazer).
