====== H3545: כֵּהָה (kêhâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:strongs:h3544|H3544]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **weakening**; figuratively, **alleviation**, i.e. **cure**

===== Definitions =====

  - weakening
  - alleviation
  - cure

===== Usage =====

healing.
