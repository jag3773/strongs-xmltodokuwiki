====== H5153: נָחוּשׁ (nâchûwsh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

apparently passive participle of H5172 (perhaps in the sense of ringing), i.e. bell-metal; or from the red color of the throat of a serpent (H5175, as denominative) when hissing);

===== Meaning =====

**coppery**, i.e. (figuratively) **hard**

===== Definitions =====

  - coppery
  - hard

===== Usage =====

of brass.
