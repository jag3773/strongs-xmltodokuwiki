====== H4242: מְחִיר (mᵉchîyr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to **buy**;

===== Meaning =====

**price**, **payment**, **wages**

===== Definitions =====

  - buy
  - price
  - payment
  - wages

===== Usage =====

gain, hire, price, sold, worth.
