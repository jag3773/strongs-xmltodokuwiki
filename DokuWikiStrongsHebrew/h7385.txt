====== H7385: רִיק (rîyq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h7324|H7324]];

===== Meaning =====

**emptiness**; figuratively, a **worthless** thing; adverbially, **in vain**

===== Definitions =====

  - emptiness
  - worthless
  - in vain

===== Usage =====

empty, to no purpose, (in) vain (thing), vanity.
