====== H1184: בַּעֲלֵי יְהוּדָה (Baʻălêy Yᵉhûwdâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - locative

===== Source =====

from the plural of [[en:strongs:h1167|H1167]] and [[en:strongs:h3063|H3063]]; masters of Judah;

===== Meaning =====

**Baale-Jehudah**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Baale-Jehudah

===== Usage =====

Baale of Judah.
