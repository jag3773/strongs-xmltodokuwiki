====== H1184: בַּעֲלֵי יְהוּדָה (Baʻălêy Yᵉhûwdâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from the plural of H1167 and H3063; masters of Judah;

===== Meaning =====

**Baale-Jehudah**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Baale-Jehudah

===== Usage =====

Baale of Judah.
