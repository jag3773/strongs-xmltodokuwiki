===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

probably by erroneous transcription for [[en:strongs:|]]; patronymically from [[en:strongs:h3612|H3612]];

===== Meaning =====

a **Calebite** or descendant of Caleb

===== Definitions =====

===== Usage =====
