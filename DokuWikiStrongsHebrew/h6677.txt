====== H6677: צַוָּאר (tsavvâʼr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; (Nehemiah 3:5), or [[en:strongs:|]]; (Song of Solomon 4:9), or (feminine) [[en:strongs:|]]; (Micah 2:3), intensively from [[en:strongs:h6696|H6696]] in the sense of **binding**;

===== Meaning =====

the back of the **neck** (as that on which burdens are **bound**)

===== Definitions =====

  - binding
  - neck
  - bound

===== Usage =====

neck.
