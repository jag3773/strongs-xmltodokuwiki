====== H2389: חָזָק (châzâq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h2388|H2388]];

===== Meaning =====

**strong** (usu. in a bad sense, **hard**, **bold**, **violent**)

===== Definitions =====

  - strong
  - hard
  - bold
  - violent

===== Usage =====

harder, hottest,   impudent, loud, mighty, sore, stiff(-hearted), strong(-er).
