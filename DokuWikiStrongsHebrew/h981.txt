====== H981: בָּטָא (bâṭâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **babble**; hence, to **vociferate angrily**

===== Definitions =====

  - babble
  - vociferate angrily

===== Usage =====

pronounce, speak (unadvisedly).
