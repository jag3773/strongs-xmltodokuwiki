====== H1019: בֵּית הַגִּלְגָּל (Bêyth hag-Gilgâl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from H1004 and H1537 with the article interposed; house of Gilgal (or rolling);

===== Meaning =====

**Beth-hag-Gilgal**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Beth-hag-Gilgal

===== Usage =====

Beth-gilgal.
