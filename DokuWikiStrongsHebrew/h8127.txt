====== H8127: שֵׁן (shên) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h8150|H8150]]; compare	[[en:strongs:h8143|H8143]]

===== Meaning =====

a **tooth** (as **sharp**); specifically **ivory**; figuratively, a **cliff**

===== Definitions =====

  - tooth
  - sharp
  - ivory
  - cliff

===== Usage =====

crag, × forefront, ivory, × sharp, tooth.
