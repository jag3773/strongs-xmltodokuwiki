====== H8127: שֵׁן (shên) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from H8150; compare	H8143

===== Meaning =====

a **tooth** (as **sharp**); specifically **ivory**; figuratively, a **cliff**

===== Definitions =====

  - tooth
  - sharp
  - ivory
  - cliff

===== Usage =====

crag, × forefront, ivory, × sharp, tooth.
