====== H3494: יִתְלָה (Yithlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

probably from [[en:strongs:h8518|H8518]]; it will hang, i.e. **be high**;

===== Meaning =====

**Jithlah**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - be high
  - Jithlah

===== Usage =====

Jethlah.
