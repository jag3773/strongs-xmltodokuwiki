====== H6596: פֹּת (pôth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; (Ezekiel 13:19), from an unused root meaning to **open**;

===== Meaning =====

a **hole**, i.e. **hinge** or the female **pudenda**

===== Definitions =====

  - open
  - hole
  - hinge
  - pudenda

===== Usage =====

hinge, secret participle
