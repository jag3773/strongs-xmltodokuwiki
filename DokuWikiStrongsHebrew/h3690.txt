====== H3690: כִּסְלָה (kiçlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:strongs:h3689|H3689]];

===== Meaning =====

in a good sense, **trust**; in a bad one, **silliness**

===== Definitions =====

  - trust
  - silliness

===== Usage =====

confidence, folly.
