====== H3833: לָבִיא (lâbîyʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun

===== Source =====

or (Ezekiel 19:2) [[en:strongs:|]]; irregular masculine plural [[en:strongs:|]]; irregular feminine plural [[en:strongs:|]]; from an unused root meaning to **roar**; compare [[en:strongs:h738|H738]]

===== Meaning =====

a **lion** (properly, a lioness as the fiercer (although not a roarer;))

===== Definitions =====

  - roar
  - lion

===== Usage =====

(great, old, stout) lion, lioness, young (lion).
