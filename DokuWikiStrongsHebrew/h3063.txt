====== H3063: יְהוּדָה (Yᵉhûwdâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h3034|H3034]]; **celebrated**;

===== Meaning =====

**Jehudah** (or Judah), the name of five Israelites; also of the tribe descended from the first, and of its territory

===== Definitions =====

  - celebrated
  - Jehudah

===== Usage =====

Judah.
