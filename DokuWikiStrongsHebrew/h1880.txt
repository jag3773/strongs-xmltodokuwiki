====== H1880: דֶּשֶׁן (deshen) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H1878;

===== Meaning =====

the **fat**; abstractly **fatness**, i.e. (figuratively) **abundance**; specifically the (fatty) **ashes** of sacrifices

===== Definitions =====

  - fat
  - fatness
  - abundance
  - ashes

===== Usage =====

ashes, fatness.
