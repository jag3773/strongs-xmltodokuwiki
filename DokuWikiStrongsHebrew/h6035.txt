====== H6035: עָנָו (ʻânâv) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (by intermixture with [[en:strongs:h6041|H6041]]) [[en:strongs:|]]; from [[en:strongs:h6031|H6031]];

===== Meaning =====

**depressed** (figuratively), in mind (**gentle**) or circumstances (**needy**, especially **saintly**)

===== Definitions =====

  - depressed
  - gentle
  - needy
  - saintly

===== Usage =====

humble, lowly, meek, poor. Compare
