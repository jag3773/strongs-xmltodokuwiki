====== H1690: דְּבֵלָה (dᵉbêlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from an unused root (akin to H2082) probably meaning to **press** together;

===== Meaning =====

a **cake** of pressed figs

===== Definitions =====

  - press
  - cake

===== Usage =====

cake (lump) of figs.
