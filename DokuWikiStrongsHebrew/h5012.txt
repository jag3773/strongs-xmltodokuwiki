====== H5012: נָבָא (nâbâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **prophesy**, i.e. **speak** (or sing) **by inspiration** (in prediction or simple discourse)

===== Definitions =====

  - prophesy
  - speak
  - by inspiration

===== Usage =====

prophesy(-ing), make self a prophet.
