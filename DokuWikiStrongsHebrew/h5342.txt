====== H5342: נֵצֶר (nêtser) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H5341 in the sense of **greenness** as a striking color;

===== Meaning =====

a **shoot**; figuratively, a **descendant**

===== Definitions =====

  - greenness
  - shoot
  - descendant

===== Usage =====

branch.
