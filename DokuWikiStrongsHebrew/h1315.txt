====== H1315: בׇּשְׂמַת (Bosmath) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-f

===== Source =====

feminine of H1314 (the second form); **fragrance**;

===== Meaning =====

**Bosmath**, the name of a wife of Esau, and of a daughter of Solomon

===== Definitions =====

  - fragrance
  - Bosmath

===== Usage =====

Bashemath, Basmath.
