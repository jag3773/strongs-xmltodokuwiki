====== H831: אַשְׁקְלוֹן (ʼAshqᵉlôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

probably from H8254 in the sense of weighing-place (i.e. mart);

===== Meaning =====

**Ashkelon**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Ashkelon

===== Usage =====

Ashkelon, Askalon.
