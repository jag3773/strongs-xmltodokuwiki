====== H1100: בְּלִיַּעַל (bᵉlîyaʻal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H1097 and H3276;(often in connection with H376, H802, H1121, etc.)

===== Meaning =====

**without profit**, **worthlessness**; by extension, **destruction**, **wickedness**

===== Definitions =====

  - without profit
  - worthlessness
  - destruction
  - wickedness

===== Usage =====

Belial, evil, naughty, ungodly (men), wicked.
