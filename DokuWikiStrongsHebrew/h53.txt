====== H53: אֲבִישָׁלוֹם (ʼĂbîyshâlôwm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

or (shortened)  ; from H1 and H7965; father of peace (i.e. friendly);

===== Meaning =====

**Abshalom**, a son of David; also (the fuller form) a later Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - Abshalom

===== Usage =====

Abishalom, Absalom.
