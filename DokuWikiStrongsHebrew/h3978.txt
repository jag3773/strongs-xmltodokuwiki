====== H3978: מַאֲכָל (maʼăkâl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h398|H398]];

===== Meaning =====

an **eatable** (includ. provender, flesh and fruit)

===== Definitions =====

  - eatable

===== Usage =====

food, fruit, (bake-)meat(-s), victual.
