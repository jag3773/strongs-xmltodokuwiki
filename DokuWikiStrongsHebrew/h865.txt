====== H865: אֶתְמוֹל (ʼethmôwl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adv

===== Source =====

or ; or ; probably from H853 or H854 and H4136;

===== Meaning =====

**heretofore**; definitely **yesterday**

===== Definitions =====

  - heretofore
  - yesterday

===== Usage =====

  before (that) time,   heretofore, of late (old),   times past, yester(day).
