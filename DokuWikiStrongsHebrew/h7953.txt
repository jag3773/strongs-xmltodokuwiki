====== H7953: שָׁלָה (shâlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root (rather cognate (by contraction) to the base of H5394, H7997 and their congeners through the idea of extracting);

===== Meaning =====

to **draw out** or **off**, i.e. **remove** (the soul by death)

===== Definitions =====

  - draw out
  - off
  - remove

===== Usage =====

take away.
