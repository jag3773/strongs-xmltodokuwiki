====== H7662: שְׁבַק (shᵉbaq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to the root of [[en:strongs:h7733|H7733]];

===== Meaning =====

to **quit**, i.e. **allow to remain**

===== Definitions =====

  - quit
  - allow to remain

===== Usage =====

leave, let alone.
