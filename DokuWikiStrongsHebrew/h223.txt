====== H223: אוּרִיָּה (ʼÛwrîyâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

or (prolonged)  ; from H217 and H3050; flame of Jah;

===== Meaning =====

**Urijah**, the name of one Hittite and five Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - Urijah

===== Usage =====

Uriah, Urijah.
