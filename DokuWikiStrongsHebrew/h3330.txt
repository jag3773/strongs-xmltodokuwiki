====== H3330: יַצִּיב (yatstsîyb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from [[en:strongs:h3321|H3321]];

===== Meaning =====

**fixed**, **sure**; concretely, **certainty**

===== Definitions =====

  - fixed
  - sure
  - certainty

===== Usage =====

certain(-ty), true, truth.
