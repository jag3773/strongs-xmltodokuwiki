====== H5106: נוּא (nûwʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **refuse**, **forbid**, **dissuade**, or **neutralize**

===== Definitions =====

  - refuse
  - forbid
  - dissuade
  - neutralize

===== Usage =====

break, disallow, discourage, make of none effect.
