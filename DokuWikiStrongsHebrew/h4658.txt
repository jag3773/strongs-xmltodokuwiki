====== H4658: מַפֶּלֶת (mappeleth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from H5307;

===== Meaning =====

**fall**, i.e. **decadence**; concretely, a **ruin**; specifically a **carcase**

===== Definitions =====

  - fall
  - decadence
  - ruin
  - carcase

===== Usage =====

carcase, fall, ruin.
