====== H4671: מֹץ (môts) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or ; (Zephaniah 2:2), from H4160;

===== Meaning =====

**chaff** (as **pressed** out, i.e. **winnowed** or (rather) threshed loose)

===== Definitions =====

  - chaff
  - pressed
  - winnowed

===== Usage =====

chaff.
