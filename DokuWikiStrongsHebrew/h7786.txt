====== H7786: שׂוּר (sûwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **vanquish**; by implication, to **rule** (causatively, crown)

===== Definitions =====

  - vanquish
  - rule
