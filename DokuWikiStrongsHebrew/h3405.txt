====== H3405: יְרִיחוֹ (Yᵉrîychôw) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

or ; or variation (1 Kings 16:34) ; perhaps from H3394; its month; or else from H7306; **fragrant**;

===== Meaning =====

**Jericho** or **Jerecho**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - fragrant
  - Jericho
  - Jerecho

===== Usage =====

Jericho.
