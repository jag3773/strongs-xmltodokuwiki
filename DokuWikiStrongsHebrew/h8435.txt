====== H8435: תּוֹלְדָה (tôwlᵉdâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

or ; from H3205;

===== Meaning =====

(plural only) **descent**, i.e. **family**; (figuratively) **history**

===== Definitions =====

  - descent
  - family
  - history

===== Usage =====

birth, generations.
