====== H5909: עַכְבָּר (ʻakbâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

probably from the same as H5908 in the secondary sense of **attacking**;

===== Meaning =====

a **mouse** (as **nibbling**)

===== Definitions =====

  - attacking
  - mouse
  - nibbling

===== Usage =====

mouse.
