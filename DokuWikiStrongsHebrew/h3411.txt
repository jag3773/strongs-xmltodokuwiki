====== H3411: יְרֵכָה (yᵉrêkâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine of H3409;

===== Meaning =====

properly, the **flank**; but used only figuratively, the **rear** or **recess**

===== Definitions =====

  - flank
  - rear
  - recess

===== Usage =====

border, coast, part, quarter, side.
