====== H1626: גֵּרָה (gêrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from H1641 (as in H1625); properly, (like H1620) a **kernel** (round as if **scraped**), i.e.

===== Meaning =====

a **gerah** or small weight (and coin)

===== Definitions =====

  - kernel
  - scraped
  - gerah

===== Usage =====

gerah.
