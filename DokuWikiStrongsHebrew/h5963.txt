====== H5963: עַלְמֹן דִּבְלָתָיְמָה (ʻAlmôn Diblâthâyᵉmâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from the same as H5960 and the dual of H1960 (compare H1015) with enclitic of direction; Almon towards Diblathajim;

===== Meaning =====

**Almon-Diblathajemah**, a place in Moab

===== Definitions =====

  - Almon-Diblathajemah

===== Usage =====

Almondilathaim.
