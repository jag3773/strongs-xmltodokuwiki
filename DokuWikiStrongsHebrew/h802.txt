====== H802: אִשָּׁה (ʼishshâh) ======

===== Source =====

feminine of  H376  or  H582 ; irregular plural,   ;(used in the same wide sense as  H582 )

===== Meaning =====

a  woman

===== Definitions =====

  - woman

===== Usage =====

(adulter) ess, each, every, female, × many,   none, one,   together, wife, woman. Often unexpressed in English.
