====== H8219: שְׁפֵלָה (shᵉphêlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h8213|H8213]];

===== Meaning =====

**Lowland**, i.e. (with the article) the maritime slope of Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Lowland

===== Usage =====

low country, (low) plain, vale(-ley).
