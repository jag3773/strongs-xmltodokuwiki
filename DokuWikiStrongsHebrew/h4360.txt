====== H4360: מִכְלֻל (miklul) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h3634|H3634]];

===== Meaning =====

something **perfect**, i.e. a **splendid garment**

===== Definitions =====

  - perfect
  - splendid garment

===== Usage =====

all sorts.
