====== H575: אָן (ʼân) ======

===== Source =====

or   ; contracted from  H370 ;

===== Meaning =====

where? ; hence,  whither? ,  when? ; also  hither  and  thither

===== Definitions =====

  - where?
  - whither?
  - when?
  - hither
  - thither

===== Usage =====

  any (no) whither, now, where, whither(-soever).
