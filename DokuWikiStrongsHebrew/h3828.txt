====== H3828: לְבוֹנָה (lᵉbôwnâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; from [[en:strongs:h3836|H3836]];

===== Meaning =====

**frankincense** (from its **whiteness** or perhaps that of its smoke)

===== Definitions =====

  - frankincense
  - whiteness

===== Usage =====

(frank-) incense.
