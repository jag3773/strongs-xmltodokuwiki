====== H5635: סָרַף (çâraph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **cremate**, i.e. to **be** (near) **of kin** (such being privileged to kindle the pyre)

===== Definitions =====

  - cremate
  - be
  - of kin

===== Usage =====

burn.
