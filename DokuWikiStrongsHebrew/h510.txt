====== H510: אַלְקוּם (ʼalqûwm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

probably from [[en:strongs:h408|H408]] and [[en:strongs:h6965|H6965]];

===== Meaning =====

a **non-rising** (i.e. resistlessness)

===== Definitions =====

  - non-rising

===== Usage =====

no rising up.
