====== H6287: פְּאֵר (pᵉʼêr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h6286|H6286]];

===== Meaning =====

an **embellishment**, i.e. fancy **head-dress**

===== Definitions =====

  - embellishment
  - head-dress

===== Usage =====

beauty, bonnet, goodly, ornament, tire.
