====== H7741: שָׁוֵה קִרְיָתַיִם (Shâvêh Qiryâthayim) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from the same as H7740 and the dual of H7151; **plain of a double city**;

===== Meaning =====

**Shaveh-Kirjathajim**, a place East of the Jordan

===== Definitions =====

  - plain of a double city
  - Shaveh-Kirjathajim

===== Usage =====

Shaveh Kiriathaim.
