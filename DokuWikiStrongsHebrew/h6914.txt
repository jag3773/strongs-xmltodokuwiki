====== H6914: קִבְרוֹת הַתַּאֲוָה (Qibrôwth hat-Taʼă-vâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr

===== Source =====

from the feminine plural of H6913 and H8378 with the article interposed; **graves of the longing**;

===== Meaning =====

**Kibroth-hat-Taavh**, a place in the Desert

===== Definitions =====

  - graves of the longing
  - Kibroth-hat-Taavh

===== Usage =====

Kibroth-hattaavah.
