====== H7645: שְׁבַנְיָה (Shᵉbanyâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

or ; from the same as H7644 and H3050; **Jah has grown** (i.e. prospered);

===== Meaning =====

**Shebanjah**, the name of three or four Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - Jah has grown
  - Shebanjah

===== Usage =====

Shebaniah.
