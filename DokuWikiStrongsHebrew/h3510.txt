====== H3510: כָּאַב (kâʼab) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **feel pain**; by implication, to **grieve**; figuratively, to **spoil**

===== Definitions =====

  - feel pain
  - grieve
  - spoil

===== Usage =====

grieving, mar, have pain, make sad (sore), (be) sorrowful.
