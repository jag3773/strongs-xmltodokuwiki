====== H4651: מַפָּל (mappâl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h5307|H5307]];

===== Meaning =====

a **falling off**, i.e. **chaff**; also something **pendulous**, i.e. a **flap**

===== Definitions =====

  - falling off
  - chaff
  - pendulous
  - flap

===== Usage =====

flake, refuse.
