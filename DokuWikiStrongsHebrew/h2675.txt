====== H2675: חָצוֹר חֲדַתָּה (Châtsôwr Chădattâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h2674|H2674]] and a Aramaizing form of the feminine of [[en:strongs:h2319|H2319]] (compare [[en:strongs:h2323|H2323]]);

===== Meaning =====

**new Chatsor**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - new Chatsor

===== Usage =====

Hazor, Hadattah (as if two places).
