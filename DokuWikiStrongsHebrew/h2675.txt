====== H2675: חָצוֹר חֲדַתָּה (Châtsôwr Chădattâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from H2674 and a Aramaizing form of the feminine of H2319 (compare H2323);

===== Meaning =====

**new Chatsor**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - new Chatsor

===== Usage =====

Hazor, Hadattah (as if two places).
