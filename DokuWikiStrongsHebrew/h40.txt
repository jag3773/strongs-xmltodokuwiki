====== H40: אֲבִימֶלֶךְ (ʼĂbîymelek) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from H1 and H4428; father of (the) king;

===== Meaning =====

**Abimelek**, the name of two Philistine kings and of two Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - Abimelek

===== Usage =====

Abimelech.
