====== H3430: יִשְׁבּוֹ בְּנֹב (Yishbôw bᵉ-Nôb) ======

===== Source =====

from  H3427  and  H5011 , with a pronominal suffix and a preposition interposed; his dwelling (is) in Nob;

===== Meaning =====

Jishbo-be-Nob , a Philistine

===== Definitions =====

  - Jishbo-be-Nob

===== Usage =====

Ishbi-benob (from the margin).
