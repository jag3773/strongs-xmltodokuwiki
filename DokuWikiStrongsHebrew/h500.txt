====== H500: אֶלְעָלֵא (ʼElʻâlêʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

or (more properly) ; from H410 and H5927; **God** (is) **going up**;

===== Meaning =====

**Elale** or **Elaleh**, a place east of the Jordan

===== Definitions =====

  - God
  - going up
  - Elale
  - Elaleh

===== Usage =====

Elealeh.
