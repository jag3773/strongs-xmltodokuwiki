====== H4120: מְהֵרָה (mᵉhêrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:strongs:h4118|H4118]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **hurry**; hence (adverbially) **promptly**

===== Definitions =====

  - hurry
  - promptly

===== Usage =====

hastily, quickly, shortly, soon, make (with) speed(-ily), swiftly.
