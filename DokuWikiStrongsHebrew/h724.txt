====== H724: אֲרוּכָה (ʼărûwkâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

or ; feminine passive participle of H748 (in the sense of **restoring** to soundness);

===== Meaning =====

**wholeness** (literally or figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - restoring
  - wholeness

===== Usage =====

health, made up, perfected.
