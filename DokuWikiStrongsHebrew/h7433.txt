====== H7433: רָמֹת גִּלעָד (Râmôth Gilʻâd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

or ; (2 Chronicles 22:5), from the plural of H7413 and H1568; **heights of Gilad**;

===== Meaning =====

**Ramoth-Gilad**, a place East of the Jordan

===== Definitions =====

  - heights of Gilad
  - Ramoth-Gilad

===== Usage =====

Ramoth-gilead, Ramoth in Gilead. See also
