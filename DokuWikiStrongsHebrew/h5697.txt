====== H5697: עֶגְלָה (ʻeglâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:strongs:h5695|H5695]];

===== Meaning =====

a (female) **calf**, especially one nearly grown (i.e. a **heifer**)

===== Definitions =====

  - calf
  - heifer

===== Usage =====

calf, cow, heifer.
