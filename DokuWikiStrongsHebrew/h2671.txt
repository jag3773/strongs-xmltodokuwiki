====== H2671: חֵץ (chêts) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h2686|H2686]]; also by interchange for [[en:strongs:h6086|H6086]]

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **piercer**, i.e. an **arrow**; by implication, a **wound**; figuratively, (of God) thunder-**bolt**; the **shaft** of a spear

===== Definitions =====

  - piercer
  - arrow
  - wound
  - bolt
  - shaft

===== Usage =====

  archer, arrow, dart, shaft, staff, wound.
