====== H1331: בְּתוּלִים (bᵉthûwlîym) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

masculine plural of the same as [[en:strongs:h1330|H1330]];

===== Meaning =====

(collectively and abstractly) **virginity**; by implication and concretely, the **tokens** of it

===== Definitions =====

  - virginity
  - tokens

===== Usage =====

× maid, virginity.
