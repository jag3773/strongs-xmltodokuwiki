====== H5298: נֶפֶג (Nepheg) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from an unused root probably meaning to **spring forth**; a **sprout**;

===== Meaning =====

**Nepheg**, the name of two Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - spring forth
  - sprout
  - Nepheg

===== Usage =====

Nepheg.
