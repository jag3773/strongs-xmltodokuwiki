====== H5574: סְנוּאָה (Çᵉnûwʼâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

or  from the same as H5570;

===== Meaning =====

**pointed**; (used with the article as a proper name) **Senuah**, the name of two Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - pointed
  - Senuah

===== Usage =====

Hasenuah (including the art), Senuah.
