====== H6737: צָיַר (tsâyar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a denominative from H6735 in the sense of ambassador;

===== Meaning =====

to **make an errand**, i.e. **betake oneself**

===== Definitions =====

  - make an errand
  - betake oneself

===== Usage =====

make as if...had been ambassador.
