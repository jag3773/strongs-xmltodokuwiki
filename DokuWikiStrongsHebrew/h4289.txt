====== H4289: מַחְתָּה (machtâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

the same as [[en:strongs:h4288|H4288]] in the sense of **removal**;

===== Meaning =====

a **pan** for live coals

===== Definitions =====

  - removal
  - pan

===== Usage =====

censer, firepan, snuffdish.
