====== H7620: שָׁבוּעַ (shâbûwaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; also (feminine) [[en:strongs:|]]; properly, passive participle of [[en:strongs:h7650|H7650]] as a denominative of [[en:strongs:h7651|H7651]];

===== Meaning =====

literally, **sevened**, i.e. a **week** (specifically, of years)

===== Definitions =====

  - sevened
  - week

===== Usage =====

seven, week.
