====== H5410: נָתִיב (nâthîyb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or (feminine) [[en:strongs:|]]; or [[en:strongs:|]]; (Jeremiah 6:16), from an unused root meaning to **tramp**;

===== Meaning =====

a (beaten) **track**

===== Definitions =====

  - tramp
  - track

===== Usage =====

path(-way), × travel(-ler), way.
