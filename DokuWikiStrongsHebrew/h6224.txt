====== H6224: עֲשִׂירִי (ʻăsîyrîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h6235|H6235]];

===== Meaning =====

**tenth**; by abbreviation, **tenth month** or (feminine) **part**

===== Definitions =====

  - tenth
  - tenth month
  - part

===== Usage =====

tenth (part).
