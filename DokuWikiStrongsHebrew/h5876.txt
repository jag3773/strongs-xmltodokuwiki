====== H5876: עֵין חַדָּה (ʻÊyn Chaddâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from H5869 and the feminine of a derivative from H2300; **fountain of sharpness**;

===== Meaning =====

**En-Chaddah**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - fountain of sharpness
  - En-Chaddah

===== Usage =====

En-haddah.
