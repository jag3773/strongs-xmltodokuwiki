====== H8263: שֶׁקֶץ (sheqets) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h8262|H8262]];

===== Meaning =====

**filth**, i.e. (figuratively and specifically) an **idolatrous** object

===== Definitions =====

  - filth
  - idolatrous

===== Usage =====

abominable(-tion).
