====== H3726: כְּפַר הָעַמּוֹנִי (Kᵉphar hâ-ʻAmmôwnîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from H3723 and H5984, with the article interposed; village of the Ammonite;

===== Meaning =====

**Kefar-ha-Ammoni**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Kefar-ha-Ammoni

===== Usage =====

Chefarhaamonai.
