====== H1470: גּוֹזָן (Gôwzân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

probably from [[en:strongs:h1468|H1468]]; a **quarry** (as a place of **cutting** stones);

===== Meaning =====

**Gozan**, a province of Assyria

===== Definitions =====

  - quarry
  - cutting
  - Gozan

===== Usage =====

Gozan.
