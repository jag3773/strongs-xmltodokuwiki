====== H7230: רֹב (rôb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H7231;

===== Meaning =====

**abundance** (in any respect)

===== Definitions =====

  - abundance

===== Usage =====

abundance(-antly), all, × common (sort), excellent, great(-ly, -ness, number), huge, be increased, long, many, more in number, most, much, multitude, plenty(-ifully), × very (age).
