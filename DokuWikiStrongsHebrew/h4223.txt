====== H4223: מְחָא (mᵉchâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to [[en:strongs:h4222|H4222]];

===== Meaning =====

to **strike in pieces**; also to **arrest**; specifically to **impale**

===== Definitions =====

  - strike in pieces
  - arrest
  - impale

===== Usage =====

hang, smite, stay.
