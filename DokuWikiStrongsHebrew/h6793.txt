====== H6793: צִנָּה (tsinnâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:strongs:h6791|H6791]];

===== Meaning =====

a **hook** (as **pointed**); also a (large) **shield** (as if guarding by **prickliness**); also **cold** (as **piercing**)

===== Definitions =====

  - hook
  - pointed
  - shield
  - prickliness
  - cold
  - piercing

===== Usage =====

buckler, cold, hook, shield, target.
