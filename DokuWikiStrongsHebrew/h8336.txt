====== H8336: שֵׁשׁ (shêsh) ======

===== Source =====

or (for alliteration with  H4897 )   ; for  H7893 ;

===== Meaning =====

bleached  stuff, i.e.  white  linen or (by analogy) marble

===== Definitions =====

  - bleached
  - white

===== Usage =====

× blue, fine (twined) linen, marble, silk.
