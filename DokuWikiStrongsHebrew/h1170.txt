====== H1170: בַּעַל בְּרִית (Baʻal Bᵉrîyth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from H1168 and H1285; Baal of (the) covenant;

===== Meaning =====

**Baal-Berith**, a special deity of the Shechemites

===== Definitions =====

  - Baal-Berith

===== Usage =====

Baal-berith.
