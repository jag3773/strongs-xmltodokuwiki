====== H8487: תֵּימָן (Têymân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

or ; the same as H8486;

===== Meaning =====

**Teman**, the name of two Edomites, and of the region and descendant of one of them

===== Definitions =====

  - Teman

===== Usage =====

south, Teman.
