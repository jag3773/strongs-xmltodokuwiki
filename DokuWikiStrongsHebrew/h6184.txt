====== H6184: עָרִיץ (ʻârîyts) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

from H6206;

===== Meaning =====

**fearful**, i.e. **powerful** or **tyrannical**

===== Definitions =====

  - fearful
  - powerful
  - tyrannical

===== Usage =====

mighty, oppressor, in great power, strong, terrible, violent.
