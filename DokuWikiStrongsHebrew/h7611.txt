====== H7611: שְׁאֵרִית (shᵉʼêrîyth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from H7604;

===== Meaning =====

a **remainder** or residual (surviving, final) portion

===== Definitions =====

  - remainder

===== Usage =====

that had escaped, be left, posterity, remain(-der), remnant, residue, rest.
