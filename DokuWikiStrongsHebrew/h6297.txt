====== H6297: פֶּגֶר (peger) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h6296|H6296]];

===== Meaning =====

a **carcase** (as limp), whether of man or beast; figuratively, an **idolatrous image**

===== Definitions =====

  - carcase
  - idolatrous image

===== Usage =====

carcase, corpse, dead body.
