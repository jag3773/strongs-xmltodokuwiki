====== H2953: טְפַר (ṭᵉphar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from a root corresponding to H6852, and meaning the same as H6856;

===== Meaning =====

a **finger-nail**; also a **hoof** or **claw**

===== Definitions =====

  - finger-nail
  - hoof
  - claw

===== Usage =====

nail.
