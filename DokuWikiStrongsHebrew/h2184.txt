====== H2184: זְנוּת (zᵉnûwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h2181|H2181]];

===== Meaning =====

**adultery**, i.e. (figuratively) **infidelity**, **idolatry**

===== Definitions =====

  - adultery
  - infidelity
  - idolatry

===== Usage =====

whoredom.
