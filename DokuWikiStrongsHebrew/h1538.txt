====== H1538: גֻּלְגֹּלֶת (gulgôleth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

by reduplication from [[en:strongs:h1556|H1556]];

===== Meaning =====

a **skull** (as **round**); by implication, a **head** (in enumeration of persons)

===== Definitions =====

  - skull
  - round
  - head

===== Usage =====

head, every man, poll, skull.
