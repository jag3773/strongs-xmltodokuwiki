====== H8003: שָׁלֵם (shâlêm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

from H7999;

===== Meaning =====

**complete** (literally or figuratively); especially **friendly**

===== Definitions =====

  - complete
  - friendly

===== Usage =====

full, just, made ready, peaceable, perfect(-ed), quiet, Shalem (by mistake for a name), whole.
