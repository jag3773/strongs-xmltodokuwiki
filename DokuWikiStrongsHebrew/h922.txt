====== H922: בֹּהוּ (bôhûw) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from an unused root (meaning to be empty);

===== Meaning =====

a **vacuity**, i.e. (superficially) an **undistinguishable ruin**

===== Definitions =====

  - vacuity
  - undistinguishable ruin

===== Usage =====

emptiness, void.
