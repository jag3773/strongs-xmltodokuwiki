====== H2680: חֲצִי הַמְּנַחְתִּי (Chătsîy ham-Mᵉnachtîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

patronymically from [[en:strongs:h2679|H2679]];

===== Meaning =====

a **Chatsi-ham-Menachtite** or descendant of Chatsi-ham-Menuchoth

===== Definitions =====

  - Chatsi-ham-Menachtite

===== Usage =====

half of the Manahethites.
