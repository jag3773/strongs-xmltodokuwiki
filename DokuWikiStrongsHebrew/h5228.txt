====== H5228: נָכֹחַ (nâkôach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

from the same as H5226;

===== Meaning =====

**straightforward**, i.e. (figuratively), **equitable**, **correct**, or (abstractly), **integrity**

===== Definitions =====

  - straightforward
  - equitable
  - correct
  - integrity

===== Usage =====

plain, right, uprightness.
