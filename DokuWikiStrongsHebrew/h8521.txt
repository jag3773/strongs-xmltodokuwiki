====== H8521: תֵּל חַרְשָׁא (Têl Charshâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from H8510 and the feminine of H2798; **mound of workmanship**;

===== Meaning =====

**Tel-Charsha**, a place in Babylonia

===== Definitions =====

  - mound of workmanship
  - Tel-Charsha

===== Usage =====

Tel-haresha, Tel-harsa.
