====== H6593: פִּשְׁתֶּה (pishteh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:strongs:h6580|H6580]] as in the sense of **comminuting**;

===== Meaning =====

**linen** (i.e. the thread, as **carded**)

===== Definitions =====

  - comminuting
  - linen
  - carded

===== Usage =====

flax, linen.
