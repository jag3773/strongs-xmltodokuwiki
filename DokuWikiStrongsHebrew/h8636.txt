====== H8636: תַּרְבִּית (tarbîyth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h7235|H7235]];

===== Meaning =====

**multiplication**, i.e. **percentage** or **bonus** in addition to principal

===== Definitions =====

  - multiplication
  - percentage
  - bonus

===== Usage =====

increase, unjust gain.
