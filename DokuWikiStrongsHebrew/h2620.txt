====== H2620: חָסָה (châçâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root; (compare H982)

===== Meaning =====

to **flee** for protection; figuratively, to **confide** in

===== Definitions =====

  - flee
  - confide

===== Usage =====

have hope, make refuge, (put) trust.
