====== H4971: מַתְכֹנֶת (mathkôneth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

or ; from H8505 in the transferred sense of measuring;

===== Meaning =====

**proportion** (in size, number or ingredients)

===== Definitions =====

  - proportion

===== Usage =====

composition, measure, state, tale.
