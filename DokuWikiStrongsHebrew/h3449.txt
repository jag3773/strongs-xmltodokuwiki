====== H3449: יִשִּׁיָּה (Yishshîyâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

or ; from H5383 and H3050; **Jah will lend**;

===== Meaning =====

**Jishshijah**, the name of five Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - Jah will lend
  - Jishshijah

===== Usage =====

Ishiah, Isshiah, Ishijah, Jesiah.
