====== H1721: דֹּדָנִים (Dôdânîym) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

or (by orthographical error)  (1 Chronicles 1:7); a plural of uncertain derivation;

===== Meaning =====

**Dodanites**, or descendants of a son of Javan

===== Definitions =====

  - Dodanites

===== Usage =====

Dodanim.
