====== H2723: חׇרְבָּה (chorbâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:strongs:h2721|H2721]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **drought**, i.e. (by implication) a **desolation**

===== Definitions =====

  - drought
  - desolation

===== Usage =====

decayed place, desolate (place, -tion), destruction, (laid) waste (place).
