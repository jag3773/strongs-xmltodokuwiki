====== H374: אֵיפָה (ʼêyphâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

or (shortened) [[en:strongs:|]] ; of Egyptian derivation;

===== Meaning =====

an **ephah** or measure for grain; hence, a **measure** in general

===== Definitions =====

  - ephah
  - measure

===== Usage =====

ephah, (divers) measure(-s).
