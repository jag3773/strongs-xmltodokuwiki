====== H4260: מַחֲמָאָה (machămâʼâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

a denominative from [[en:strongs:h2529|H2529]];

===== Meaning =====

something **buttery** (i.e. unctuous and pleasant), as (figuratively) **flattery**

===== Definitions =====

  - buttery
  - flattery

===== Usage =====

× than butter.
