====== H692: אַרְאֵלִי (ʼArʼêlîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from H691; **heroic**;

===== Meaning =====

**Areli** (or an Arelite, collectively), an Israelite and his descendants

===== Definitions =====

  - heroic
  - Areli

===== Usage =====

Areli, Arelites.
