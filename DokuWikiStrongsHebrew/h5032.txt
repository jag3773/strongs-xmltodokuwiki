====== H5032: נְבָיוֹת (Nᵉbâyôwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-f

===== Source =====

===== Meaning =====

**Nebajoth**, a son of Ismael, and the country settled by him

===== Definitions =====

  - fruitfulnesses
  - Nebajoth

===== Usage =====

Nebaioth, Nebajoth.
