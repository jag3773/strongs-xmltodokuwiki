====== H7782: שׁוֹפָר (shôwphâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; from [[en:strongs:h8231|H8231]] in the original sense of **incising**;

===== Meaning =====

a **cornet** (as giving a **clear** sound) or curved horn

===== Definitions =====

  - incising
  - cornet
  - clear

===== Usage =====

cornet, trumpet.
