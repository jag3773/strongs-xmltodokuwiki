====== H7160: קָרַן (qâran) ======

===== Source =====

a primitive root; to   or gore; used only as denominative from  H7161 ,

===== Meaning =====

push to  shoot out horns ; figuratively,  rays

===== Definitions =====

  - push
  - shoot out horns
  - rays

===== Usage =====

have horns, shine.
