====== H7160: קָרַן (qâran) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root; to **push** or gore; used only as denominative from H7161,

===== Meaning =====

to **shoot out horns**; figuratively, **rays**

===== Definitions =====

  - push
  - shoot out horns
  - rays

===== Usage =====

have horns, shine.
