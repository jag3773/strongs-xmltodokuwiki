====== H6055: עֲנַנְיָה (ʻĂnanyâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from H6049 and H3050; **Jah has covered**;

===== Meaning =====

**Ananjah**, the name of an Israelite and of a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Jah has covered
  - Ananjah

===== Usage =====

Ananiah.
