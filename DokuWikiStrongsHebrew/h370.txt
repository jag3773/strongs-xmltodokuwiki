====== H370: אַיִן (ʼayin) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adv

===== Source =====

probably identical with H369 in the sense of query (compare H336)

===== Meaning =====

**where?** (only in connection with prepositional prefix, whence)

===== Definitions =====

  - where?

===== Usage =====

whence, where.
