====== H5771: עָוֺן (ʻâvôn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; (2 Kings 7:9; Psalm 51:5 [7]), from [[en:strongs:h5753|H5753]];

===== Meaning =====

**perversity**, i.e. (moral) **evil**

===== Definitions =====

  - perversity
  - evil

===== Usage =====

fault, iniquity, mischeif, punishment (of iniquity), sin.
