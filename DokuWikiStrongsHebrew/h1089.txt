====== H1089: בָּלַהּ (bâlahh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (rather by transposition for [[en:strongs:h926|H926]]);

===== Meaning =====

to **palpitate**; hence, (causatively) to **terrify**

===== Definitions =====

  - palpitate
  - terrify

===== Usage =====

trouble.
