====== H289: אֲחִימַן (ʼĂchîyman) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

or  ; from H251 and H4480; brother of a portion (i.e. gift);

===== Meaning =====

**Achiman**, the name of an Anakite and of an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - Achiman

===== Usage =====

Ahiman.
