====== H7722: שׁוֹא (shôwʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m n-f

===== Source =====

or (feminine) ; or ; from an unused root meaning to rush over;

===== Meaning =====

a **tempest**; by implication, **devastation**

===== Definitions =====

  - tempest
  - devastation

===== Usage =====

desolate(-ion), destroy, destruction, storm, wasteness.
