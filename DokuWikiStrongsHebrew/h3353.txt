====== H3353: יָקוּשׁ (yâqûwsh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

passive participle of H3369;

===== Meaning =====

properly, **entangled**, i.e. by implication (intransitively) a **snare**, or (transitive) a **snarer**

===== Definitions =====

  - entangled
  - snare
  - snarer

===== Usage =====

fowler, snare.
