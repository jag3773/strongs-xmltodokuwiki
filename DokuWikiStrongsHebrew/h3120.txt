====== H3120: יָוָן (Yâvân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

probably from the same as [[en:strongs:h3196|H3196]]; effervescing (i.e. hot and active);

===== Meaning =====

**Javan**, the name of a son of Joktan, and of the race (Ionians, i.e. Greeks) descended from him, with their territory; also of a place in Arabia

===== Definitions =====

  - Javan

===== Usage =====

Javan.
