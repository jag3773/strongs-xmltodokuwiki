====== H7802: שׁוּשַׁן עֵדוּת (Shûwshan ʻÊdûwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or (plural of former) [[en:strongs:|]]; from [[en:strongs:h7799|H7799]] and [[en:strongs:h5715|H5715]]; lily (or trumpet) of assemblage;

===== Meaning =====

**Shushan-Eduth** or **Shoshannim-Eduth**, the title of a popular song

===== Definitions =====

  - Shushan-Eduth
  - Shoshannim-Eduth

===== Usage =====

Shoshannim-Eduth, Shushan-eduth.
