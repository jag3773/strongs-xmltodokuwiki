====== H7802: שׁוּשַׁן עֵדוּת (Shûwshan ʻÊdûwth) ======

===== Source =====

or (plural of former)   ; from  H7799  and  H5715 ; lily (or trumpet) of assemblage;

===== Meaning =====

Shushan-Eduth  or  Shoshannim-Eduth , the title of a popular song

===== Definitions =====

  - Shushan-Eduth
  - Shoshannim-Eduth

===== Usage =====

Shoshannim-Eduth, Shushan-eduth.
