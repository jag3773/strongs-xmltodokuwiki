====== H7565: רֶשֶׁף (resheph) ======

===== Source =====

from  H8313 ;

===== Meaning =====

a  live coal ; by analogy  lightning ; figuratively, an  arrow , (as flashing through the air); specifically,  fever

===== Definitions =====

  - live coal
  - lightning
  - arrow
  - fever

===== Usage =====

arrow, (burning) coal, burning heat,   spark, hot thunderbolt.
