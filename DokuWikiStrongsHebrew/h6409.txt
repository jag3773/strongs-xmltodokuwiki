====== H6409: פַּלְטִיאֵל (Palṭîyʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from the same as H6404 and H410; **deliverance of God**;

===== Meaning =====

**Paltiel**, the name of two Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - deliverance of God
  - Paltiel

===== Usage =====

Paltiel, Phaltiel.
