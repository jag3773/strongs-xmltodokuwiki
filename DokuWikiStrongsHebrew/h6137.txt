====== H6137: עַקְרָב (ʻaqrâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

of uncertain derivation;

===== Meaning =====

a **scorpion**; figuratively, a **scourge** or **knotted whip**

===== Definitions =====

  - scorpion
  - scourge
  - knotted whip

===== Usage =====

scorpion.
