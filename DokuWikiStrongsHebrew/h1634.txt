====== H1634: גֶּרֶם (gerem) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h1633|H1633]];

===== Meaning =====

a **bone** (as the skeleton of the body); hence, **self**, i.e. (figuratively) **very**

===== Definitions =====

  - bone
  - self
  - very

===== Usage =====

bone, strong, top.
