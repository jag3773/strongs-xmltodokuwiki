====== H7342: רָחָב (râchâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adjective

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h7337|H7337]];

===== Meaning =====

**roomy**, in any (or every) direction, literally or figuratively

===== Definitions =====

  - roomy

===== Usage =====

broad, large, at liberty, proud, wide.
