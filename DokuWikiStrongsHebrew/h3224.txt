====== H3224: יְמִימָה (Yᵉmîymâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-f

===== Source =====

perhaps from the same as H3117; properly, warm, i.e. affectionate; hence, dove (compare H3123);

===== Meaning =====

**Jemimah**, one of Job's daughters

===== Definitions =====

  - Jemimah

===== Usage =====

Jemimah.
