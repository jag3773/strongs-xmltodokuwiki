====== H1420: גְּדוּלָה (gᵉdûwlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

or ; (shortened) or (less accurately) ; feminine of H1419;

===== Meaning =====

**greatness**; (concretely) **mighty acts**

===== Definitions =====

  - greatness
  - mighty acts

===== Usage =====

dignity, great things(-ness), majesty.
