====== H4610: מַעֲלֵה עַקְרַבִּים (Maʻălêh ʻAqrabbîym) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h4608|H4608]] and (the plural of) [[en:strongs:h6137|H6137]];

===== Meaning =====

**Steep of Scorpions**, a place in the Desert

===== Definitions =====

  - Steep of Scorpions

===== Usage =====

Maaleh-accrabim, the ascent (going up) of Akrabbim.
