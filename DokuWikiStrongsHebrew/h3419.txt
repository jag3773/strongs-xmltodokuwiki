====== H3419: יָרָק (yârâq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:strongs:h3418|H3418]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **green**; concretely, a **vegetable**

===== Definitions =====

  - green
  - vegetable

===== Usage =====

green, herbs.
