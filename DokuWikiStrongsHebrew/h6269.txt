====== H6269: עֲתָךְ (ʻĂthâk) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - locative

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to sojourn; **lodging**;

===== Meaning =====

**Athak**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - lodging
  - Athak

===== Usage =====

Athach.
