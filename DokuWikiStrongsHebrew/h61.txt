====== H61: אֲבָל (ʼăbâl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adv

===== Source =====

apparently from [[en:strongs:h56|H56]] through the idea of negation;

===== Meaning =====

**nay**, i.e. **truly** or **yet**

===== Definitions =====

  - nay
  - truly
  - yet

===== Usage =====

but, indeed, nevertheless, verily.
