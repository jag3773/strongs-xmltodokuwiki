====== H4069: מַדּוּעַ (maddûwaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

adv

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; from [[en:strongs:h4100|H4100]] and the passive participle of [[en:strongs:h3045|H3045]];

===== Meaning =====

**what** (is) **known?**; i.e. (by implication) (adverbially) **why?**

===== Definitions =====

  - what
  - known?
  - why?

===== Usage =====

how, wherefore, why.
