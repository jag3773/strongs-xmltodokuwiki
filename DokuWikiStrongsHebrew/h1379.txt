====== H1379: גָּבַל (gâbal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root; also as a denominative from H1366

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **twist** as a rope; to **bound** (as by a line)

===== Definitions =====

  - twist
  - bound

===== Usage =====

be border, set (bounds about).
