====== H1745: דּוּמָה (dûwmâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to be dumb (compare [[en:strongs:h1820|H1820]]);

===== Meaning =====

**silence**; figuratively, **death**

===== Definitions =====

  - silence
  - death

===== Usage =====

silence.
