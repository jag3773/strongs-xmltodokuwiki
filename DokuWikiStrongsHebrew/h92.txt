====== H92: אֲגֻדָּה (ʼăguddâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine passive participle of an unused root (meaning to **bind**);

===== Meaning =====

a **band**, **bundle**, **knot**, or **arch**

===== Definitions =====

  - bind
  - band
  - bundle
  - knot
  - arch

===== Usage =====

bunch, burden, troop.
