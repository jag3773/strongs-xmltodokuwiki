====== H92: אֲגֻדָּה (ʼăguddâh) ======

===== Source =====

feminine passive participle of an unused root (meaning to  );

===== Meaning =====

bind a  band ,  bundle ,  knot , or  arch

===== Definitions =====

  - bind
  - band
  - bundle
  - knot
  - arch

===== Usage =====

bunch, burden, troop.
