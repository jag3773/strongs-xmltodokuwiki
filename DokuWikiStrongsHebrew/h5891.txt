====== H5891: עֵיפָה (ʻÊyphâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m n-pr-f

===== Source =====

the same as [[en:strongs:h5890|H5890]];

===== Meaning =====

**Ephah**, the name of a son of Midian, and of the region settled by him; also of an Israelite and of an Israelitess

===== Definitions =====

  - Ephah

===== Usage =====

Ephah.
