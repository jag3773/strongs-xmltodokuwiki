====== H5069: נְדַב (nᵉdab) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to H5068;

===== Meaning =====

**be** (or give) **liberal**(-ly)

===== Definitions =====

  - be
  - liberal

===== Usage =====

(be minded of...own) freewill (offering), offer freely (willingly).
