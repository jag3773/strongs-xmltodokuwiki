====== H7756: שׂוּכָתִי (Sûwkâthîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

probably patronymic from a name corresponding to H7754 (feminine);

===== Meaning =====

a **Sukathite** or descendant of an unknown Israelite named Sukah

===== Definitions =====

  - Sukathite

===== Usage =====

Suchathite.
