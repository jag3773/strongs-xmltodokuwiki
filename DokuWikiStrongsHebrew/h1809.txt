====== H1809: דָּלַל (dâlal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root (compare [[en:strongs:h1802|H1802]]);

===== Meaning =====

to **slacken** or **be feeble**; figuratively, to **be oppressed**

===== Definitions =====

  - slacken
  - be feeble
  - be oppressed

===== Usage =====

bring low, dry up, be emptied, be not equal, fail, be impoverished, be made thin.
