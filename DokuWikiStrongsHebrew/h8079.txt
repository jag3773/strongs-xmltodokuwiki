====== H8079: שְׂמָמִית (sᵉmâmîyth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

probably from [[en:strongs:h8074|H8074]] (in the sense of poisoning);

===== Meaning =====

a **lizard** (from the superstition of its noxiousness)

===== Definitions =====

  - lizard

spider.
