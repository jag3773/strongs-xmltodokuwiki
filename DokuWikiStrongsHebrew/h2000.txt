====== H2000: הָמַם (hâmam) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root (compare [[en:strongs:h1949|H1949]], [[en:strongs:h1993|H1993]]);

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **put in commotion**; by implication, to **disturb**, **drive**, **destroy**

===== Definitions =====

  - put in commotion
  - disturb
  - drive
  - destroy

===== Usage =====

break, consume, crush, destroy, discomfit, trouble, vex.
