====== H2892: טֹהַר (ṭôhar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h2891|H2891]];

===== Meaning =====

literally **brightness**; ceremonially **purification**

===== Definitions =====

  - brightness
  - purification

===== Usage =====

clearness, glory, purifying.
