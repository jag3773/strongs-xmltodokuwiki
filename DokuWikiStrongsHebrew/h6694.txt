====== H6694: צוּק (tsûwq) ======

===== Source =====

a primitive root (identical with through the idea of   (of orifice));

===== Meaning =====

narrowness to  pour  out, i.e. (figuratively)  smelt ,  utter

===== Definitions =====

  - narrowness
  - pour
  - smelt
  - utter

===== Usage =====

be molten, pour.
