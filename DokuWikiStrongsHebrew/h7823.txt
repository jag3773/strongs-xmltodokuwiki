====== H7823: שָׁחִיס (shâchîyç) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or ; from an unused root apparently meaning to **sprout**;

===== Meaning =====

**after-growth**

===== Definitions =====

  - sprout
  - after-growth

===== Usage =====

(that) which springeth of the same.
