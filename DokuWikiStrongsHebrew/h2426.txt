====== H2426: חֵיל (chêyl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or (shortened) [[en:strongs:|]]; a collateral form of [[en:strongs:h2428|H2428]];

===== Meaning =====

an **army**; also (by analogy,) an **intrenchment**

===== Definitions =====

  - army
  - intrenchment

===== Usage =====

army, bulwark, host,   poor, rampart, trench, wall.
