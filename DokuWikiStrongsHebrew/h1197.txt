====== H1197: בָּעַר (bâʻar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root; also as denominative from H1198

===== Meaning =====

to **kindle**, i.e. **consume** (by fire or by eating); to **be (-come) brutish**

===== Definitions =====

  - kindle
  - consume
  - be (-come) brutish

===== Usage =====

be brutish, bring (put, take) away, burn, (cause to) eat (up), feed, heat, kindle, set (on fire), waste.
