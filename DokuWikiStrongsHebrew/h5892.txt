====== H5892: עִיר (ʻîyr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or (in the plural) [[en:strongs:|]]; or [[en:strongs:|]]; (Judges 10:4), from [[en:strongs:h5782|H5782]]

===== Meaning =====

a **city** (a place guarded by **waking** or a watch) in the widest sense (even of a mere **encampment** or **post**)

===== Definitions =====

  - city
  - waking
  - encampment
  - post

===== Usage =====

Ai (from margin), city, court (from margin), town.
