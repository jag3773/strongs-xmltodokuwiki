====== H6638: צָבָה (tsâbâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **amass**, i.e. **grow turgid**; specifically, to **array** an army against

===== Definitions =====

  - amass
  - grow turgid
  - array

===== Usage =====

fight swell.
