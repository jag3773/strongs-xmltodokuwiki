====== H8482: תַּחְתִּי (tachtîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h8478|H8478]];

===== Meaning =====

**lowermost**; as noun (feminine plural) the **depths** (figuratively, a **pit**, the **womb**)

===== Definitions =====

  - lowermost
  - depths
  - pit
  - womb

===== Usage =====

low (parts, -er, -er parts, -est), nether (part).
