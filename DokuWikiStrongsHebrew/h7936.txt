====== H7936: שָׂכַר (sâkar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

or (by permutation) ; (Ezra 4:5), a primitive root (apparently akin (by prosthesis) to H3739 through the idea of temporary purchase; compare H7937);

===== Meaning =====

to **hire**

===== Definitions =====

  - hire

===== Usage =====

earn wages, hire (out self), reward, × surely.
