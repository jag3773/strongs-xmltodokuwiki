====== H7936: שָׂכַר (sâkar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

or (by permutation) [[en:strongs:|]]; (Ezra 4:5), a primitive root (apparently akin (by prosthesis) to [[en:strongs:h3739|H3739]] through the idea of temporary purchase; compare [[en:strongs:h7937|H7937]]);

to **hire**

===== Definitions =====

===== Usage =====
