====== H3939: לַעֲנָה (laʻănâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from an unused root supposed to mean to curse;

===== Meaning =====

**wormwood** (regarded as poisonous, and therefore accursed)

===== Definitions =====

  - wormwood

===== Usage =====

hemlock, wormwood.
