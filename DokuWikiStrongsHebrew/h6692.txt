====== H6692: צוּץ (tsûwts) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **twinkle**, i.e. **glance**; by analogy, to **blossom** (figuratively, **flourish**)

===== Definitions =====

  - twinkle
  - glance
  - blossom
  - flourish

===== Usage =====

bloom, blossom, flourish, shew self.
