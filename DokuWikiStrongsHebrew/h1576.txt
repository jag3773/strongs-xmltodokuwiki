====== H1576: גְּמוּל (gᵉmûwl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h1580|H1580]];

===== Meaning =====

**treatment**, i.e. an **act** (of good or ill); by implication, **service** or **requital**

===== Definitions =====

  - treatment
  - act
  - service
  - requital

===== Usage =====

  as hast served, benefit, desert, deserving, that which he hath given, recompense, reward.
