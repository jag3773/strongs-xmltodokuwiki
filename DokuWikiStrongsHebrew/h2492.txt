====== H2492: חָלַם (châlam) ======

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to  bind firmly , i.e. (by implication) to  be  (causatively to  make )  plump ; also (through the figurative sense of dumbness) to  dream

===== Definitions =====

  - bind firmly
  - be
  - make
  - plump
  - dream

===== Usage =====

(cause to) dream(-er), be in good liking, recover.
