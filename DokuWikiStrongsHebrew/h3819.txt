====== H3819: לֹא רֻחָמָה (Lôʼ Ruchâmâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-f

===== Source =====

from H3808 and H7355; **not pitied**;

===== Meaning =====

**Lo-Ruchamah**, the symbolic name of a son of Hosea

===== Definitions =====

  - not pitied
  - Lo-Ruchamah

===== Usage =====

Lo-ruhamah.
