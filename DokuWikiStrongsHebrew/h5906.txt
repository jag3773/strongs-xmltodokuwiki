====== H5906: עַיִשׁ (ʻAyish) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

or ; from H5789;

===== Meaning =====

the **constellation of the Great Bear** (perhaps from its migration through the heavens)

===== Definitions =====

  - constellation of the Great Bear

===== Usage =====

Arcturus.
