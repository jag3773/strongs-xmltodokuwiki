====== H1081: בַּלְאֲדָן (Balʼădân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from H1078 and H113 (contracted); **Bel** (is his) **lord**;

===== Meaning =====

**Baladan**, the name of a Babylonian prince

===== Definitions =====

  - Bel
  - lord
  - Baladan

===== Usage =====

Baladan.
