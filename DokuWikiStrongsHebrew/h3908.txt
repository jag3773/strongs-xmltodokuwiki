====== H3908: לַחַשׁ (lachash) ======

===== Source =====

from  H3907 ;

===== Meaning =====

properly, a  whisper , i.e. by implication, (in a good sense) a private  prayer , (in a bad one) an  incantation ; concretely, an  amulet

===== Definitions =====

  - whisper
  - prayer
  - incantation
  - amulet

===== Usage =====

charmed, earring, enchantment, orator, prayer.
