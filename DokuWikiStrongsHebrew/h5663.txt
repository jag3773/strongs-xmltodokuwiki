====== H5663: עֶבֶד מֶלֶךְ (ʻEbed Melek) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from H5650 and H4428; **servant of a king**;

===== Meaning =====

**Ebed-Melek**, a eunuch of Zedekeah

===== Definitions =====

  - servant of a king
  - Ebed-Melek

===== Usage =====

Ebed-melech.
