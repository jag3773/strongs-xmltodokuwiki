====== H2235: זֵרֹעַ (zêrôaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; from [[en:strongs:h2232|H2232]];

===== Meaning =====

something **sown** (only in the plural), i.e. a **vegetable** (as food)

===== Definitions =====

  - sown
  - vegetable

===== Usage =====

pulse.
