====== H4866: מִשְׁבֵּר (mishbêr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h7665|H7665]];

===== Meaning =====

the **orifice** of the womb (from which the fœtus **breaks** forth)

===== Definitions =====

  - orifice
  - breaks

===== Usage =====

birth, breaking forth.
