====== H8314: שָׂרָף (sârâph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h8313|H8313]];

===== Meaning =====

**burning**, i.e. (figuratively) **poisonous** (serpent); specifically, a **saraph** or symbolical creature (from their copper color)

===== Definitions =====

  - burning
  - poisonous
  - saraph

===== Usage =====

fiery (serpent), seraph.
