===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; from an unused root meaning to **grow**; **growth**;

===== Meaning =====

**Shebna** or **Shebnah**, an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - grow
  - growth
  - Shebna
  - Shebnah

===== Usage =====
