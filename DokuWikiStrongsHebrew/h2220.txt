====== H2220: זְרוֹעַ (zᵉrôwaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or (shortened) [[en:strongs:|]]; and (feminine) [[en:strongs:|]]; or [[en:strongs:|]]; from [[en:strongs:h2232|H2232]];

===== Meaning =====

the **arm** (as **stretched** out), or (of animals) the **foreleg**; figuratively, **force**

===== Definitions =====

  - arm
  - stretched
  - foreleg
  - force

===== Usage =====

arm,   help, mighty, power, shoulder, strength.
