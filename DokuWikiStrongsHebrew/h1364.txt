====== H1364: גָּבֹהַּ (gâbôahh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

or ; (fully) from H1361;

===== Meaning =====

**elevated** (or elated), **powerful**, **arrogant**

===== Definitions =====

  - elevated
  - powerful
  - arrogant

===== Usage =====

haughty, height, high(-er), lofty, proud, × exceeding proudly.
