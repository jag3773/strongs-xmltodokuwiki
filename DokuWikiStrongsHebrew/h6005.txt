====== H6005: עִמָּנוּאֵל (ʻImmânûwʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from H5973 and with a pronominal suffix inserted; **with us** (is) **God**;

===== Meaning =====

**Immanuel**, a type name of Isaiah's son

===== Definitions =====

  - with us
  - God
  - Immanuel

===== Usage =====

Immanuel.
