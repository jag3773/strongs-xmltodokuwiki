====== H226: אוֹת (ʼôwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

probably from [[en:strongs:h225|H225]] (in the sense of **appearing**);

===== Meaning =====

a **signal** (literally or figuratively), as a **flag**, **beacon**, **monument**, **omen**, **prodigy**, **evidence**, etc.

===== Definitions =====

  - appearing
  - signal
  - flag
  - beacon
  - monument
  - omen
  - prodigy
  - evidence

===== Usage =====

mark, miracle, (en-) sign, token.
