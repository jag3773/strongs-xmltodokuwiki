====== H5057: נָגִיד (nâgîyd) ======

===== Source =====

or   ; from  H5046 ;

===== Meaning =====

a  commander  (as occupying the  front ), civil, military or religious; generally (abstractly, plural),  honorable  themes

===== Definitions =====

  - commander
  - front
  - honorable

===== Usage =====

captain, chief, excellent thing, (chief) governor, leader, noble, prince, (chief) ruler.
