====== H7667: שֶׁבֶר (sheber) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or ; from H7665;

===== Meaning =====

a **fracture**, figuratively, **ruin**; specifically, a **solution** (of a dream)

===== Definitions =====

  - fracture
  - ruin
  - solution

===== Usage =====

affliction, breach, breaking, broken(-footed, -handed), bruise, crashing, destruction, hurt, interpretation, vexation.
