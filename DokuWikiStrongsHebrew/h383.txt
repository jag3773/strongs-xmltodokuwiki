====== H383: אִיתַי (ʼîythay) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

prt

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to H3426;

===== Meaning =====

properly, **entity**; used only as a **particle of affirmation**, **there is**

===== Definitions =====

  - entity
  - particle of affirmation
  - there is

===== Usage =====

art thou, can, do ye, have, it be, there is (are), × we will not.
