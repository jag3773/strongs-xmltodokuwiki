====== H6085: עֶפְרוֹן (ʻEphrôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from the same as H6081; **fawn-like**;

===== Meaning =====

**Ephron**, the name of a Canaanite and of two places in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - fawn-like
  - Ephron

===== Usage =====

Ephron, Ephrain (from the margin).
