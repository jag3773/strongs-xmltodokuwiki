====== H4912: מָשָׁל (mâshâl) ======

===== Source =====

apparently from  H4910  in some original sense of   in mental action;

===== Meaning =====

superiority properly, a  pithy  maxim, usually of metaphorical nature; hence, a  simile  (as an adage, poem, discourse)

===== Definitions =====

  - superiority
  - pithy
  - simile

===== Usage =====

byword, like, parable, proverb.
