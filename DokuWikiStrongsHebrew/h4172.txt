====== H4172: מוֹרָא (môwrâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; or [[en:strongs:|]]; (Psalm 9:20), from [[en:strongs:h3372|H3372]];

===== Meaning =====

**fear**; by implication, a **fearful** thing or deed

===== Definitions =====

  - fear
  - fearful

===== Usage =====

dread, (that ought to be) fear(-ed), terribleness, terror.
