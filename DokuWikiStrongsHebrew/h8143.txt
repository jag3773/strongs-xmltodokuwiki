====== H8143: שֶׁנְהַבִּים (shenhabbîym) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H8127 and the plural apparently of a foreign word;

===== Meaning =====

probably, **tooth of elephants**, i.e. **ivory tusk**

===== Definitions =====

  - tooth of elephants
  - ivory tusk

===== Usage =====

ivory.
