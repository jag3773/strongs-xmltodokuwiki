====== H830: אַשְׁפֹּת (ʼashpôth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or ; or ; (contraction) plural of a noun of the same form as H827, from H8192 (in the sense of **scraping**);

===== Meaning =====

a heap of **rubbish** or **filth**

===== Definitions =====

  - scraping
  - rubbish
  - filth

===== Usage =====

dung (hill).
