====== H4694: מְצוּרָה (mᵉtsûwrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

or ; feminine of H4692;

===== Meaning =====

a **hemming** in, i.e. (objectively) a **mound** (of siege), or (subjectively) a **rampart** (of protection), (abstractly) **fortification**

===== Definitions =====

  - hemming
  - mound
  - rampart
  - fortification

===== Usage =====

fenced (city, fort, munition, strong hold.
