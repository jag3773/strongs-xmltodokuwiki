====== H4122: מַהֵר שָׁלָל חָשׁ בַּז (Mahêr Shâlâl Châsh Baz) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from H4118 and H7998 and H2363 and H957; **hasting** (is he (the enemy) to the) **booty**, **swift** (to the) **prey**;

===== Meaning =====

**Maher-Shalal-Chash-Baz**; the symbolical name of the son of Isaiah

===== Definitions =====

  - hasting
  - booty
  - swift
  - prey
  - Maher-Shalal-Chash-Baz

===== Usage =====

Maher-sha-lal-bash-baz.
