====== H6783: צְמִיתֻת (tsᵉmîythuth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; from [[en:strongs:h6789|H6789]];

===== Meaning =====

**excision**, i.e. **destruction**; used only (adverbially) with prepositional prefix to **extinction**, i.e. **perpetually**

===== Definitions =====

  - excision
  - destruction
  - extinction
  - perpetually

===== Usage =====

ever.
