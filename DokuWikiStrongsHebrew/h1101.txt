====== H1101: בָּלַל (bâlal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root; (also	denominatively from H1098) to fodder

===== Meaning =====

to **overflow** (specifically with oil.); by implication, to **mix**; to **fodder**

===== Definitions =====

  - overflow
  - mix
  - fodder

===== Usage =====

anoint, confound, × fade, mingle, mix (self), give provender, temper.
