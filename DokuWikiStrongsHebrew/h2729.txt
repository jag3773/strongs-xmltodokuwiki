====== H2729: חָרַד (chârad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **shudder** with terror; hence, to **fear**; also to **hasten** (with anxiety)

===== Definitions =====

  - shudder
  - fear
  - hasten

===== Usage =====

be (make) afraid, be careful, discomfit, fray (away), quake, tremble.
