====== H5797: עֹז (ʻôz) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or (fully) ; from H5810;

===== Meaning =====

**strength** in various applications (**force**, **security**, **majesty**, **praise**)

===== Definitions =====

  - strength
  - force
  - security
  - majesty
  - praise

===== Usage =====

boldness, loud, might, power, strength, strong.
