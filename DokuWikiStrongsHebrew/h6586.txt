====== H6586: פָּשַׁע (pâshaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root (identical with through the idea of **expansion**);

===== Meaning =====

to **break** away (from just authority), i.e. **trespass**, **apostatize**, **quarrel**

===== Definitions =====

  - expansion
  - break
  - trespass
  - apostatize
  - quarrel

===== Usage =====

offend, rebel, revolt, transgress(-ion, -or).
