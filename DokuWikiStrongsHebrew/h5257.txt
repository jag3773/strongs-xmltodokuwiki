====== H5257: נְסִיךְ (nᵉçîyk) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h5258|H5258]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, something **poured** out, i.e. a **libation**; also a molten **image**; by implication, a **prince** (as **anointed**)

===== Definitions =====

  - poured
  - libation
  - image
  - prince
  - anointed

===== Usage =====

drink offering, duke, prince(-ipal).
