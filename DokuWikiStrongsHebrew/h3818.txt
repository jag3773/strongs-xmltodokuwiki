====== H3818: לֹא עַמִּי (Lôʼ ʻAmmîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from H3808 and H5971 with pronominal suffix; **not my people**;

===== Meaning =====

**Lo-Ammi**, the symbolic name of a son of Hosea

===== Definitions =====

  - not my people
  - Lo-Ammi

===== Usage =====

Lo-ammi.
