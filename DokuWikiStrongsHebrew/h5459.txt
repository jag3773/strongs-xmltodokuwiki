====== H5459: סְגֻלָּה (çᵉgullâh) ======

===== Source =====

feminine passive participle of an unused root meaning to   up;

===== Meaning =====

shut wealth  (as closely  shut  up)

===== Definitions =====

  - shut
  - wealth
  - shut

===== Usage =====

jewel, peculiar (treasure), proper good, special.
