====== H7251: רָבַע (râbaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

verb

===== Source =====

a primitive root (rather identical with [[en:strongs:h7250|H7250]] through the idea of sprawling 'at all fours' (or possibly the reverse is the order of deriv.); compare [[en:strongs:h702|H702]]); properly, to be four (sided); used only as denominative of [[en:strongs:h7253|H7253]];

===== Meaning =====

to **be quadrate**

===== Definitions =====

  - be quadrate

===== Usage =====

(four-) square(-d).
