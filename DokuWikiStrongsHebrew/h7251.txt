====== H7251: רָבַע (râbaʻ) ======

===== Source =====

a primitive root (rather identical with  H7250  through the idea of sprawling 'at all fours' (or possibly the reverse is the order of deriv.); compare  H702 ); properly, to be four (sided); used only as denominative of  H7253 ;

===== Meaning =====

to  be quadrate

===== Definitions =====

  - be quadrate

===== Usage =====

(four-) square(-d).
