====== H5401: נָשַׁק (nâshaq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root (identical with H5400, through the idea of **fastening** up; compare H2388, H2836);

===== Meaning =====

to **kiss**, literally or figuratively (**touch**); also (as a mode of **attachment**), to **equip** with weapons

===== Definitions =====

  - fastening
  - kiss
  - touch
  - attachment
  - equip

===== Usage =====

armed (men), rule, kiss, that touched.
