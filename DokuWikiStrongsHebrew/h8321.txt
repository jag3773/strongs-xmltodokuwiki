====== H8321: שֹׂרֵק (sôrêq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or ; and (feminine) ; from H8319 in the sense of **redness** (compare H8320);

===== Meaning =====

a **vine** stock (properly, one yielding **purple** grapes, the richest variety)

===== Definitions =====

  - redness
  - vine
  - purple

===== Usage =====
