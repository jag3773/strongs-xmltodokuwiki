====== H4590: מַעַזְיָה (Maʻazyâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

or ; probably from H5756 (in the sense of protection) and H3050; **rescue of Jah**;

===== Meaning =====

**Maazjah**, the name of two Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - rescue of Jah
  - Maazjah

===== Usage =====

Maaziah.
