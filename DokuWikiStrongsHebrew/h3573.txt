====== H3573: כּוּשַׁן רִשְׁעָתַיִם (Kûwshan Rishʻâthayim) ======

===== Source =====

apparently from  H3572  and the dual of  H7564 ; Cushan of double wickedness;

===== Meaning =====

Cushan-Rishathajim , a Mesopotamian king

===== Definitions =====

  - Cushan-Rishathajim

===== Usage =====

Chushan-rishathayim.
