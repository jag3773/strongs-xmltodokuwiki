====== H8426: תּוֹדָה (tôwdâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from H3034;

===== Meaning =====

properly, an **extension** of the hand, i.e. (by implication) **avowal**, or (usually) **adoration**; specifically, a **choir** of worshippers

===== Definitions =====

  - extension
  - avowal
  - adoration
  - choir

===== Usage =====

confession, (sacrifice of) praise, thanks(-giving, offering).
