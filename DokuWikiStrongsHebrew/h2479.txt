====== H2479: חַלְחָלָה (chalchâlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

feminine from the same as [[en:strongs:h2478|H2478]];

===== Meaning =====

**writhing** (in childbirth); by implication, **terror**

===== Definitions =====

  - writhing
  - terror

===== Usage =====

(great, much) pain.
