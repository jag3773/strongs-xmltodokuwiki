====== H6495: פְּקַח־קוֹחַ (pᵉqach-qôwach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h6491|H6491]] redoubled;

===== Meaning =====

**opening** (of a dungeon), i.e. **jail-delivery** (figuratively, **salvation** for sin)

===== Definitions =====

  - opening
  - jail-delivery
  - salvation

===== Usage =====

opening of the prison.
