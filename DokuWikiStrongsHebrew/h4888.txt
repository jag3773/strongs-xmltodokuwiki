====== H4888: מִשְׁחָה (mishchâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; from [[en:strongs:h4886|H4886]];

===== Meaning =====

**unction** (the act); by implication, a consecratory **gift**

===== Definitions =====

  - unction
  - gift

===== Usage =====

(to be) anointed(-ing), ointment.
