====== H899: בֶּגֶד (beged) ======

===== Source =====

from  H898 ;

===== Meaning =====

a  covering , i.e.  clothing ; also  treachery  or  pillage

===== Definitions =====

  - covering
  - clothing
  - treachery
  - pillage

===== Usage =====

apparel, cloth(-es, ing), garment, lap, rag, raiment, robe, × very (treacherously), vesture, wardrobe.
