====== H1037: בֵּית מִלּוֹא (Bêyth Millôwʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

or ; from H1004 and H4407; house of (the) rampart;

===== Meaning =====

**Beth-Millo**, the name of two citadels

===== Definitions =====

  - Beth-Millo

===== Usage =====

house of Millo.
