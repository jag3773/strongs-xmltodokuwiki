====== H8421: תּוּב (tûwb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to [[en:strongs:h7725|H7725]], to come back;

===== Meaning =====

specifically (transitive and ellip.) to **reply**

===== Definitions =====

  - reply

===== Usage =====

answer, restore, return (an answer).
