====== H3791: כָּתָב (kâthâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H3789;

===== Meaning =====

something **written**, i.e. a **writing**, **record** or **book**

===== Definitions =====

  - written
  - writing
  - record
  - book

===== Usage =====

register, scripture, writing.
