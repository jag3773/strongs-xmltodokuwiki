====== H6746: צְלֹחִית (tsᵉlôchîyth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h6743|H6743]];

===== Meaning =====

something **prolonged** or **tall**, i.e. a **vial** or **salt-cellar**

===== Definitions =====

  - prolonged
  - tall
  - vial
  - salt-cellar

===== Usage =====

cruse.
