====== H3070: יְהֹוָה יִרְאֶה (Yᵉhôvâh yirʼeh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h3068|H3068]] and [[en:strongs:h7200|H7200]]; Jehovah will see (to it);

===== Meaning =====

**Jehovah-Jireh**, a symbolical name for Mount Moriah

===== Definitions =====

  - Jehovah-Jireh

===== Usage =====

Jehovah-jireh.
