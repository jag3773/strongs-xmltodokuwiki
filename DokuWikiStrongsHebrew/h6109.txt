====== H6109: עׇצְמָה (ʻotsmâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:strongs:h6108|H6108]];

===== Meaning =====

**powerfulness**; by extension, **numerousness**

===== Definitions =====

  - powerfulness
  - numerousness

===== Usage =====

abundance, strength.
