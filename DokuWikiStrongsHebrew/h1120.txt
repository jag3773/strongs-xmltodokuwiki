====== H1120: בָּמוֹת (Bâmôwth) ======

===== Source =====

plural of  H1116 ; heights; or (fully)   ; from the same and  H1168 ; heights of Baal;

===== Meaning =====

Bamoth  or  Bamoth-Baal , a place East of the Jordan

===== Definitions =====

  - Bamoth
  - Bamoth-Baal

===== Usage =====

Bamoth, Bamoth-baal.
