====== H3672: כִּנְּרוֹת (Kinnᵉrôwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

or ; respectively plural and singular feminine from the same as H3658; perhaps harp-shaped;

===== Meaning =====

**Kinneroth** or **Kinnereth**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Kinneroth
  - Kinnereth

===== Usage =====

Chinnereth, Chinneroth, Cinneroth.
