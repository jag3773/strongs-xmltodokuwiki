====== H3672: כִּנְּרוֹת (Kinnᵉrôwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - locative

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; respectively plural and singular feminine from the same as [[en:strongs:h3658|H3658]]; perhaps harp-shaped;

===== Meaning =====

**Kinneroth** or **Kinnereth**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Kinneroth
  - Kinnereth

===== Usage =====

Chinnereth, Chinneroth, Cinneroth.
