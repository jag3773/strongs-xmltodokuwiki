====== H4628: מַעֲרָב (maʻărâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or (feminine) ; from H6150, in the sense of **shading**;

===== Meaning =====

the **west** (as a region of the **evening** sun)

===== Definitions =====

  - shading
  - west
  - evening

===== Usage =====

west.
