====== H3491: יָתוּר (yâthûwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

passive participle of [[en:strongs:h3498|H3498]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **what is left**, i.e. (by implication) a **gleaning**

===== Definitions =====

  - what is left
  - gleaning

===== Usage =====

range.
