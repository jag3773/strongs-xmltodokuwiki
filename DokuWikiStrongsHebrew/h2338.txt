====== H2338: חוּט (chûwṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to the root of H2339, perhaps as a denominative;

===== Meaning =====

to **string together**, i.e. (figuratively) to **repair**

===== Definitions =====

  - string together
  - repair

===== Usage =====

join.
