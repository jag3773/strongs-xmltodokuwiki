====== H1109: בִּלְעָם (Bilʻâm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

probably from H1077 and H5971; not (of the) people, i.e. foreigner;

===== Meaning =====

**Bilam**, a Mesopotamian prophet; also a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Bilam

===== Usage =====

Balaam, Bileam.
