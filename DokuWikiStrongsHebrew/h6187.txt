====== H6187: עֵרֶךְ (ʻêrek) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H6186;

===== Meaning =====

a **pile**, **equipment**, **estimate**

===== Definitions =====

  - pile
  - equipment
  - estimate

===== Usage =====

equal, estimation, (things that are set in) order, price, proportion, × set at, suit, taxation, × valuest.
