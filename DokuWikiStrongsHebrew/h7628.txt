====== H7628: שְׁבִי (shᵉbîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H7618;

===== Meaning =====

**exiled**; **captured**; as noun, **exile** (abstractly or concretely and collectively); by extension, **booty**

===== Definitions =====

  - exiled
  - captured
  - exile
  - booty

===== Usage =====

captive(-ity), prisoners, × take away, that was taken.
