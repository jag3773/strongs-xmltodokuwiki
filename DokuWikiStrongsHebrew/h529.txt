====== H529: אֵמוּן (ʼêmûwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h539|H539]];

===== Meaning =====

**established**, i.e. (figuratively) **trusty**; also (abstractly) **trustworthiness**

===== Definitions =====

  - established
  - trusty
  - trustworthiness

===== Usage =====

faith(-ful), truth.
