====== H7468: רְעוּת (rᵉʻûwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from H7462 in the sense of H7453;

===== Meaning =====

a female **associate**; generally an **additional** one

===== Definitions =====

  - associate
  - additional

===== Usage =====

  another, mate, neighbour.
