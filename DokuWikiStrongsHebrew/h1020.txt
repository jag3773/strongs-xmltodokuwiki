====== H1020: בֵּית הַיְשִׁימוֹת (Bêyth ha-Yshîy-môwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - locative

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h1004|H1004]] and the plural of [[en:strongs:h3451|H3451]] with the article interposed; house of the deserts;

===== Meaning =====

**Beth-ha-Jeshimoth**, a town East of the Jordan

===== Definitions =====

  - Beth-ha-Jeshimoth

===== Usage =====

Beth-jeshimoth.
