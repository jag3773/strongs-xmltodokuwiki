====== H1020: בֵּית הַיְשִׁימוֹת (Bêyth ha-Yshîy-môwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from H1004 and the plural of H3451 with the article interposed; house of the deserts;

===== Meaning =====

**Beth-ha-Jeshimoth**, a town East of the Jordan

===== Definitions =====

  - Beth-ha-Jeshimoth

===== Usage =====

Beth-jeshimoth.
