====== H1319: בָּשַׂר (bâsar) ======

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to  be fresh , i.e.  full  ( rosy , figuratively  cheerful );  to announce  (glad news)

===== Definitions =====

  - be fresh
  - full
  - rosy
  - cheerful
  - to announce

===== Usage =====

messenger, preach, publish, shew forth, (bear, bring, carry, preach, good, tell good) tidings.
