====== H5161: נֶחֱלָמִי (Nechĕlâmîy) ======

===== Source =====

apparently a patronymic from an unused name (apparently passive participle of  H2492 );  ;

===== Meaning =====

dreamed a  Nechelamite , or descendant of Nechlam

===== Definitions =====

  - dreamed
  - Nechelamite

===== Usage =====

Nehelamite.
