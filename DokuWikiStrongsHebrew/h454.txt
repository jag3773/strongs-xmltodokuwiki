====== H454: אֶלְיְהוֹעֵינַי (ʼElyᵉhôwʻêynay) ======

===== Source =====

or (shortened)   ; from  H413  and  H3068  and  H5869 ; towards Jehovah (are) my eyes;

===== Meaning =====

Eljehoenai  or  Eljoenai , the name of seven Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - Eljehoenai
  - Eljoenai

===== Usage =====

Elihoenai, Elionai.
