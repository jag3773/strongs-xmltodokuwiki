====== H6240: עָשָׂר (ʻâsâr) ======

===== Source =====

for  H6235 ;

===== Meaning =====

ten  (only in combination), i.e.  -teen ; also (ordinal)  -teenth

===== Definitions =====

  - ten
  - -teen
  - -teenth

===== Usage =====

(eigh-, fif-, four-, nine-, seven-, six-, thir-) teen(-th), + eleven(-th), + sixscore thousand, + twelve(-th).
