====== H1546: גָּלוּת (gâlûwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine from [[en:strongs:h1540|H1540]];

===== Meaning =====

**captivity**; concretely, **exiles** (collectively)

===== Definitions =====

  - captivity
  - exiles

===== Usage =====

(they that are carried away) captives(-ity).
