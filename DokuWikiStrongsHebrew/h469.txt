====== H469: אֱלִיצָפָן (ʼĔlîytsâphân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

or (shortened) ; from H410 and H6845; God of treasure;

===== Meaning =====

**Elitsaphan** or **Eltsaphan**, an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - Elitsaphan
  - Eltsaphan

===== Usage =====

Elizaphan, Elzaphan.
