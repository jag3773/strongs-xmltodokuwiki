====== H2107: זוּל (zûwl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root (compare H2151);

===== Meaning =====

probably to **shake** out, i.e. (by implication) to **scatter** profusely; figuratively, to **treat lightly**

===== Definitions =====

  - shake
  - scatter
  - treat lightly

===== Usage =====

lavish, despise.
