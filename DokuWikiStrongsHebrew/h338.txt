====== H338: אִי (ʼîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

probably identical with H337 (through the idea of a **doleful** sound);

===== Meaning =====

a **howler** (used only in the plural), i.e. any solitary wild creature

===== Definitions =====

  - doleful
  - howler

===== Usage =====

wild beast of the islands.
