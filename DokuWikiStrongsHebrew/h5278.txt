====== H5278: נֹעַם (nôʻam) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h5276|H5276]];

===== Meaning =====

**agreeableness**, i.e. **delight**, **suitableness**, **splendor** or **grace**

===== Definitions =====

  - agreeableness
  - delight
  - suitableness
  - splendor
  - grace

===== Usage =====

beauty, pleasant(-ness).
