====== H5278: נֹעַם (nôʻam) ======

===== Source =====

from  H5276 ;

===== Meaning =====

agreeableness , i.e.  delight ,  suitableness ,  splendor  or  grace

===== Definitions =====

  - agreeableness
  - delight
  - suitableness
  - splendor
  - grace

===== Usage =====

beauty, pleasant(-ness).
