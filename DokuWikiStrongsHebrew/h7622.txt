====== H7622: שְׁבוּת (shᵉbûwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

or ; from H7617;

===== Meaning =====

**exile**, concretely, **prisoners**; figuratively, a **former state of prosperity**

===== Definitions =====

  - exile
  - prisoners
  - former state of prosperity

===== Usage =====

captive(-ity).
