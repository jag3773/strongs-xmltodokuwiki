====== H3244: יַנְשׁוּף (yanshûwph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; apparently from [[en:strongs:h5398|H5398]]; (compare [[en:strongs:h5399|H5399]])

===== Meaning =====

an **unclean** (acquatic) **bird**; probably the **heron** (perhaps from its blowing cry, or because the nightheron is meant)

===== Definitions =====

  - unclean
  - bird
  - heron

===== Usage =====

(great) owl.s
