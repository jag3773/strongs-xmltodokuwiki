====== H7835: שָׁחַר (shâchar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root (identical with H7836 through the idea of the duskiness of early dawn);

===== Meaning =====

to **be dim** or **dark** (in color)

===== Definitions =====

  - be dim
  - dark

===== Usage =====

be black.
