====== H113: אָדוֹן (ʼâdôwn) ======

===== Source =====

or (shortened)   ; from an unused root (meaning to  );

===== Meaning =====

rule sovereign , i.e.  controller  (human or divine)

===== Definitions =====

  - rule
  - sovereign
  - controller

===== Usage =====

lord, master, owner. Compare also names beginning with 'Adoni-'.
