====== H28: אֲבִידָע (ʼĂbîydâʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from H1 and H3045; **father of knowledge** (i.e. knowing);

===== Meaning =====

**Abida**, a son of Abraham by Keturah

===== Definitions =====

  - father of knowledge
  - Abida

===== Usage =====

Abida, Abidah.
