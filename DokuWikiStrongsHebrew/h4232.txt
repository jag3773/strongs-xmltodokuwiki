====== H4232: מְחוּיָאֵל (Mᵉchûwyâʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

or ; from H4229 and H410; smitten of God;

===== Meaning =====

**Mechujael** or **Mechijael**, an antediluvian patriarch

===== Definitions =====

  - Mechujael
  - Mechijael

===== Usage =====

Mehujael.
