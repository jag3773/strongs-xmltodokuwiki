====== H3026: יְגַר שַׂהֲדוּתָא (Yᵉgar Sahădûwthâʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from a word derived from an unused root (meaning to gather) and a derivation of a root corresponding to [[en:strongs:h7717|H7717]]; heap of the testimony;

===== Meaning =====

**Jegar-Sahadutha**, a cairn East of the Jordan

===== Definitions =====

  - Jegar-Sahadutha

===== Usage =====

Jegar-Sahadutha.
