====== H2914: טְחֹר (ṭᵉchôr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to burn;

===== Meaning =====

a **boil** or **ulcer** (from the inflammation), especially a **tumor** in the anus or pudenda (the piles)

===== Definitions =====

  - boil
  - ulcer
  - tumor

===== Usage =====

emerod.
