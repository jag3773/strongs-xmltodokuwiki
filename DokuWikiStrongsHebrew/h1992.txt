====== H1992: הֵם (hêm) ======

===== Source =====

or (prolonged)   ; masculine plural from  H1931 ;

===== Meaning =====

they  (only used when emphatic)

===== Definitions =====

  - they

===== Usage =====

it, like, × (how, so) many (soever, more as) they (be), (the) same, × so, × such, their, them, these, they, those, which, who, whom, withal, ye.
