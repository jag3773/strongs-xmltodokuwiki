====== H3225: יָמִין (yâmîyn) ======

===== Source =====

from  H3231 ;

===== Meaning =====

the  right  hand or side (leg, eye) of a person or other object (as the  stronger  and more dexterous); locally, the  south

===== Definitions =====

  - right
  - stronger
  - south

===== Usage =====

  left-handed, right (hand, side), south.
