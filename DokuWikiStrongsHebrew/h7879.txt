====== H7879: שִׂיחַ (sîyach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h7878|H7878]];

===== Meaning =====

a **contemplation**; by implication, an **utterance**

===== Definitions =====

  - contemplation
  - utterance

===== Usage =====

babbling, communication, complaint, meditation, prayer, talk.
