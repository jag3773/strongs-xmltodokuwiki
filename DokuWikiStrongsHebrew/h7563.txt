====== H7563: רָשָׁע (râshâʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

from H7561;

===== Meaning =====

morally **wrong**; concretely, an (actively) **bad** person

===== Definitions =====

  - wrong
  - bad

===== Usage =====

  condemned, guilty, ungodly, wicked (man), that did wrong.
