====== H7989: שַׁלִּיט (shallîyṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h7980|H7980]];

===== Meaning =====

**potent**; concretely, a **prince** or **warrior**

===== Definitions =====

  - potent
  - prince
  - warrior

===== Usage =====

governor, mighty, that hath power, ruler.
