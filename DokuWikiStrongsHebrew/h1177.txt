====== H1177: בַּעַל חָנָן (Baʻal Chânân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from H1167 and H2603; **possessor of grace**;

===== Meaning =====

**Baal-Chanan**, the name of an Edomite, also of an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - possessor of grace
  - Baal-Chanan

===== Usage =====

Baal-hanan.
