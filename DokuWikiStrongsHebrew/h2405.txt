====== H2405: חֲטֻבָה (chăṭubâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine passive participle of [[en:strongs:h2404|H2404]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **carving**; hence, a **tapestry** (as figured)

===== Definitions =====

  - carving
  - tapestry

===== Usage =====

carved.
