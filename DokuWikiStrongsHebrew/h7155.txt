====== H7155: קִרְיַת חֻצוֹת (Qiryath Chutsôwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from H7151 and the feminine plural of H2351; **city of streets**;

===== Meaning =====

**Kirjath-Chutsoth**, a place in Moab

===== Definitions =====

  - city of streets
  - Kirjath-Chutsoth

===== Usage =====

Kirjath-huzoth.
