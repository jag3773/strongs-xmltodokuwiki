====== H7219: רֹאשׁ (rôʼsh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; (Deuteronomy 32:32), apparently the same as [[en:strongs:h7218|H7218]];

===== Meaning =====

a **poisonous plant**, probably the **poppy** (from its conspicuous head); generally **poison** (even of serpents)

===== Definitions =====

  - poisonous plant
  - poppy
  - poison

===== Usage =====

gall, hemlock, poison, venom.
