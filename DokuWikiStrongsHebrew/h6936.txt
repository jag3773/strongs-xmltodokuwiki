====== H6936: קׇדְקֹד (qodqôd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h6915|H6915]];

===== Meaning =====

the **crown** of the head (as the part most **bowed**)

===== Definitions =====

  - crown
  - bowed

===== Usage =====

crown (of the head), pate, scalp, top of the head.
