====== H4132: מוֹט (môwṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h4131|H4131]];

===== Meaning =====

a **wavering**, i.e. **fall**; by implication, a **pole** (as shaking); hence, a **yoke** (as essentially a bent pole)

===== Definitions =====

  - wavering
  - fall
  - pole
  - yoke

===== Usage =====

bar, be moved, staff, yoke.
