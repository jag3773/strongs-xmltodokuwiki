====== H152: אֲדְרַמֶּלֶךְ (ʼĂdrammelek) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from H142 and H4428; **splendor of** (the) **king**;

===== Meaning =====

**Adrammelek**, the name of an Assyrian idol, also of a son of Sennacherib

===== Definitions =====

  - splendor of
  - king
  - Adrammelek

===== Usage =====

Adrammelech.
