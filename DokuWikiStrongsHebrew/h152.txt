====== H152: אֲדְרַמֶּלֶךְ (ʼĂdrammelek) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h142|H142]] and [[en:strongs:h4428|H4428]]; **splendor of** (the) **king**;

===== Meaning =====

**Adrammelek**, the name of an Assyrian idol, also of a son of Sennacherib

===== Definitions =====

  - splendor of
  - king
  - Adrammelek

===== Usage =====

Adrammelech.
