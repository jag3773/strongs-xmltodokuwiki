====== H5954: עֲלַל (ʻălal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to H5953 (in the sense of thrusting oneself in),

===== Meaning =====

to **enter**; causatively, to **introduce**

===== Definitions =====

  - enter
  - introduce

===== Usage =====

bring in, come in, go in.
