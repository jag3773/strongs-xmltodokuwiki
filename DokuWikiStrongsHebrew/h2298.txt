====== H2298: חַד (chad) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to [[en:strongs:h2297|H2297]];

===== Meaning =====

as card. **one**; as article **single**; as an ordinal, **first**; adverbially, **at once**

===== Definitions =====

  - one
  - single
  - first
  - at once

===== Usage =====

a, first, one, together.
