====== H4768: מַרְבִּית (marbîyth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from H7235;

===== Meaning =====

a **multitude**; also **offspring**; specifically **interest** (on capital)

===== Definitions =====

  - multitude
  - offspring
  - interest

===== Usage =====

greatest part, greatness, increase, multitude.
