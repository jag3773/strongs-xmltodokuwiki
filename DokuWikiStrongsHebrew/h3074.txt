====== H3074: יְהֹוָה שָׁמָּה (Yᵉhôvâh shâmmâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from H3068 and H8033 with directive enclitic; Jehovah (is) thither;

===== Meaning =====

**Jehovah-Shammah**, a symbolic title of Jerusalem

===== Definitions =====

  - Jehovah-Shammah

===== Usage =====

Jehovahshammah.
