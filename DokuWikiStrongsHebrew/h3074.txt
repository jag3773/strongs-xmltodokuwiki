====== H3074: יְהֹוָה שָׁמָּה (Yᵉhôvâh shâmmâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - locative

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h3068|H3068]] and [[en:strongs:h8033|H8033]] with directive enclitic; Jehovah (is) thither;

===== Meaning =====

**Jehovah-Shammah**, a symbolic title of Jerusalem
