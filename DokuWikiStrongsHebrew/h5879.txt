====== H5879: עֵינַיִם (ʻÊynayim) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

or ; dual of H5869; **double fountain**;

===== Meaning =====

**Enajim** or **Enam**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - double fountain
  - Enajim
  - Enam

===== Usage =====

Enaim, openly (Genesis 38:21).
