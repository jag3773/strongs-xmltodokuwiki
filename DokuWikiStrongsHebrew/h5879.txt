====== H5879: עֵינַיִם (ʻÊynayim) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - locative

===== Source =====

===== Meaning =====

**Enajim** or **Enam**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - double fountain
  - Enajim
  - Enam

===== Usage =====

Enaim, openly (Genesis 38:21).
