====== H319: אַחֲרִית (ʼachărîyth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from H310;

===== Meaning =====

the **last** or **end**, hence, the **future**; also **posterity**

===== Definitions =====

  - last
  - end
  - future
  - posterity

===== Usage =====

(last, latter) end (time), hinder (utter) -most, length, posterity, remnant, residue, reward.
