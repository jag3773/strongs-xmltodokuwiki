====== H2900: טוֹבִיָּה (Ṭôwbîyâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

or ; from H2896 and H3050; goodness of Jehovah;

===== Meaning =====

**Tobijah**, the name of three Israelites and of one Samaritan

===== Definitions =====

  - Tobijah

===== Usage =====

Tobiah, Tobijah.
