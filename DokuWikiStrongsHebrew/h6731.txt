====== H6731: צִיץ (tsîyts) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; from [[en:strongs:h6692|H6692]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **glistening**, i.e. a burnished **plate**; also a **flower** (as **bright** colored); a **wing** (as **gleaming** in the air)

===== Definitions =====

  - glistening
  - plate
  - flower
  - bright
  - wing
  - gleaming

===== Usage =====

blossom, flower, plate, wing.
