====== H3984: מָאן (mâʼn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) probably from a root corresponding to [[en:strongs:h579|H579]] in the sense of an inclosure by sides;

===== Meaning =====

a **utensil**

===== Definitions =====

  - utensil

===== Usage =====

vessel.
