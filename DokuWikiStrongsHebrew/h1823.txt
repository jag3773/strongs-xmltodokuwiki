====== H1823: דְּמוּת (dᵉmûwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h1819|H1819]];

===== Meaning =====

**resemblance**; concretely, **model**, **shape**; adverbially, **like**

===== Definitions =====

  - resemblance
  - model
  - shape
  - like

===== Usage =====

fashion, like (-ness, as), manner, similitude.
