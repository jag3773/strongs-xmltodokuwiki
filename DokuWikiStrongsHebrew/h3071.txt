====== H3071: יְהֹוָה נִסִּי (Yᵉhôvâh niççîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h3068|H3068]] and [[en:strongs:h5251|H5251]] with the prononimal suffix; Jehovah (is) my banner;

===== Meaning =====

**Jehovah-Nissi**, a symbolical name of an altar in the Desert

===== Definitions =====

  - Jehovah-Nissi

===== Usage =====

Jehovah-nissi.
