====== H5207: נִיחוֹחַ (nîychôwach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or ; from H5117;

===== Meaning =====

properly, **restful**, i.e. **pleasant**; abstractly, **delight**

===== Definitions =====

  - restful
  - pleasant
  - delight

===== Usage =====

sweet (odour).
