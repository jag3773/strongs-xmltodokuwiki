====== H1964: הֵיכָל (hêykâl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

probably from H3201 (in the sense of **capacity**);

===== Meaning =====

a large public building, such as a **palace** or **temple**

===== Definitions =====

  - capacity
  - palace
  - temple

===== Usage =====

palace, temple.
