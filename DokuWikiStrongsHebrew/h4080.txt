====== H4080: מִדְיָן (Midyân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

the same as H4079;

===== Meaning =====

**Midjan**, a son of Abraham; also his country and (collectively) his descendants

===== Definitions =====

  - Midjan

===== Usage =====

Midian, Midianite.
