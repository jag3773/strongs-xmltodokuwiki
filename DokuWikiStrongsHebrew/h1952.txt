====== H1952: הוֹן (hôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from the same as H1951 in the sense of H202;

===== Meaning =====

**wealth**; by implication, **enough**

===== Definitions =====

  - wealth
  - enough

===== Usage =====

enough,   for nought, riches, substance, wealth.
