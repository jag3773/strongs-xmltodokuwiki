====== H1614: גׇּפְרִית (gophrîyth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

probably feminine of H1613;

===== Meaning =====

properly, **cypress-resin**; by analogy, **sulphur** (as equally inflammable)

===== Definitions =====

  - cypress-resin
  - sulphur

===== Usage =====

brimstone.
