====== H7884: שִׁיחוֹר לִבְנָת (Shîychôwr Libnâth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from the same as H7883 and H3835; **darkish whiteness**;

===== Meaning =====

**Shichor-Libnath**, a stream of Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - darkish whiteness
  - Shichor-Libnath

===== Usage =====

Shihor-libnath.
