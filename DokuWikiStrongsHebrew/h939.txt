====== H939: בּוּזָה (bûwzâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine passive participle of [[en:strongs:h936|H936]];

===== Meaning =====

something **scorned**; an object of **contempt**

===== Definitions =====

  - scorned
  - contempt

===== Usage =====

despised.
