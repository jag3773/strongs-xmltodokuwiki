====== H2560: חָמַר (châmar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root; also	as denominative (from [[en:strongs:h2564|H2564]])

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **boil** up; hence, to **ferment** (with scum); to **glow** (with redness); to **smear** with pitch

===== Definitions =====

  - boil
  - ferment
  - glow
  - smear

===== Usage =====

daub, befoul, be red, trouble.
