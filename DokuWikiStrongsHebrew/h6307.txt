====== H6307: פַּדָּן (Paddân) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - locative

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to extend; a plateau; or [[en:strongs:|]]; from the same and [[en:strongs:h758|H758]]; the table-land of Aram;

===== Meaning =====

**Paddan** or **Paddan-Aram**, a region of Syria

===== Definitions =====

  - Paddan
  - Paddan-Aram

===== Usage =====

Padan, Padan-aram.
