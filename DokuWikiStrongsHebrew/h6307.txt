====== H6307: פַּדָּן (Paddân) ======

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to extend; a plateau; or   ; from the same and  H758 ; the table-land of Aram;

===== Meaning =====

Paddan  or  Paddan-Aram , a region of Syria

===== Definitions =====

  - Paddan
  - Paddan-Aram

===== Usage =====

Padan, Padan-aram.
