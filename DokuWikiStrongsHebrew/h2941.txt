====== H2941: טַעַם (ṭaʻam) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from H2939; compare H2940)

===== Meaning =====

properly, **a taste**, i.e.**	a judicial sentence**

===== Definitions =====

  - a taste
  - 	a judicial sentence

===== Usage =====

account, × to be commanded, commandment, matter.
