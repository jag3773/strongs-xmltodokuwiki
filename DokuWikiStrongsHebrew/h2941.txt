====== H2941: טַעַם (ṭaʻam) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from [[en:strongs:h2939|H2939]]; compare [[en:strongs:h2940|H2940]])

===== Meaning =====

properly, **a taste**, i.e.**	a judicial sentence**

===== Definitions =====

  - a taste
  - 	a judicial sentence

===== Usage =====

account, × to be commanded, commandment, matter.
