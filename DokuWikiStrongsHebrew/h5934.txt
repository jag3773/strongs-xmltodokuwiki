====== H5934: עָלוּם (ʻâlûwm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

passive participle of H5956 in the denominative sense of H5958;

===== Meaning =====

(only in plural as abstract) **adolescence**; figuratively, **vigor**

===== Definitions =====

  - adolescence
  - vigor

===== Usage =====

youth.
