====== H579: אָנָה (ʼânâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root (perhaps rather identical with H578 through the idea of **contraction** in anguish);

===== Meaning =====

to **approach**; hence, to **meet** in various senses

===== Definitions =====

  - contraction
  - approach
  - meet

===== Usage =====

befall, deliver, happen, seek a quarrel.
