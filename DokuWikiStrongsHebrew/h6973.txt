====== H6973: קוּץ (qûwts) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root (identical with through the idea of severing oneself from (compare H6962));

===== Meaning =====

to **be** (causatively, **make**) **disgusted** or **anxious**

===== Definitions =====

  - be
  - make
  - disgusted
  - anxious

===== Usage =====

abhor, be distressed, be grieved, loathe, vex, be weary.
