====== H2715: חֹר (chôr) ======

===== Source =====

or (fully)   ; from  H2787 ; (compare  H2751 )

===== Meaning =====

properly,  white  or  pure  (from the  cleansing  or  shining  power of fire); hence (figuratively)  noble  (in rank)

===== Definitions =====

  - white
  - pure
  - cleansing
  - shining
  - noble

===== Usage =====

noble.
