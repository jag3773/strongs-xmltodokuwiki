====== H4772: מַרְגְלָה (margᵉlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

denominative from H7272;

===== Meaning =====

(plural for collective) a **footpiece**, i.e. (adverbially) **at the foot**, or (direct.) the **foot** itself

===== Definitions =====

  - footpiece
  - at the foot
  - foot

===== Usage =====

feet. Compare
