====== H4820: מִרְמָה (mirmâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from H7411 in the sense of **deceiving**;

===== Meaning =====

**fraud**

===== Definitions =====

  - deceiving
  - fraud

===== Usage =====

craft, deceit(-ful, -fully), false, feigned, guile, subtilly, treachery.
