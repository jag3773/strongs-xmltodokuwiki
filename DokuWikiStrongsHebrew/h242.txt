====== H242: אֻזֵּן שֶׁאֱרָה (ʼUzzên Sheʼĕrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from H238 and H7609; plot of Sheerah (i.e. settled by him);

===== Meaning =====

**Uzzen-Sheerah**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Uzzen-Sheerah

===== Usage =====

Uzzen-sherah.
