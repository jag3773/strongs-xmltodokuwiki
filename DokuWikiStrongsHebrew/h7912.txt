====== H7912: שְׁכַח (shᵉkach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to H7911 through the idea of disclosure of a covered or forgotten thing;

===== Meaning =====

to **discover** (literally or figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - discover

===== Usage =====

find.
