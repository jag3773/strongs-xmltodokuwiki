====== H7990: שַׁלִּיט (shallîyṭ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to H7989;

===== Meaning =====

**mighty**; abstractly, **permission**; concretely, a **premier**

===== Definitions =====

  - mighty
  - permission
  - premier

===== Usage =====

captain, be lawful, rule(-r).
