===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; from [[en:strongs:h7686|H7686]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **aberration**, i.e. (technically) a **dithyramb** or **rambling poem**

===== Definitions =====

  - aberration

===== Usage =====
