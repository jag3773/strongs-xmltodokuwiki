====== H5980: עֻמָּה (ʻummâh) ======

===== Source =====

from  H6004 ;

===== Meaning =====

conjunction , i.e.  society ; mostly adverb or preposition (with prepositional prefix),  near ,  beside ,  along with

===== Definitions =====

  - conjunction
  - society
  - near
  - beside
  - along with

===== Usage =====

(over) against, at, beside, hard by, in points.
