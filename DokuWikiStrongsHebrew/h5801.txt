====== H5801: עִזָּבוֹן (ʻizzâbôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H5800 in the sense of letting go (for a price, i.e. selling);

===== Meaning =====

**trade**, i.e. the **place** (mart) or the **payment** (revenue)

===== Definitions =====

  - trade
  - place
  - payment

===== Usage =====

fair, ware.
