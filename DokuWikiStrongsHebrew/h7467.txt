====== H7467: רְעוּאֵל (Rᵉʻûwʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from the same as H7466 and H410; friend of God;

===== Meaning =====

**Reuel**, the name of Moses' father-in-law, also of an Edomite and an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - Reuel

===== Usage =====

Raguel, Reuel.
