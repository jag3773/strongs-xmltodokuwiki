====== H14: אָבָה (ʼâbâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **breathe** after, i.e. (figuratively) to **be acquiescent**

===== Definitions =====

  - breathe
  - be acquiescent

===== Usage =====

consent, rest content will, be willing.
