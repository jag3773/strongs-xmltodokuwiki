====== H39: אֲבִימָאֵל (ʼĂbîymâʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from H1 and an elsewhere unused (probably foreign) word; father of Mael (apparently some Arab tribe);

===== Meaning =====

**Abimael**, a son of Joktan

===== Definitions =====

  - Abimael

===== Usage =====

Abimael.
