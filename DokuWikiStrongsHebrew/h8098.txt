====== H8098: שְׁמַעְיָה (Shᵉmaʻyâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

or ; from H8085 and H3050; **Jah has heard**;

===== Meaning =====

**Shemajah**, the name of twenty-five Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - Jah has heard
  - Shemajah

===== Usage =====

Shemaiah.
