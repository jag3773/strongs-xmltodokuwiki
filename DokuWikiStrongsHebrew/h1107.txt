====== H1107: בִּלְעֲדֵי (bilʻădêy) ======

===== Source =====

or   ; constructive plural from  H1077  and  H5703 , not till, i.e. (as preposition or adverb)

===== Meaning =====

except ,  without ,  besides

===== Definitions =====

  - except
  - without
  - besides

===== Usage =====

beside, not (in), save, without.
