====== H1412: גֻּדְגֹּדָה (Gudgôdâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - locative

===== Source =====

by reduplication from [[en:strongs:h1413|H1413]] (in the sense of cutting) **cleft**;

===== Meaning =====

**Gudgodah**, a place in the Desert

===== Definitions =====

  - cleft
  - Gudgodah

===== Usage =====

Gudgodah.
