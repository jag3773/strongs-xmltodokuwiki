====== H1025: בֵּית הָעֵמֶק (Bêyth hâ-ʻÊmeq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from H1004 and H6010 with the article interposed; house of the valley;

===== Meaning =====

**Beth-ha-Emek**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Beth-ha-Emek

===== Usage =====

Beth-emek.
