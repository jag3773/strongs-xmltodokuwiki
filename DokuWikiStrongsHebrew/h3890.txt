====== H3890: לְוָת (lᵉvâth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

prep

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) from a root corresponding to [[en:strongs:h3867|H3867]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **adhesion**, i.e. (as preposition) **with**

===== Definitions =====

  - adhesion
  - with

===== Usage =====

× thee.
