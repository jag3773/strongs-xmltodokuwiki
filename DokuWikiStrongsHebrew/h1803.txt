====== H1803: דַּלָּה (dallâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from H1802;

===== Meaning =====

properly, something **dangling**, i.e. a **loose thread** or **hair**; figuratively, **indigent**

===== Definitions =====

  - dangling
  - loose thread
  - hair
  - indigent

===== Usage =====

hair, pining sickness, poor(-est sort).
