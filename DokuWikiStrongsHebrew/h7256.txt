====== H7256: רִבֵּעַ (ribbêaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h7251|H7251]];

===== Meaning =====

a descendant of the **fourth** generation, i.e. **great great grandchild**

===== Definitions =====

  - fourth
  - great great grandchild

===== Usage =====

fourth.
