====== H3096: יַהַץ (Yahats) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - locative

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; or (feminine) [[en:strongs:|]]; from an unused root meaning to **stamp**; perhaps **threshing**-floor;

===== Meaning =====

**Jahats** or **Jahtsah**, a place East of the Jordan

===== Definitions =====

  - stamp
  - threshing
  - Jahats
  - Jahtsah

===== Usage =====

Jahaz, Jahazah, Jahzah.
