====== H6725: צִיּוּן (tsîyûwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from the same as H6723 in the sense of conspicuousness (compare H5329);

===== Meaning =====

a **monumental** or **guiding pillar**

===== Definitions =====

  - monumental
  - guiding pillar

===== Usage =====

sign, title, waymark.
