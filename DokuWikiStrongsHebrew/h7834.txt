====== H7834: שַׁחַק (shachaq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h7833|H7833]];

===== Meaning =====

a **powder** (as **beaten** small); by analogy, a thin **vapor**; by extension, the **firmament**

===== Definitions =====

  - powder
  - beaten
  - vapor
  - firmament

===== Usage =====

cloud, small dust, heaven, sky.
