====== H2436: חֵיק (chêyq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or ; and ; from an unused root, apparently meaning to inclose;

===== Meaning =====

the **bosom** (literally or figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - bosom

===== Usage =====

bosom, bottom, lap, midst, within.
