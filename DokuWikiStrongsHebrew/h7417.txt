====== H7417: רִמּוֹן (Rimmôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

or (shorter) (; or ; (1 Chronicles 6:62 (H77)), the same as H7416; in Joshua 19:13:  is mistaken for part of a name; article with the passive participle of H8388; the (one) marked off, i.e. which pertains).

===== Meaning =====

**Rimmon**, the name of a Syrian deity, also of five places in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Rimmon

===== Usage =====

Remmon, Rimmon.
