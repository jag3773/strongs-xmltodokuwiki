====== H1285: בְּרִית (bᵉrîyth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h1262|H1262]] (in the sense of cutting [like [[en:strongs:h1254|H1254]]]);

===== Meaning =====

a **compact** (because made by passing between pieces of flesh)

===== Definitions =====

  - compact

===== Usage =====

confederacy, (con-) feder(-ate), covenant, league.
