====== H5275: נַעַל (naʻal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

or (feminine) ; from H5274;

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **sandal tongue**; by extension a **sandal** or **slipper** (sometimes as a symbol of occupancy, a refusal to marry, or of something valueless)

===== Definitions =====

  - sandal tongue
  - sandal
  - slipper

===== Usage =====

dryshod, (pair of) shoe((-latchet), -s).
