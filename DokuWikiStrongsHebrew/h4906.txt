====== H4906: מַשְׂכִּית (maskîyth) ======

===== Source =====

from the same as  H7906 ;

===== Meaning =====

a  figure  (carved on stone, the wall, or any object); figuratively,  imagination

===== Definitions =====

  - figure
  - imagination

===== Usage =====

conceit, image(-ry), picture, × wish.
