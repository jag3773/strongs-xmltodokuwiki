====== H1281: בָּרִיחַ (bârîyach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or ; (shortened) from H1272;

===== Meaning =====

a **fugitive**, i.e. the **serpent** (as **fleeing**), and the constellation by that name

===== Definitions =====

  - fugitive
  - serpent
  - fleeing

===== Usage =====

crooked, noble, piercing.
