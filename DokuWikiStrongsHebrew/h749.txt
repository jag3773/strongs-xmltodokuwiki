====== H749: אֲרַךְ (ʼărak) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) properly, corresponding to [[en:strongs:h748|H748]], but used only in the sense of **reaching** to a given point;

===== Meaning =====

to **suit**

===== Definitions =====

  - reaching
  - suit

===== Usage =====

be meet.
