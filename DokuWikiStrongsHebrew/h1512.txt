====== H1512: גָּחוֹן (gâchôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

probably from [[en:strongs:h1518|H1518]]; (compare [[en:strongs:h1521|H1521]])

===== Meaning =====

the **external abdomen**, **belly** (as the source of the faetus)

===== Definitions =====

  - external abdomen
  - belly

===== Usage =====

belly.
