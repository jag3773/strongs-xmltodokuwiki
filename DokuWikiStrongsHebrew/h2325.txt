====== H2325: חוּב (chûwb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

also [[en:strongs:|]]; a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, perhaps to **tie**, i.e. (figuratively and reflexively) to **owe**, or (by implication) to **forfeit**

===== Definitions =====

  - tie
  - owe
  - forfeit

===== Usage =====

make endanger.
