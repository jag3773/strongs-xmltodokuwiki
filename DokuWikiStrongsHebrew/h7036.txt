====== H7036: קָלוֹן (qâlôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h7034|H7034]];

===== Meaning =====

**disgrace**; (by implication) the **pudenda**

===== Definitions =====

  - disgrace
  - pudenda

===== Usage =====

confusion, dishonour, ignominy, reproach, shame.
