====== H3371: יׇקְתְאֵל (Yoqthᵉʼêl) ======

===== Source =====

probably from the same as  H3348  and  H410 ; veneration of God (compare  H3354 );

===== Meaning =====

Joktheel , the name of a place in Palestine, and of one in Idumaea

===== Definitions =====

  - Joktheel

===== Usage =====

Joktheel.
