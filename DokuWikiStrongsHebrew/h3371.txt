====== H3371: יׇקְתְאֵל (Yoqthᵉʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

probably from the same as [[en:strongs:h3348|H3348]] and [[en:strongs:h410|H410]]; veneration of God (compare [[en:strongs:h3354|H3354]]);

===== Meaning =====

**Joktheel**, the name of a place in Palestine, and of one in Idumaea

===== Definitions =====

  - Joktheel

===== Usage =====

Joktheel.
