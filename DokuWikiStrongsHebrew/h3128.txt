====== H3128: יוֹנַת אֵלֶם רְחֹקִים (yôwnath ʼêlem rᵉchôqîym) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n

===== Source =====

from H3123 and H482 and the plural of H7350;

===== Meaning =====

**dove of** (the) **silence** (i.e. dumb Israel) **of** (i.e. among) **distances** (i.e. strangers); the title of a ditty (used for a name of its melody)

===== Definitions =====

  - dove of
  - silence
  - of
  - distances

===== Usage =====

Jonath-elem-rechokim.
