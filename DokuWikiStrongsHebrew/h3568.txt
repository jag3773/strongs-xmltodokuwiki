====== H3568: כּוּשׁ (Kûwsh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

probably of foreign origin;

===== Meaning =====

**Cush** (or Ethiopia), the name of a son of Ham, and of his territory; also of an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - Cush

===== Usage =====

Chush, Cush, Ethiopia.
