====== H1323: בַּת (bath) ======

===== Source =====

from  H1129  (as feminine of  H1121 );

===== Meaning =====

a  daughter  (used in the same wide sense as other terms of relationship, literally and figuratively)

===== Definitions =====

  - daughter

===== Usage =====

apple (of the eye), branch, company, daughter, × first, × old,   owl, town, village.
