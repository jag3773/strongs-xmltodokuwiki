====== H3960: לָשַׁן (lâshan) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root; properly, to lick; but used only as a denominative from H3956;

===== Meaning =====

to **wag the tongue**, i.e. to **calumniate**

===== Definitions =====

  - wag the tongue
  - calumniate

===== Usage =====

accuse, slander.
