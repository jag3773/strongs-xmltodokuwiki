====== H3915: לַיִל (layil) ======

===== Source =====

or (Isaiah 21:11)   ; also   ; from the same as  H3883 ;

===== Meaning =====

properly, a  twist  (away of the light), i.e.  night ; figuratively,  adversity

===== Definitions =====

  - twist
  - night
  - adversity

===== Usage =====

(mid-)night (season).
