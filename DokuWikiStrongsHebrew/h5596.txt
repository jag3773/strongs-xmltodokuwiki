====== H5596: סָפַח (çâphach) ======

===== Source =====

or   ; (Isaiah 3:17), a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to  scrape  out, but in certain peculiar senses (of  removal  or  association )

===== Definitions =====

  - scrape
  - removal
  - association

===== Usage =====

abiding, gather together, cleave, smite with the scab.
