====== H6890: צֶרֶת הַשַּׁחַר (Tsereth hash-Shachar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from the same as H6889 and H7837 with the article interposed; **splendor of the dawn**;

===== Meaning =====

**Tsereth-hash-Shachar**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - splendor of the dawn
  - Tsereth-hash-Shachar

===== Usage =====

Zareth-shahar.
