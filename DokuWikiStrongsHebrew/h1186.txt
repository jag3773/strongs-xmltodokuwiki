====== H1186: בַּעַל מְעוֹן (Baʻal Mᵉʻôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from H1168 and H4583; **Baal of** (the) **habitation** (of) (compare H1010);

===== Meaning =====

**Baal-Meon**, a place East of the Jordan

===== Definitions =====

  - Baal of
  - habitation
  - Baal-Meon

===== Usage =====

Baal-meon.
