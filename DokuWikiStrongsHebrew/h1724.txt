====== H1724: דָּהַם (dâham) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root (compare [[en:strongs:h1740|H1740]]);

===== Meaning =====

to **be dumb**, i.e. (figuratively) **dumbfounded**

===== Definitions =====

  - be dumb
  - dumbfounded

===== Usage =====

astonished.
