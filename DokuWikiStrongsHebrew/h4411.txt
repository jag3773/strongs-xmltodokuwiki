====== H4411: מָלוֹן (mâlôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H3885;

===== Meaning =====

a **lodgment**, i.e. **caravanserai** or **encampment**

===== Definitions =====

  - lodgment
  - caravanserai
  - encampment

===== Usage =====

inn, place where...lodge, lodging (place).
