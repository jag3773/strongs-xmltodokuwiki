====== H2331: חָוָה (châvâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root; (compare H2324, H2421);

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **live**; by implication (intensively) to **declare** or **show**

===== Definitions =====

  - live
  - declare
  - show

===== Usage =====

show.
