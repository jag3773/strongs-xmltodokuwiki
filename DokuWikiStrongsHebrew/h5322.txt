====== H5322: נֵץ (nêts) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H5340;

===== Meaning =====

a **flower** (from its **brilliancy**); also a **hawk** (from it **flashing** speed)

===== Definitions =====

  - flower
  - brilliancy
  - hawk
  - flashing

===== Usage =====

blossom, hawk.
