====== H1166: בָּעַל (bâʻal) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root; also as denominative from [[en:strongs:h1167|H1167]]

===== Meaning =====

to **be master**; hence, to **marry**

===== Definitions =====

  - be master
  - marry

===== Usage =====

have dominion (over), be husband, marry(-ried, × wife).
