====== H8047: שַׁמָּה (shammâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h8074|H8074]];

===== Meaning =====

**ruin**; by implication, **consternation**

===== Definitions =====

  - ruin
  - consternation

===== Usage =====

astonishment, desolate(-ion), waste, wonderful thing.
