====== H2654: חָפֵץ (châphêts) ======

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to  incline  to; by implication (literally but rarely) to  bend ; figuratively, to  be pleased  with,  desire

===== Definitions =====

  - incline
  - bend
  - be pleased
  - desire

===== Usage =====

× any at all, (have, take) delight, desire, favour, like, move, be (well) pleased, have pleasure, will, would.
