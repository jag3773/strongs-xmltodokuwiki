====== H268: אָחוֹר (ʼâchôwr) ======

===== Source =====

or (shortened)    ; from  H299 ;

===== Meaning =====

the  hinder  part; hence (adverb)  behind ,  backward ; also (as facing north) the  West

===== Definitions =====

  - hinder
  - behind
  - backward
  - West

===== Usage =====

after(-ward), back (part, -side, -ward), hereafter, (be-) hind(-er part), time to come, without.
