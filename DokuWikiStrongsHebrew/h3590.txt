====== H3590: כִידוֹד (kîydôwd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:strongs:h3589|H3589]] (compare [[en:strongs:h3539|H3539]]);

===== Meaning =====

properly, something **struck off**, i.e. a **spark** (as struck)

===== Definitions =====

  - struck off
  - spark

===== Usage =====

spark.
