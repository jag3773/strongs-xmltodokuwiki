====== H8110: שִׁמְרוֹן (Shimrôwn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from H8105 in its original sense; **guardianship**;

===== Meaning =====

**Shimron**, the name of an Israelite and of a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - guardianship
  - Shimron

===== Usage =====

Shimron.
