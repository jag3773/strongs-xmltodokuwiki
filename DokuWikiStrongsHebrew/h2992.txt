====== H2992: יָבַם (yâbam) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root of doubtful meaning; used only as a denominative from H2993;

===== Meaning =====

to **marry** a (deceased) brother's widow

===== Definitions =====

  - marry

===== Usage =====

perform the duty of a husband's brother, marry.
