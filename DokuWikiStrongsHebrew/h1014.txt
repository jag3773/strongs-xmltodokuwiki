====== H1014: בֵּית גָּמוּל (Bêyth Gâmûwl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from H1004 and the passive participle of H1576; house of (the) weaned;

===== Meaning =====

**Beth-Gamul**, a place East of the Jordan

===== Definitions =====

  - Beth-Gamul

===== Usage =====

Beth-gamul.
