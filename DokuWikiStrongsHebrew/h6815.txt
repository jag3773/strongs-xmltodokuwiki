====== H6815: צַעֲנַנִּים (Tsaʻănannîym) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

or (dual) ; plural from H6813; **removals**;

===== Meaning =====

**Tsaanannim** or **Tsaanajim**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - removals
  - Tsaanannim
  - Tsaanajim

===== Usage =====

Zaannannim, Zaanaim.
