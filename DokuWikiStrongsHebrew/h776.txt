====== H776: אֶרֶץ (ʼerets) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from an unused root probably meaning to **be firm**;

===== Meaning =====

the **earth** (at large, or partitively a **land**)

===== Definitions =====

  - be firm
  - earth
  - land

===== Usage =====

× common, country, earth, field, ground, land, × natins, way, + wilderness, world.
