====== H4967: מְתוּשָׁאֵל (Mᵉthûwshâʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from H4962 and H410, with the relative interposed; man who (is) of God;

===== Meaning =====

**Methusael**, an antediluvian patriarch

===== Definitions =====

  - Methusael

===== Usage =====

Methusael.
