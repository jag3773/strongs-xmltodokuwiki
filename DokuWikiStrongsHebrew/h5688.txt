====== H5688: עֲבֹת (ʻăbôth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; or (feminine) [[en:strongs:|]]; the same as [[en:strongs:h5687|H5687]];

===== Meaning =====

something **intwined**, i.e. a **string**, **wreath** or **foliage**

===== Definitions =====

  - intwined
  - string
  - wreath
  - foliage

===== Usage =====

band, cord, rope, thick bough (branch), wreathen (chain).
