====== H5077: נָדָה (nâdâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; (2 Kings 17:21), a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **toss**; figuratively, to **exclude**, i.e. **banish**, **postpone**, **prohibit**

===== Definitions =====

  - toss
  - exclude
  - banish
  - postpone
  - prohibit

===== Usage =====

cast out, drive, put far away.
