====== H7643: שְׂבָם (Sᵉbâm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

or (feminine) ; probably from H1313; **spice**;

===== Meaning =====

**Sebam** or **Sibmah**, a place in Moab

===== Definitions =====

  - spice
  - Sebam
  - Sibmah

===== Usage =====

Shebam, Shibmah, Sibmah.
