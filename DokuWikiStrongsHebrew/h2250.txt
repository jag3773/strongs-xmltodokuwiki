====== H2250: חַבּוּרָה (chabbûwrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; or [[en:strongs:|]]; from [[en:strongs:h2266|H2266]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **bound** (with stripes), i.e. a **weal** (or black-and-blue mark itself)

===== Definitions =====

  - bound
  - weal

===== Usage =====

blueness, bruise, hurt, stripe, wound.
