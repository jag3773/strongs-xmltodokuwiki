====== H8622: תְּקוּפָה (tᵉqûwphâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

or ; from H5362;

===== Meaning =====

a **revolution**, i.e. (of the sun) **course**, (of time) **lapse**

===== Definitions =====

  - revolution
  - course
  - lapse

===== Usage =====

circuit, come about, end.
