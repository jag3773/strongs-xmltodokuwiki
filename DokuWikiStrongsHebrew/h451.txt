====== H451: אַלְיָה (ʼalyâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h422|H422]] (in the original sense of **strength**);

===== Meaning =====

the **stout** part, i.e. the fat **tail** of the Oriental sheep

===== Definitions =====

  - strength
  - stout
  - tail

===== Usage =====

rump.
