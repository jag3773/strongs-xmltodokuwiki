====== H6041: עָנִי (ʻânîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

from H6031; (practically the same as H6035, although the margin constantly disputes this, making H6035 subjective and objective);

===== Meaning =====

**depressed**, in mind or circumstances

===== Definitions =====

  - depressed

===== Usage =====

afflicted, humble, lowly, needy, poor.
