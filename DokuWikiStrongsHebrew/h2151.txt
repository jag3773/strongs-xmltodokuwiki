====== H2151: זָלַל (zâlal) ======

===== Source =====

a primitive root (compare  H2107 );

===== Meaning =====

to  shake  (as in the wind), i.e. to  quake ; figuratively, to  be loose  morally,  worthless  or  prodigal

===== Definitions =====

  - shake
  - quake
  - be loose
  - worthless
  - prodigal

===== Usage =====

blow down, glutton, riotous (eater), vile.
