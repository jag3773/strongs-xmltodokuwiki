====== H6233: עֹשֶׁק (ʻôsheq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h6231|H6231]];

===== Meaning =====

**injury**, **fraud**, (subjectively) **distress**, (concretely) **unjust gain**

===== Definitions =====

  - injury
  - fraud
  - distress
  - unjust gain

===== Usage =====

cruelly, extortion, oppression, thing (deceitfully gotten).
