====== H3366: יְקָר (yᵉqâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h3365|H3365]];

===== Meaning =====

**value**, i.e. (concretely) **wealth**; abstractly, **costliness**, **dignity**

===== Definitions =====

  - value
  - wealth
  - costliness
  - dignity

===== Usage =====

honour, precious (things), price.
