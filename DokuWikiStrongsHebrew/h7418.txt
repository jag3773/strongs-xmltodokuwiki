====== H7418: רָמוֹת (Râmôwth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - locative

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; from the plural or construct form of [[en:strongs:h7413|H7413]] and [[en:strongs:h5045|H5045]]; heights (or height) of the South;

===== Meaning =====

**Ramoth-Negeb** or **Ramath-Negeb**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Ramoth-Negeb
  - Ramath-Negeb

===== Usage =====

south Ramoth, Ramath of the south.
