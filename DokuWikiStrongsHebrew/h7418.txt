====== H7418: רָמוֹת (Râmôwth) ======

===== Source =====

or   ; from the plural or construct form of  H7413  and  H5045 ; heights (or height) of the South;

===== Meaning =====

Ramoth-Negeb  or  Ramath-Negeb , a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - Ramoth-Negeb
  - Ramath-Negeb

===== Usage =====

south Ramoth, Ramath of the south.
