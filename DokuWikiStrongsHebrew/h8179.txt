====== H8179: שַׁעַר (shaʻar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h8176|H8176]] in its original sense;

===== Meaning =====

an **opening**, i.e. **door** or **gate**

===== Definitions =====

  - opening
  - door
  - gate

===== Usage =====

city, door, gate, port (× -er).
