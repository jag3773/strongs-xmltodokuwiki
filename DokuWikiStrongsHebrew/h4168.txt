====== H4168: מוֹקֵד (môwqêd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h3344|H3344]];

===== Meaning =====

a **fire** or **fuel**; abstractly, a **conflagration**

===== Definitions =====

  - fire
  - fuel
  - conflagration

===== Usage =====

burning, hearth.
