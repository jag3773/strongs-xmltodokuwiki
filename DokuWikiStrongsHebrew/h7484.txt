====== H7484: רַעְמָה (Raʻmâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

the same as [[en:strongs:h7483|H7483]];

**Ramah**, the name of a grandson of Ham, and of a place (perhaps founded by him)
