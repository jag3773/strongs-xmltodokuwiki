====== H5182: נְחַת (nᵉchath) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

(Aramaic) corresponding to H5181;

===== Meaning =====

to **descend**; causatively, to **bring away**, **deposit**, **depose**

===== Definitions =====

  - descend
  - bring away
  - deposit
  - depose

===== Usage =====

carry, come down, depose, lay up, place.
