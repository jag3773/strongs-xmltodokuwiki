====== H4105: מְהֵיטַבְאֵל (Mᵉhêyṭabʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m n-pr-f

===== Source =====

from H3190 (augmented) and H410; **bettered of God**;

===== Meaning =====

**Mehetabel**, the name of an Edomitish man and woman

===== Definitions =====

  - bettered of God
  - Mehetabel

===== Usage =====

Mehetabeel, Mehetabel.
