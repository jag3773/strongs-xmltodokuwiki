====== H5387: נָשִׂיא (nâsîyʼ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; from [[en:strongs:h5375|H5375]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, an **exalted** one, i.e. a **king** or **sheik**; also a rising **mist**

===== Definitions =====

  - exalted
  - king
  - sheik
  - mist

===== Usage =====

captain, chief, cloud, governor, prince, ruler, vapour.
