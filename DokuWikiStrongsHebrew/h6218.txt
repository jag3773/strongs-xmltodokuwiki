====== H6218: עָשׂוֹר (ʻâsôwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - masculine

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; from [[en:strongs:h6235|H6235]];

===== Meaning =====

**ten**; by abbreviated form **ten strings**, and so a **decachord**

===== Definitions =====

  - ten
  - ten strings
  - decachord

===== Usage =====

(instrument of) ten (strings, -th).
