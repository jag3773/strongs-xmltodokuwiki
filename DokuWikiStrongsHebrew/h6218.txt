====== H6218: עָשׂוֹר (ʻâsôwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or ; from H6235;

===== Meaning =====

**ten**; by abbreviated form **ten strings**, and so a **decachord**

===== Definitions =====

  - ten
  - ten strings
  - decachord

===== Usage =====

(instrument of) ten (strings, -th).
