====== H6178: עָרוּץ (ʻârûwts) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

a

===== Source =====

passive participle of [[en:strongs:h6206|H6206]];

===== Meaning =====

**feared**, i.e. (concretely) a **horrible place** or **chasm**

===== Definitions =====

  - feared
  - horrible place
  - chasm

===== Usage =====

cliffs.
