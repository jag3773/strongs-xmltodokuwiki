====== H2910: טֻחָה (ṭuchâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from H2909 (or H2902) in the sense of overlaying;

===== Meaning =====

(in the plural only) the **kidneys** (as being covered); hence (figuratively) the **inmost thought**

===== Definitions =====

  - kidneys
  - inmost thought

===== Usage =====

inward parts.
