====== H4690: מָצוּק (mâtsûwq) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; from [[en:strongs:h6693|H6693]];

===== Meaning =====

something **narrow**, i.e. a **column** or **hilltop**

===== Definitions =====

  - narrow
  - column
  - hilltop

===== Usage =====

pillar, situate.
