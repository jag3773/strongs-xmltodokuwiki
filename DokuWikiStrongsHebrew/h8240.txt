====== H8240: שָׁפָת (shâphâth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h8239|H8239]];

===== Meaning =====

a (double) **stall** (for cattle); also a (two-pronged) **hook** (for flaying animals on)

===== Definitions =====

  - stall
  - hook

===== Usage =====

hook, pot.
