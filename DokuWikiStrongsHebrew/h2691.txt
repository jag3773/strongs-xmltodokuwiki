====== H2691: חָצֵר (châtsêr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

(masculine and feminine); from H2690 in its original sense;

===== Meaning =====

a **yard** (as inclosed by a fence); also a **hamlet** (as similarly surrounded with walls)

===== Definitions =====

  - yard
  - hamlet

===== Usage =====

court, tower, village.
