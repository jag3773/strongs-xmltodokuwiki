====== H7848: שִׁטָּה (shiṭṭâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine of a derivative (only in the plural [[en:strongs:|]]; meaning the **sticks** of wood) from the same as [[en:strongs:h7850|H7850]];

===== Meaning =====

the **acacia** (from its **scourging** thorns)

===== Definitions =====

  - sticks
  - acacia
  - scourging

===== Usage =====

shittah, shittim. See also
