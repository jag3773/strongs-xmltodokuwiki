====== H8337: שֵׁשׁ (shêsh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n

===== Source =====

masculine [[en:strongs:|]]; a primitive number; see [[en:strongs:h7797|H7797]]

===== Meaning =====

**six** (as an overplus	beyond five or the fingers of the hand); as ordinal **sixth**

===== Definitions =====

  - six
  - sixth

===== Usage =====

six(-teen, -teenth), sixth.
