====== H6483: פִּצֵּץ (Pitstsêts) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to dissever; **dispersive**;

===== Meaning =====

**Pitstsets**, a priest

===== Definitions =====

  - dispersive
  - Pitstsets

===== Usage =====

Apses (including the article).
