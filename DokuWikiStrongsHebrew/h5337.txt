====== H5337: נָצַל (nâtsal) ======

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to  snatch  away, whether in a good or a bad sense

===== Definitions =====

  - snatch

===== Usage =====

× at all, defend, deliver (self), escape, × without fail, part, pluck, preserve, recover, rescue, rid, save, spoil, strip, × surely, take (out).
