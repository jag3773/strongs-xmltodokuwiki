====== H8207: שְׁפִיפֹן (shᵉphîyphôn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning the same as [[en:strongs:h7779|H7779]];

===== Meaning =====

a kind of **serpent** (as **snapping**), probably the **cerastes** or **horned adder**

===== Definitions =====

  - serpent
  - snapping
  - cerastes
  - horned adder

===== Usage =====

adder.
