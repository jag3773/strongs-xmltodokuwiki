====== H6921: קָדִים (qâdîym) ======

===== Source =====

or   ; from  H6923 ;

===== Meaning =====

the  fore  or front part; hence (by orientation) the  East  (often adverbially,  eastward , for brevity the  east wind )

===== Definitions =====

  - fore
  - East
  - eastward
  - east wind

===== Usage =====

east(-ward, wind).
