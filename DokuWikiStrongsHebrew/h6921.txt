====== H6921: קָדִים (qâdîym) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or ; from H6923;

===== Meaning =====

the **fore** or front part; hence (by orientation) the **East** (often adverbially, **eastward**, for brevity the **east wind**)

===== Definitions =====

  - fore
  - East
  - eastward
  - east wind

===== Usage =====

east(-ward, wind).
