====== H6807: צְעָדָה (tsᵉʻâdâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:strongs:h6806|H6806]];

===== Meaning =====

a **march**; (concretely) an (ornamental) **ankle-chain**

===== Definitions =====

  - march
  - ankle-chain

===== Usage =====

going, ornament of the legs.
