====== H5997: עָמִית (ʻâmîyth) ======

===== Source =====

from a primitive root meaning to  ;

===== Meaning =====

associate companionship ; hence (concretely) a  comrade  or kindred man

===== Definitions =====

  - associate
  - companionship
  - comrade

===== Usage =====

another, fellow, neighbour.
