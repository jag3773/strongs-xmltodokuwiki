====== H5039: נְבָלָה (nᵉbâlâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine of H5036;

===== Meaning =====

**foolishness**, i.e. (morally) **wickedness**; concretely, a **crime**; by extension, **punishment**

===== Definitions =====

  - foolishness
  - wickedness
  - crime
  - punishment

===== Usage =====

folly, vile, villany.
