====== H3519: כָּבוֹד (kâbôwd) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

rarely ; from H3513;

===== Meaning =====

properly, **weight**, but only figuratively in a good sense, **splendor** or **copiousness**

===== Definitions =====

  - weight
  - splendor
  - copiousness

===== Usage =====

glorious(-ly), glory, honour(-able).
