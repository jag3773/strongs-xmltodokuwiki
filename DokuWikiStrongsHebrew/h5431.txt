====== H5431: סָאַן (çâʼan) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root; to be miry; used only as denominative from [[en:strongs:h5430|H5430]];

===== Meaning =====

to **shoe**, i.e. (active participle) a **soldier shod**

===== Definitions =====

  - shoe
  - soldier shod

===== Usage =====

warrior.
