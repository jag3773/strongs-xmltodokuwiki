====== H2291: חַגִּי (Chaggîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m a

===== Source =====

from H2287; **festive**

===== Meaning =====

**Chaggi**, an Israelite; also (patronymically) a **Chaggite**, or descendant of the same

===== Definitions =====

  - festive
  - Chaggi
  - Chaggite

===== Usage =====

Haggi, Haggites.
