====== H32: אֲבִיהַיִל (ʼĂbîyhayil) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

or (more correctly)  ; from H1 and H2428; father (i.e. possessor) of might;

===== Meaning =====

**Abihail** or **Abichail**, the name of three Israelites and two Israelitesses

===== Definitions =====

  - Abihail
  - Abichail

===== Usage =====

Abihail.
