====== H8149: שְׁנִיר (Shᵉnîyr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr

===== Source =====

or ; from an unused root meaning to be pointed; **peak**;

===== Meaning =====

**Shenir** or **Senir**, a summit of Lebanon

===== Definitions =====

  - peak
  - Shenir
  - Senir

===== Usage =====

Senir, Shenir.
