====== H2007: הֵנָּה (hênnâh) ======

===== Source =====

prolongation for  H2004 ;

===== Meaning =====

themselves  (often used emphatic for the copula, also in indirect relation)

===== Definitions =====

  - themselves

===== Usage =====

× in, × such (and such things), their, (into) them, thence, therein, these, they (had), on this side, whose, wherein.
