====== H6621: פֶּתַע (pethaʻ) ======

===== Source =====

from an unused root meaning to   (the eyes); (compare  H6597 )

===== Meaning =====

open a  wink , i.e.  moment  (used only [with or without preposition] adverbially,  quickly  or  unexpectedly )

===== Definitions =====

  - open
  - wink
  - moment
  - quickly
  - unexpectedly

===== Usage =====

at an instant, suddenly, × very.
