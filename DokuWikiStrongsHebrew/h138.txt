====== H138: אֲדֹנִיָּה (ʼĂdônîyâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

original (prolonged)  ; from H113 and H3050; lord (i.e. worshipper) of Jah;

===== Meaning =====

**Adonijah**, the name of three Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - Adonijah

===== Usage =====

Adonijah.
