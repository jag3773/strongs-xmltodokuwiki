====== H590: אֳנִי (ʼŏnîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n

===== Source =====

probably from H579 (in the sense of **conveyance**);

===== Meaning =====

a **ship** or (collectively) a **fleet**

===== Definitions =====

  - conveyance
  - ship
  - fleet

===== Usage =====

galley, navy (of ships).
