====== H256: אַחְאָב (ʼAchʼâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

once (by contraction)  (Jeremiah 29:22); from H251 and H1; brother (i.e. friend) of (his) father;

===== Meaning =====

**Achab**, the name of a king of Israel and of a prophet at Babylon

===== Definitions =====

  - Achab

===== Usage =====

Ahab.
