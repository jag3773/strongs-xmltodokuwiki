====== H6140: עָקַשׁ (ʻâqash) ======

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to  knot  or  distort ; figuratively, to  pervert  (act or declare perverse)

===== Definitions =====

  - knot
  - distort
  - pervert

===== Usage =====

make crooked, (prove, that is) perverse(-rt).
