====== H885: בְּאֵרֹת בְּנֵי־יַעֲקַן (Bᵉʼêrôth Bᵉnêy-Yaʻăqan) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from the feminine plural of H875, and the plural contraction of H1121, and H3292; wells of (the) sons of Jaakan;

===== Meaning =====

**Beeroth-Bene-Jaakan**, a place in the Desert

===== Definitions =====

  - Beeroth-Bene-Jaakan

===== Usage =====

Beeroth of the children of Jaakan.
