====== H1372: גַּבַּחַת (gabbachath) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from the same as [[en:strongs:h1371|H1371]];

===== Meaning =====

**baldness** in the forehead; by analogy, a **bare spot** on the right side of cloth

===== Definitions =====

  - baldness
  - bare spot

===== Usage =====

bald forehead, × without.
