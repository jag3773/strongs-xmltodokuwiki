====== H6856: צִפֹּרֶן (tsippôren) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H6852 (in the denominative sense (from H6833) of scratching);

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **claw**, i.e. (human) **nail**; also the **point** of a style (or pen, tipped with adamant)

===== Definitions =====

  - claw
  - nail
  - point

===== Usage =====

nail, point.
