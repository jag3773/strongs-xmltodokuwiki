====== H6868: צְרֵדָה (Tsᵉrêdâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - locative

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; apparently from an unused root meaning to pierce; **puncture**;

===== Meaning =====

**Tseredah**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - puncture
  - Tseredah

===== Usage =====

Zereda, Zeredathah.
