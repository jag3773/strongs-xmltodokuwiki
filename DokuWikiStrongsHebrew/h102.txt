====== H102: אַגָּף (ʼaggâph) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

probably from [[en:strongs:h5062|H5062]] (through the idea of impending); a cover or heap; i.e.

===== Meaning =====

(only plural) **wings of an army**, or **crowds of troops**

===== Definitions =====

  - wings of an army
  - crowds of troops

===== Usage =====

bands.
