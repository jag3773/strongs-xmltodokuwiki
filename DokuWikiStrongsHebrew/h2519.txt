====== H2519: חֲלַקְלַקָּה (chălaqlaqqâh) ======

===== Source =====

by reduplication from  H2505 ;

===== Meaning =====

properly, something  very smooth ; i.e. a  treacherous  spot; figuratively,  blandishment

===== Definitions =====

  - very smooth
  - treacherous
  - blandishment

===== Usage =====

flattery, slippery.
