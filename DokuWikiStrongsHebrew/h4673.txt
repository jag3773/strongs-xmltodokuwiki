====== H4673: מַצָּב (matstsâb) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H5324;

===== Meaning =====

a fixed **spot**; figuratively, an **office**, a military **post**

===== Definitions =====

  - spot
  - office
  - post

===== Usage =====

garrison, station, place where...stood.
