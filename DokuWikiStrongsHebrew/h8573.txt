====== H8573: תְּנוּפָה (tᵉnûwphâh) ======

===== Source =====

from  H5130 ;

===== Meaning =====

a  brandishing  (in threat); by implication,  tumult ; specifically, the official  undulation  of sacrificial offerings

===== Definitions =====

  - brandishing
  - tumult
  - undulation

===== Usage =====

offering, shaking, wave (offering).
