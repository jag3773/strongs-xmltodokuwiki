====== H734: אֹרַח (ʼôrach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h732|H732]];

===== Meaning =====

a well-trodden **road** (literally or figuratively); also a **caravan**

===== Definitions =====

  - road
  - caravan

===== Usage =====

manner, path, race, rank, traveller, troop, (by-, high-) way.
