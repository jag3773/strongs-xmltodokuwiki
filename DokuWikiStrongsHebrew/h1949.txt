====== H1949: הוּם (hûwm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root (compare [[en:strongs:h2000|H2000]]);

===== Meaning =====

to **make an uproar**, or **agitate** greatly

===== Definitions =====

  - make an uproar
  - agitate

===== Usage =====

destroy, move, make a noise, put, ring again.
