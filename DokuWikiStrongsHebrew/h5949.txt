====== H5949: עֲלִילָה (ʻălîylâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

or [[en:strongs:|]]; from [[en:strongs:h5953|H5953]] in the sense of **effecting**;

===== Meaning =====

an **exploit** (of God), or a **performance** (of man, often in a bad sense); by implication, an **opportunity**

===== Definitions =====

  - effecting
  - exploit
  - performance
  - opportunity

===== Usage =====

act(-ion), deed, doing, invention, occasion, work.
