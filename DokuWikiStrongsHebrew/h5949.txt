====== H5949: עֲלִילָה (ʻălîylâh) ======

===== Source =====

or   ; from  H5953  in the sense of  ;

===== Meaning =====

effecting an  exploit  (of God), or a  performance  (of man, often in a bad sense); by implication, an  opportunity

===== Definitions =====

  - effecting
  - exploit
  - performance
  - opportunity

===== Usage =====

act(-ion), deed, doing, invention, occasion, work.
