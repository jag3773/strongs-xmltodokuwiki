====== H1062: בְּכוֹרָה (bᵉkôwrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - feminine

===== Source =====

or (short) [[en:strongs:|]]; feminine of [[en:strongs:h1060|H1060]];

===== Meaning =====

the **firstling** of man or beast; abstractly **primogeniture**

===== Definitions =====

  - firstling
  - primogeniture

===== Usage =====

birthright, firstborn(-ling).
