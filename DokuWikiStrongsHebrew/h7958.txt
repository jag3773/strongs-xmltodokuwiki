====== H7958: שְׂלָו (sᵉlâv) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

or ; by orthographical variation from H7951 through the idea of **sluggishness**;

===== Meaning =====

the **quail** collectively (as **slow** in flight from its weight)

===== Definitions =====

  - sluggishness
  - quail
  - slow

===== Usage =====

quails.
