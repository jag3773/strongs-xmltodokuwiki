====== H4748: מִקְשֶׁה (miqsheh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h7185|H7185]] in the sense of **knotting** up round and hard;

===== Meaning =====

something **turned** (rounded), i.e. a **curl** (of tresses)

===== Definitions =====

  - knotting
  - turned
  - curl

===== Usage =====

× well (set) hair.
