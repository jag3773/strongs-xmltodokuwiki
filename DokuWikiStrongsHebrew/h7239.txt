====== H7239: רִבּוֹ (ribbôw) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from H7231; or  from H7231;

===== Meaning =====

a **myriad**, i.e. indefinitely, **large number**

===== Definitions =====

  - myriad
  - large number

===== Usage =====

great things, ten ((eight) -een, (for) -ty,   sixscore,   threescore, × twenty, (twen) -ty) thousand.
