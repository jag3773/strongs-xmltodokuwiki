===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from an unused root (meaning to sprinkle) and H410; sprinkled of God;

===== Meaning =====

**Jezavel**, an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - Jezavel

===== Usage =====

Jeziel (from the margin).
