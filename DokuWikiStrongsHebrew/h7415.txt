====== H7415: רִמָּה (rimmâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from H7426 in the sense of **breeding** (compare H7311);

===== Meaning =====

a **maggot** (as rapidly **bred**), literally or figuratively

===== Definitions =====

  - breeding
  - maggot
  - bred

===== Usage =====

worm.
