====== H7966: שִׁלּוּם (shillûwm) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or ; from H7999;

===== Meaning =====

a **requital**, i.e. (secure) **retribution**, (venal) a **fee**

===== Definitions =====

  - requital
  - retribution
  - fee

===== Usage =====

recompense, reward.
