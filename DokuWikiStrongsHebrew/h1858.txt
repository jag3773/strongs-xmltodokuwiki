====== H1858: דַּר (dar) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

apparently from the same as [[en:strongs:h1865|H1865]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, a **pearl** (from its sheen as rapidly turned); by analogy, **pearl-stone**, i.e. **mother-of-pearl** or **alabaster**

===== Definitions =====

  - pearl
  - pearl-stone
  - mother-of-pearl
  - alabaster

===== Usage =====

× white.
