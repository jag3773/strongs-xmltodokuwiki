====== H3530: כִּבְרָה (kibrâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

feminine of [[en:strongs:h3528|H3528]];

===== Meaning =====

properly, **length**, i.e. a **measure** (of uncertain dimension)

===== Definitions =====

  - measure

===== Usage =====
