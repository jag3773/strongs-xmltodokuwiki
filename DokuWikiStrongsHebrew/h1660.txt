====== H1660: גַּת (gath) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

probably from [[en:strongs:h5059|H5059]] (in the sense of **treading** out grapes);

===== Meaning =====

a wine-**press** (or vat for holding the grapes in pressing them)

===== Definitions =====

  - treading
  - press

===== Usage =====

(wine-) press (fat).
