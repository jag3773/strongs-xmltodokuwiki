====== H1996: הֲמוֹן גּוֹג (Hămôwn Gôwg) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from H1995 and H1463;

===== Meaning =====

the **multitude of Gog**; the fanciful name of an emblematic place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - multitude of Gog

===== Usage =====
