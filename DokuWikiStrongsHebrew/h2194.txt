====== H2194: זָעַם (zâʻam) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

properly, to **foam** at the mouth, i.e. to **be enraged**

===== Definitions =====

  - foam
  - be enraged

===== Usage =====

abhor, abominable, (be) angry, defy, (have) indignation.
