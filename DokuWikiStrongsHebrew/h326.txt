====== H326: אֲחַשְׁתָּרִי (ʼăchashtârîy) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

probably of Persian derivation;

===== Meaning =====

an **achastarite** (i.e. courier); the designation (rather than name) of an Israelite

===== Definitions =====

  - achastarite

===== Usage =====

Haakashtari (includ. the article).
