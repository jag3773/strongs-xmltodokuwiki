====== H8444: תּוֹצָאָה (tôwtsâʼâh) ======

===== Source =====

or   ; from  H3318 ;

===== Meaning =====

(only in plural collective)  exit , i.e. (geographical)  boundary , or (figuratively)  deliverance , (actively)  source

===== Definitions =====

  - exit
  - boundary
  - deliverance
  - source

===== Usage =====

border(-s), going(-s) forth (out), issues, outgoings.
