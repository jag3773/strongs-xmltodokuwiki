====== H4719: מַקֵּדָה (Maqqêdâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from the same as H5348 in the denominative sense of herding (compare H5349); **fold**;

===== Meaning =====

**Makkedah**, a place in Palestine

===== Definitions =====

  - fold
  - Makkedah

===== Usage =====

Makkedah.
