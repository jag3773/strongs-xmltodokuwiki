====== H7716: שֶׂה (seh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or ; probably from H7582 through the idea of **pushing** out to graze;

===== Meaning =====

a member of a flock, i.e. a **sheep** or **goat**

===== Definitions =====

  - pushing
  - sheep
  - goat

===== Usage =====

(lesser, small) cattle, ewe, goat, lamb, sheep. Compare
