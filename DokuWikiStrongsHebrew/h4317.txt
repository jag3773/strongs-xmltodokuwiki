====== H4317: מִיכָאֵל (Mîykâʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

noun - proper noun - masculine

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h4310|H4310]] and (the prefix derivative from) [[en:strongs:h3588|H3588]] and [[en:strongs:h410|H410]]; **who** (is) **like God**?;

===== Meaning =====

**Mikael**, the name of an archangel and of nine Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - who
  - like God
  - Mikael

===== Usage =====

Michael.
