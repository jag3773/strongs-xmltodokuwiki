====== H4317: מִיכָאֵל (Mîykâʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from H4310 and (the prefix derivative from) H3588 and H410; **who** (is) **like God**?;

===== Meaning =====

**Mikael**, the name of an archangel and of nine Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - who
  - like God
  - Mikael

===== Usage =====

Michael.
