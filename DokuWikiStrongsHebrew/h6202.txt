====== H6202: עָרַף (ʻâraph) ======

===== Source =====

a primitive root (identical with through the idea of  ); properly, to   downward; but used only as a denominative from  H6203 ,

===== Meaning =====

slopping bend to  break the neck ; hence (figuratively) to  destroy

===== Definitions =====

  - slopping
  - bend
  - break the neck
  - destroy

===== Usage =====

that is beheaded, break down, break (cut off, strike off) neck.
