====== H4465: מִמְכָּר (mimkâr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h4376|H4376]];

===== Meaning =====

**merchandise**; abstractly, a **selling**

===== Definitions =====

  - merchandise
  - selling

===== Usage =====

× ought, (that which cometh of) sale, that which...sold, ware.
