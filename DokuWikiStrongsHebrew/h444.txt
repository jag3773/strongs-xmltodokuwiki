====== H444: אָלַח (ʼâlach) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

v

===== Source =====

a primitive root;

===== Meaning =====

to **muddle**, i.e. (figuratively and intransitive) to **turn** (morally) **corrupt**

===== Definitions =====

  - muddle
  - turn
  - corrupt

===== Usage =====

become filthy.
