====== H3515: כָּבֵד (kâbêd) ======

===== Source =====

from  H3513 ;

===== Meaning =====

heavy ; figuratively in a good sense ( numerous ) or in a bad sense ( severe ,  difficult ,  stupid )

===== Definitions =====

  - heavy
  - numerous
  - severe
  - difficult
  - stupid

===== Usage =====

(so) great, grievous, hard(-ened), (too) heavy(-ier), laden, much, slow, sore, thick.
