====== H1942: הַוָּה (havvâh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h1933|H1933]] (in the sense of eagerly coveting and rushing upon;

===== Meaning =====

by implication, of **falling)**; **desire**; also **ruin**

===== Definitions =====

  - falling)
  - desire
  - ruin

===== Usage =====

calamity, iniquity, mischief, mischievous (thing), naughtiness, naughty, noisome, perverse thing, substance, very wickedness.
