====== H4332: מִישָׁאֵל (Mîyshâʼêl) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

from H4310 and H410 with the abbreviated form insep. relatively (see H834) interposed; who (is) what God (is) ?;

===== Meaning =====

**Mishael**, the name of three Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - Mishael

===== Usage =====

Mishael.
