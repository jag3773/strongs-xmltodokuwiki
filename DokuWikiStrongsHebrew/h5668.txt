====== H5668: עָבוּר (ʻâbûwr) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

prep

===== Source =====

or ; passive participle of H5674;

===== Meaning =====

properly, **crossed**, i.e. (abstractly) **transit**; used only adverbially, on **account** of, in **order** that

===== Definitions =====

  - crossed
  - transit
  - account
  - order

===== Usage =====

because of, for (...'s sake), (intent) that, to.
