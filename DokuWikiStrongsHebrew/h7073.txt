====== H7073: קְנַז (Qᵉnaz) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-m

===== Source =====

probably from an unused root meaning to hunt; **hunter**;

===== Meaning =====

**Kenaz**, the name of an Edomite and of two Israelites

===== Definitions =====

  - hunter
  - Kenaz

===== Usage =====

Kenaz.
