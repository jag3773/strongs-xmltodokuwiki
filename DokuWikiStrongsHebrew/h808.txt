====== H808: אָשִׁישׁ (ʼâshîysh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-f

===== Source =====

from the same as H784 (in the sense of **pressing** down firmly; compare H803);

===== Meaning =====

a (ruined) **foundation**

===== Definitions =====

  - pressing
  - foundation

===== Usage =====

foundation.
