====== H6944: קֹדֶשׁ (qôdesh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h6942|H6942]];

===== Meaning =====

a **sacred** place or thing; rarely abstract, **sanctity**

===== Definitions =====

  - sacred
  - sanctity

===== Usage =====

consecrated (thing), dedicated (thing), hallowed (thing), holiness, (× most) holy (× day, portion, thing), saint, sanctuary.
