====== H1779: דִּין (dîyn) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

or (Job 19:29) ; from H1777;

===== Meaning =====

**judgement** (the suit, justice, sentence or tribunal); by implication also **strife**

===== Definitions =====

  - judgement
  - strife

===== Usage =====

cause, judgement, plea, strife.
