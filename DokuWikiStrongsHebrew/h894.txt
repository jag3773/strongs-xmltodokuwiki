====== H894: בָּבֶל (Bâbel) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-pr-loc

===== Source =====

from [[en:strongs:h1101|H1101]]; confusion;

===== Meaning =====

**Babel** (i.e. Babylon), including **Babylonia** and the **Babylonian empire**

===== Definitions =====

  - Babel
  - Babylonia
  - Babylonian empire

===== Usage =====

Babel, Babylon.
