====== H6429: פְּלֶשֶׁת (Pᵉlesheth) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n

===== Source =====

from H6428; **rolling**, i.e. **migratory**;

===== Meaning =====

**Pelesheth**, a region of Syria

===== Definitions =====

  - rolling
  - migratory
  - Pelesheth

===== Usage =====

Palestina, Palestine, Philistia, Philistines.
