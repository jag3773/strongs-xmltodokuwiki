====== H4175: מוֹרֶה (môwreh) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H3384; (see H3138)

===== Meaning =====

an **archer**; also **teacher** or **teaching**; also the **early rain**

===== Definitions =====

  - archer
  - teacher
  - teaching
  - early rain

===== Usage =====

(early) rain.
