====== H7549: רָקִיעַ (râqîyaʻ) ======

===== Part of Speech =====

n-m

===== Source =====

from H7554;

===== Meaning =====

properly, an **expanse**, i.e. the **firmament** or (apparently) **visible arch of the sky**

===== Definitions =====

  - expanse
  - firmament
  - visible arch of the sky

===== Usage =====

firmament.
